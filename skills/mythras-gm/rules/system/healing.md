- **Minor Wound** (location HP > 0): heal Healing Rate HP/day.
- **Serious Wound** (location <= 0): Healing Rate HP/week; 1d3 turns stunned when suffered; the limb may be disabled (Endurance opposed vs attack roll).
- **Major Wound** (location <= -starting HP): incapacitated; needs Healing skill treatment or death in minutes; Healing Rate HP/month.
- **First Aid:** once per injury, heals 1d3 HP.
