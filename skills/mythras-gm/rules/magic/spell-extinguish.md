_Instant, Ranged._ Extinguish immediately quenches flames and small fires of modest size and heat. It is useful for dousing candles, lanterns, torches, or small cook fires, but will not work on magical or larger, more ferocious conflagrations such as pyres, burning houses, or dragon flames.
