_Touch._ Fireblade is like Firearrow but cast on hand tools and melee weapons, originally to sterilize surgical equipment, aid slash-and-burn agriculture, or provide light in darkness. Cast on a weapon it inflicts an additional 1d3 damage and can set flammable materials alight if held to them for several rounds (see Fire in the Game System section). Wooden-hafted weapons under its effect are consumed as part of the spell.
