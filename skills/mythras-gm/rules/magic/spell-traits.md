- **Instant** -- happens immediately, no duration.
- **Concentration** -- lasts while the caster concentrates undisturbed.
- **Ranged** -- up to the Magic skill score in metres; unseen targets = one grade harder.
- **Touch** -- physical contact required (unwilling targets generally need Grip/surprise).
- **Resist (Endurance/Evade/Willpower)** -- opposed roll vs the casting result; an Evade resist costs the target an Action Point.
