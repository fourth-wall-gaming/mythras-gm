**Magicians.** Magicians begin with 1d4+1 spells, all cast using the Magic skill. Player and GM should consult on which spells are available and how the character came by them (apprenticeship to a local magician, learned at a mother's knee, etc.). More spells can be learned, but each costs 3 Experience Rolls and a week of dedicated study and practice. New spells must be learned from a teacher or a reliable source such as a scroll or grimoire designed to teach it.

**Superheroes.** The GM and player decide how the character came by their powers and the source (special armor, exposure to cosmic rays, genetic mutation, etc.). Superheroes start with a number of core powers determined by POW plus another characteristic based on the power source: CON for altered physiology (mutation, alien species, experimentation) or INT for technology or skill-based powers (cybernetics, battle suits, intense training).

| POW + (CON or INT) | Core Powers |
|:-:|:-:|
| 1-12 | 1 |
| 13-20 | 2 |
| 21-30 | 3 |
| 31-40 | 4 |
| 40+ | 5 |

A core power can be exchanged for two Boosts applicable to any core power. A character may also take up to two Limits, with one granting a bonus Boost or two granting a bonus core power.
