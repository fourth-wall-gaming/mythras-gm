Fire blasts of a specific type of energy out to a range of 10/30/120, choosing two Combat Effects to apply at time of creation. Damage and force are determined by POW: 8 or less: 1d6/S; 9-12: 1d8/M; 13-15: 1d10/M; 16-18: 1d12/L; 19+: 2d6/L. To hit, roll against either an applicable Combat Style for the power or use Unarmed.

Boost: Spend 2 Power Points and an action to inflict +1d6 damage on the next energy blast attack.
