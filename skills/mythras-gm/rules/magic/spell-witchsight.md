_Ranged, Resist (Willpower)._ Witchsight lets the caster see active magic, enchanted items, and invisible entities (as shadowy representations) within range and line of sight. It can also penetrate illusions or discern the true guise of shapeshifted creatures. Beings wishing to remain hidden or disguised must win an Opposed Roll of their Willpower versus the casting roll.
