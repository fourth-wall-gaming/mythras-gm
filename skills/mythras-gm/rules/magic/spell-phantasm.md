_Concentration, Ranged._ Phantasm lets the caster weave together insubstantial or near-weightless objects so they take a shape or ghostly form, such as a spectral figure from naturally occurring mist or a face formed in a pile of dead leaves. Beyond this the spell has little effect, save to frighten, intrigue, or disconcert those who view it.
