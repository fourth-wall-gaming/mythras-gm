_Instant, Touch._ Heal's effect depends on the ailment. Minor complaints (headache, back pain, hangover, cold, warts) are immediately lifted. Cast on a location with a Minor Wound, it restores all lost Hit Points instantly. Against Serious or Major Wounds no Hit Points are recovered, but the spell stabilizes the location, stops all bleeding, and prevents imminent death from inattention.
