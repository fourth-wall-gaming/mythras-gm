_Special Duration, Touch._ Lock magically secures any device that already has a mechanical bar or lock. A Locked device can be opened only by the caster and cannot be picked by mundane means, since the magic renders the mechanism immobile, though it could still be forced open by breaking the object the lock is set into. The magic remains until opened by the caster, after which Lock must be recast to restore it. The Magic Point used does not recover until the spell is dismissed or concludes naturally.
