_Concentration, Ranged._ Light must be cast on an inanimate object (a branch, sword blade, spear point, torch, and so on), producing enough light to illuminate an area as though with a lantern. It can also be cast directly against a Darkness spell to counter it; in this case both spells are consumed, leaving the ambient light to illuminate the area.
