_Instant, Touch._ Chill dramatically reduces the temperature of small objects (no larger than the caster's hand) down to the temperature of ice water. Useful for rapidly cooling hot items, chilling drinks, and so forth. The spell does not freeze an object nor cause any damage to its structure; it merely renders it very cold.
