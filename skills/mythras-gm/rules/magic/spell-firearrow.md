_Touch._ Firearrow causes all missiles thrown or fired by the recipient to burst into flame when released. Missiles add 1d3 damage but are extinguished if they impale flesh. Those that strike flammable material have a chance equal to the caster's Folk Magic skill of setting it alight, such as wooden shields or thatched roofs. Wooden ammunition is consumed as part of the spell.
