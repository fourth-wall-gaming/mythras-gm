Optional disadvantages placed on a character, core power, or Boost, subject to GM approval. Up to two may be taken: one grants a bonus Boost, two grant a bonus core power.

- **Activation Cost:** Spend 2 Power Points to activate a core power, or increase a Boost's cost by 2; a continuous power instead costs 1 Power Point at the start of each turn to maintain.
- **External Power Source:** Powers come from an artefact or device. If small or handheld, it is vulnerable to Special Effects like Disarm Weapon or Take Weapon; if large, it needs at least a minute to don or activate.
- **Fatal Flaw:** The character must consume or contact a substance every four hours or suffer a level of Fatigue; an Endurance check staves this off, but its difficulty rises one grade per additional four hours unmet.
- **Limited Control:** With actions involving the power or Boost, the critical-failure range increases to 90-00.
- **Limited Power:** The power or Boost works only under specific conditions or has a reduced effect determined by the GM.
- **Physical Limitation:** The character has a persistent limiting condition, such as blindness, permanent Hit Point loss to a location, or reduced mobility.
- **Vulnerability:** The character takes double damage from a specific form of attack, or must make Hard Endurance checks each turn within 5 metres of a specific substance or suffer a level of Fatigue.
