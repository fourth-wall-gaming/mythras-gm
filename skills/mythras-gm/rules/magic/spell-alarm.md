_Special Duration._ Casting Alarm on a location such as a room or small clearing creates a temporary psychic bond between the area and the caster. If a living creature with SIZ greater than 1 enters the area, the caster is automatically made aware no matter the distance, usually as a distinct tingling sensation or mental twinge that will awaken them. Alarm can also be cast on an individual object, triggering when touched or moved.
