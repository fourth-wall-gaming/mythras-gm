_Resist (Endurance), Touch._ Sleep sends its recipient into a deep, peaceful sleep. It has no effect on creatures with SIZ greater than the caster's POW. Unless the target resists, it slumbers for a number of hours equal to half the caster's POW. The spell takes 1d3 Rounds to take effect before the target falls unconscious. Any attempt to cast it in a combat situation automatically fails.
