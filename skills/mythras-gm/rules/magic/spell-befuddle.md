_Ranged, Resist (Willpower)._ Befuddle causes confusion within the mind of a corporeal target. The subject has difficulty thinking straight, forgetting where it is, what it is doing and why, often lapsing into disassociated thought. Befuddled targets can still act in self-defense but cannot initiate any constructive activity until the spell ends. Any attack or threatening action instantly breaks the spell, whether or not directed at the befuddled target.
