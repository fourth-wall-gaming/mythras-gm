_Ranged, Resist (Willpower)._ Calm dampens the passions of the target, perhaps ensuring a lovesick paramour doesn't press his suit, a frightened rival doesn't scream for help, or weapons are not drawn in anger. A calmed person is not otherwise mentally affected; any assault or threatening action still permits the target to defend themselves and even attack, albeit calmly and level-headedly.
