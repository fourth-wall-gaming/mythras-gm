_Touch._ Glue cements together two solid, inanimate objects for the spell's duration, such as a cartwheel to its axle or a door to its frame. The items cannot be parted unless something actively tries to wrench them apart, in which case the spell has a Brawn skill equal to five times the caster's POW and fails when a superior Brawn wins an Opposed Roll. Once the spell concludes or fails, the items part completely unharmed.
