_Instant, Ranged, Resist (Endurance)._ Disruption damages or disassembles physical objects without tools, and is commonly used to drive off or kill living creatures such as birds or vermin. When successfully cast, it inflicts 1d3 damage to a single random Hit Location or to an object's overall Hit Points. In both cases the damage ignores any armor or natural protection.
