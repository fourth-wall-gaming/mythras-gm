_Touch._ Breath lets the recipient hold their breath for an extended period, so they can temporarily venture into harmful environments such as underwater, or atmospheres tainted by rock dust, gases, smoke, or poisons. The spell lasts a maximum of half the caster's POW in minutes, during which the recipient cannot speak or the breath is lost; they must then immediately breathe from their environment, suffering any present risks (asphyxiation, drowning, poisoning, etc.).
