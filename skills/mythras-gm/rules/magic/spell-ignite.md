_Instant, Ranged._ Ignite works only on flammable inorganic matter, causing a small object or hand-sized area to burst into flame. Once burning, the flames may spread unless quenched or countered. Normally used to light candles, torches, or lanterns from afar, or to start a camp or cooking fire in adverse conditions such as damp kindling or strong winds.
