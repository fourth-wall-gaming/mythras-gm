Provides an armor value to all locations equal to one third of CON, rounded up. Characters may reduce the value of a location by up to two points to add it to another location; for example, lowering the value of the right arm to add to the head.

Boost: When struck by an attack, gain an extra armor point against it for each 1 Power Point spent.
