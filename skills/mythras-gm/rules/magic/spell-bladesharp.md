_Touch._ Bladesharp is cast on edged and piercing melee weapons. It increases the weapon's damage by one dice step and incidentally leaves the edge honed after the spell concludes. Often used on tools such as logging axes, plows, and razors. Cast on a dagger it becomes 1d6+1 damage; on a great axe, 2d8+2. (Dice steps: 1d4 -> 1d6 -> 1d8 -> 1d10 -> 2d6 -> 2d8 -> 2d10.)
