A Creature Ability is something a creature does naturally from its physiology or innate magic. As a rule of thumb, give a creature up to three Abilities suiting its nature (more in exceptional cases).

- **Adhering:** Moves on vertical surfaces and ceilings at half Move.
- **Aquatic:** Breathes water; out of water it suffocates after CON minutes (half if active).
- **Blood Sense:** Detects blood up to 1d6+6 km.
- **Breathe Flame:** Cone of CON metres (width CON/4) as a Combat Action; fire damage to all locations (Evade halves); usable once per period, retries need ever-harder Endurance rolls.
- **Burrower:** Tunnels at normal Move.
- **Camouflaged:** Perception to spot is two grades harder.
- **Characteristic Drain:** Drains temporary Characteristic points via attacks (per description).
- **Cold-Blooded:** Eats rarely; below 15C suffers -6 Initiative and loses one Action; below 5C becomes torpid/helpless.
- **Dark Sight:** Sees normally in total darkness.
- **Death Sense:** Senses death/dead flesh at INT/2 km.
- **Disease Immunity / Poison Immunity:** Immune to all diseases / poisons (automatic for creatures without SIZ).
- **Diving Strike:** Aerial/aquatic charge; +1 step Size and Damage Modifier; once per round, from one round's Move away.
- **Earth Sense:** Fights and perceives in total darkness within INS metres (halved above ground).
- **Echolocation:** Stealth to hide from it is two grades harder.
- **Engulfing:** Swallows targets up to half its SIZ whole; victim takes a bite then suffocates.
- **Flying:** Auto-succeeds routine flight; may use Fly in place of Evade aloft.
- **Formidable Natural Weapons:** Can actively parry with natural weapons.
- **Frenzy:** On Willpower failure, frenzies for CON rounds -- only attacks/advances, ignores pain, fatigue, Serious Wounds and mental control; ends Exhausted.
- **Gaze Attack:** Active or passive gaze attack (per description).
- **Grappler:** A successful strike seizes the foe; if parried, grants Grip or Pin Weapon; resists escape with Brawn.
- **Hold Breath:** Holds breath CON minutes (halved if active).
- **Immunity:** Immune to one damage source (cold, fire, iron, etc.).
- **Intimidate:** Foes make unopposed Willpower or back away/flee; fumble = flee, critical = immune for the encounter.
- **Leaper:** Combines a Leaping attack with a natural weapon, inflicting that damage on a win (unparriable except Passive Blocking).
- **Life Sense:** Reads a touched target's injuries/Fatigue/afflictions; senses life within Willpower metres.
- **Magic Sense:** As Life Sense, but for magic points, enchantments, and active spells.
- **Multi-Headed / Multi-Limbed:** +1 Combat Action per extra head / per extra pair of combat-capable limbs.
- **Night Sight:** Treats partial darkness as lit, darkness as partial.
- **Regeneration:** Regrows lost Hit Points (not limbs; vital-location loss still kills).
- **Swimmer:** Auto-succeeds routine swimming; may use Swim for Athletics/Evade in water.
- **Terrifying:** Viewers make unopposed Willpower or flee; fumble = unconscious, critical = act freely.
- **Trample:** Tramples beings of half its SIZ or less using Athletics for twice base Damage Modifier, +1 step Size.
- **Undead:** Immune to fatigue and Serious Wounds; destroyed by a Major Wound to its bound (head/chest) location.
- **Vampiric:** A damaging bite drains blood, raising the victim's Fatigue.
- **Venomous:** Has a venomous bite, sting, or other attack.
- **Wing Buffet:** Wing beats deal damage-bonus damage to those within 3 m of its front or side (Attack action).
