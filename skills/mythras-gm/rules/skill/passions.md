Passions are rated like skills (e.g. "Loyalty to the Murmuration 60%"). Use them to:

- **Augment** a related skill: +20% of the passion's value.
- **Drive behavior:** roll vs the passion; success = act in accordance.
- **Oppose** another passion or resist manipulation (substitute for Willpower).
