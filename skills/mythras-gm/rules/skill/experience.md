Award 1-3 Experience Rolls per session at natural break points. The player rolls 1d100+INT vs the skill: >= skill -> +1d4+1%; < skill -> +1%. Skills fumbled during play gain a free +1%.

Use CLI `award-experience` and `improve-skill`.
