Both sides roll. The winner is whoever achieves the higher level of success. On a tie in success level, the higher dice roll that is still within skill range wins. If both fail, re-roll or the GM narrates.

Use `roll-opposed` in the CLI. Common pairings: Stealth vs Perception, Deceit vs Insight, Influence vs Willpower, Brawn vs Brawn.
