Sometimes a character Fails a skill attempt but is left in a situation where they could try again (picking a lock, climbing a sheer wall). Rather than denying further attempts, a kindly Games Master may permit a follow-up, last-ditch attempt. However, the character suffers trepidation or lack of confidence which increases the Difficulty Grade of the skill roll by one.

If this second effort also fails, the character is assumed to have given it their best shot and cannot make any further attempts for the foreseeable future.
