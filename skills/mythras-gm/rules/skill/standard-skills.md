Athletics STR+DEX, Boating STR+CON, Brawn STR+SIZ, Combat Style STR+DEX, Conceal DEX+POW, Customs INT*2+40, Dance DEX+CHA, Deceit INT+CHA, Drive DEX+POW, Endurance CON*2, Evade DEX*2, First Aid DEX+INT, Influence CHA*2, Insight INT+POW, Locale INT*2, Native Tongue INT+CHA+40, Perception INT+POW, Ride DEX+POW, Sing POW+CHA, Stealth DEX+INT, Swim STR+CON, Unarmed STR+DEX, Willpower POW*2.

Settings may swap standard skills (e.g. the Veilwrack campaign replaces Swim with **Flight (STR+DEX)** for its avian species) -- see the campaign's lore.
