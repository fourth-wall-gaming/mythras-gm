As a final step, a pool of 150 skill points rounds out the character. These apply to any Standard skill, or to Professional skills gained from Culture or Career. The GM may cap points applied to any one skill.

### Creating Experienced Characters

Beginning characters usually start fresh, but a more experienced character may be built (replacing a dead one, or joining an ongoing campaign). Experience correlates with age. Treat the Age Bonus as approximate; substitute actual age when converting an existing character.

##### Experience Table

| Category | Age | Bonus Skill Points | Maximum Skill Increase |
| :-- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Young | 10+1d6 | 100 | +10 |
| Adult | 15+2d6 | 150 | +15 |
| Middle Aged | 25+3d6 | 200 | +20 |
| Senior | 40+4d6 | 250 | +25 |
| Old | 60+5d6 | 300 | +30 |

Bonus Skill Points are spent developing skills; Maximum Skill Increase limits how many bonus points apply to any one skill.

### The Skill Pyramid

A faster alternative to allocating from the Culture (100), Career (100), and Bonus (150) pools -- 350 points -- the Pyramid divides points into five fixed blocks:

1 skill at 50%, 2 skills at 40%, 3 skills at 30%, 4 skills at 20%, 5 skills at 10%.

```
    50
   40 40
  30 30 30
 20 20 20 20
10 10 10 10 10
```

Rules: all points must be allocated; no skill may take more than one allocation; Customs and Native Language both still gain +40 (in addition); Passions gain their normal values (in addition); no skill may exceed 80% from an allocation; six values must go to Professional Skills; players may use a Culture and Occupation as a guide or go freestyle; at least one value should (but need not) go to a Combat Style; the GM has final say.

### Larger-Than-Life / Pulp / Paragon

For heroic settings, characters may be built tougher. **Pulp Heroes** use Dice Roll Assign (4d6 drop lowest x5 for STR/CON/DEX/POW/CHA; 3d6+6 drop lowest twice for SIZ/INT, or 90-point Points Build), gain two non-stacking Advantages, double Healing Rate for Minor/Serious Wounds, and get +50 Bonus Skill Points. **Paragon** characters (4d6 drop lowest x6; 3d6+6 drop lowest x3, or 100-point Points Build) gain three Advantages and +100 Bonus Skill Points. Advantages: +1 Action Point, +1/+2 Luck Points, +1/+2 Hit Points per location, or Endurance/Stealth/Willpower rolls one grade easier.
