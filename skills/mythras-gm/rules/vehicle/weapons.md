Usually only military hulls are designed to mount weaponry (hard points, ammo storage, control systems); the Weaponized Trait lets civilian vehicles carry weapons. The maximum number of weapon systems equals 1/10th of Structure Points (40 Structure supports 4 systems). The class of weapon depends on the vehicle's Size.

**Weapons by Size** (Size | Terrestrial | Spacecraft):
- Small | Light Weapon (Assault Rifle) | Medium Weapon (Machine Gun, Rocket Launcher)
- Medium | Medium Weapon (Machine Gun, Rocket Launcher) | Turreted/Heavy Weapon (Linked MGs, Mini-Guns)
- Large | Turreted/Heavy Weapon | Weapon Battery (Battlefield Artillery)
- Huge | Weapon Battery | Advanced Weapons (Missile Systems)
- Enormous | Advanced Weapons | Spinal Mounted Weapons (one only)
- Colossal | NA | Spinal Mounted Weapons (up to 3)

**Sample Weapon Statistics** (Weapon | Damage | Range | Firing Rate | Ammo/Load):
- 50 inch Cannon | 3d6+3 | 1000/2000/6500 | 1/10/50 | 1/5
- Mini-Gun/Gatling Gun | 3d6 | 300/1000/2000 | -/20/100 | 200/4
- Rocket | 5d6 | 5000/20km/60km | 1 | 1/5
- Medium Missile/Torpedo | 6d10 | Max 200km | 1 | 1/5
- Large/Cruise Missile | 6d10+10 | Max 1000km | 1 | 1/10
- Laser/Plasma Cannon | 4d6 | 300/1000/2000 | -/5/25 | 500/5
- Heavy Laser/Plasma Cannon | 5d6 | 1000/3000/6000 | -/5/25 | 300/5
- Artillery Battery | 5d6+6 | Max 50km | 1 | 1/5
- Spinal Mount Battery | 10d6+6 | Max 20000km | 1 | 1/10

Ranges are in metres/kilometres. Firing rates are single shot / semi-auto / full auto. Ammo is shots before reload; Load is reload time in Combat Rounds with a full crew.
