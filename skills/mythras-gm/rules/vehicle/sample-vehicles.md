Quick-reference example vehicles (Size/Type, Hull, Structure, Systems, Speed, Handling, notable weapons).

**Ground Vehicles**
- *Interceptor:* Medium Military; Hull 6, Struct 40, Sys 2, Fast, Easy. Twin-linked Medium MGs (2d6+3), oil slick, smoke. Spy-car staple.
- *Pursuit Motorcycle:* Small Civilian; Hull 2, Struct 12, Sys 1, Fast, Easy. Two Rockets (4d6) or twin Light MGs (2d6).
- *Sedan Car:* Medium Civilian; Hull 4, Struct 25, Sys 2, Rapid, Standard. Unobtrusive, surveillance suite; no weapons.
- *Submersible Car:* Medium Civilian; Hull 6, Struct 25, Sys 2, Fast, Standard. Rocket (5d6), torpedo (4d10); waterborne/submersible.
- *Surveillance Van:* Large Civilian; Hull 9, Struct 60, Sys 3, Gentle, Standard. Tough, surveillance suite; no weapons.
- *Tactical Support Vehicle:* Medium Civilian; Hull 6, Struct 30, Sys 2, Rapid, Standard. Mounted Medium MG (2d6); all terrain.

**Watercraft**
- *Speedboat:* Medium Civilian; Hull 3, Struct 24, Sys 2, Fast, Standard. Mine dispenser (5d6).
- *Yacht:* Huge Civilian; Hull 10, Struct 75, Sys 4, Slow, Formidable. Luxurious, surveillance suite; no weapons.

**Aircraft / Spacecraft**
- *Dirigible:* Enormous Civilian; Hull 10, Struct 85, Sys 5, Ponderous, Herculean. Airborne, luxurious; no weapons.
- *Assault Helicopter:* Large Military; Hull 11, Struct 50, Sys 3, Moderate, Easy. Gatling Gun (3d6), 4 Rockets (5d6); stealth, surveillance.
- *Multi-Role Fighter:* Large Military; Hull 10, Struct 45, Sys 3, Moderate, Standard. 4 nose Gatling Guns (4d6+3), bomb bay (6d10).
- *Interstellar Freighter:* Medium Civilian Spaceship; Hull 10, Struct 80, Sys 2, Rapid (Parsec Factor 3), Standard. Shields 8; 2 quad Laser Cannon turrets (5d6); FTL, cargo.
- *General Contact Unit:* Colossal Civilian Spaceship; Hull 20, Struct 10,000, Sys 6, Moderate (Parsec Factor 4), Formidable. Shields 30; AI Mind, carrier, tractor beam; weapons undisclosed.
