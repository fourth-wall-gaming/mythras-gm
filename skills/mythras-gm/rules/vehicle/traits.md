The maximum traits a vehicle can have depends on hull size: Small 1, Medium 2, Large 3, Huge 4, Enormous 5, Colossal 6. Every vehicle has an inherent travel-mode trait (aircraft Airborne, boats Seaborne, etc.) that does not count toward the limit; a second travel mode must be bought as a trait. Some traits may be unavailable depending on the setting's tech level.

- **Airborne:** capable of atmospheric flight.
- **All Terrain:** (Ground only) traverses difficult, steep terrain.
- **Burrowing:** tunnels to a depth of Hull Rating x5 metres.
- **Camouflaged:** visual spotting is one grade harder.
- **Cargo:** built to haul cargo; speed two steps lower than size allows when laden.
- **Carrier:** (Enormous+) carries smaller craft, from 10 up to its Structure rating.
- **Construction:** heavy work tools; includes All Terrain; speed two steps lower.
- **EMP Resistant:** shielded against EMP attacks.
- **Enhanced Performance:** Speed one step higher than size maximum.
- **Ejector Seat:** propels occupants clear, then deploys a parachute.
- **FTL:** (Spacecraft only) separate FTL Speed rating measures parsec-crossing factor (Ponderous 10 down to Fleet 1, times a GM-set time unit). Cannot activate within 1.5 AU of a star.
- **Ground Vehicle:** capable of ground travel.
- **Hover:** hovers via air cushion or anti-grav repulsor.
- **Luxurious:** passenger luxury accommodations.
- **Rails:** Speed three steps higher than size, but restricted to rails.
- **Resilient:** systems take one extra hit; stackable (Resilient 2 = +2 hits).
- **Seaborne:** buoyant on water.
- **Spacecraft:** sealed against vacuum and radiation.
- **Stealth:** (Aircraft only) electronic-sensor spotting one grade harder.
- **Submersible:** dives to Hull Rating x10 metres; collapse depth 1.5x that.
- **Superior Handling:** handling rated Easy.
- **Surveillance Suite:** onboard comms, listening-device receiver, signal intercept within 100 m.
- **Tough:** civilian draws Hull from Military column with max Structure; military draws from next row down.
- **Tractor Beam:** (Spacecraft only) ensnares hulls up to two steps smaller.
- **VTOL:** vertical takeoff/landing; inherent for helicopters.
- **Walker:** articulated legs; capped at Gentle speed, but allows great heights.
- **Waterborne:** capable of subaqua movement.
- **Weaponized:** adapted to mount weaponry.
