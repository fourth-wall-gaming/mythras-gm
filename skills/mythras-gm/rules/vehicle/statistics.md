Vehicles are described by Structure, Hull, Speed, Systems, Traits, and Shields (if present). All have one of six Size ratings (Small to Colossal) determining the rest. Hull = Armor Points; Structure = Hit Points. Damage is reduced by Hull; any remainder reduces Structure and has a percentage chance, equal to the penetrating damage, of affecting a System. At zero Structure the vehicle is destroyed or scrapped.

**Terrestrial Hulls** (Size | Structure | Civilian Hull | Military Hull):
- Small: 1-20 | 1-3 | 4-6 (motorcycle, compact car, canoe)
- Medium: 21-40 | 1-6 | 7-9 (sedan, van, speedboat, autogyro)
- Large: 41-60 | 4-9 | 10-12 (lorry, bus, tank, helicopter, submarine)
- Huge: 61-80 | 7-12 | 13-15 (semi, airliner, yacht)
- Enormous: 81-100 | 10-15 | 16-18 (train, cruise liner, naval vessel)

**Starship Hulls** (Size | Structure | Civilian | Military):
- Small: 10-50 | 4-9 | 10-12; Medium: 51-150 | 7-12 | 13-15; Large: 151-450 | 10-15 | 16-18; Huge: 451-1350 | 13-18 | 19-21; Enormous: 1351-4050 | 16-21 | 21-24; Colossal: 4050-12110 | 19-24 | 24-30.

**Shields** (settings with energy tech) are ablative. Damage equal to or below shield value is fully blocked; excess reduces shield strength by that amount. At zero strength the shield collapses and remaining damage carries onto the hull.

**Speed** is an abstract rating used only to roughly judge whether one vehicle can overtake or catch another; compare only vehicles of the same class. Standard speed can be pushed +1 step on a successful Drive roll, sustainable 1d6 minutes (1d12 on a critical); +2 steps requires a Herculean Drive roll, sustainable 1d3 minutes.

**Speed Rating ladder** (slowest to fastest, with max for size): Ponderous (Enormous), Sluggish, Slow (Huge), Mediocre, Gentle (Large), Moderate, Rapid (Medium), Fast, Fleet (Small).
