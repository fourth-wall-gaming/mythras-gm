In the attack/defense Differential Roll, the difference in success levels equals the number of Special Effects the better side may choose. The winner picks their effects BEFORE rolling damage.

Some effects require a Critical (Bypass Armor, Circumvent Parry, Blind Opponent, Enhance Parry, Slip Free, Maximize Damage). Others trigger off an opponent's Fumble (Force Failure, Select Target). The rest can be taken on any differential win.

Offensive effects belong to a successful attacker; defensive effects to a successful defender. See the offensive and defensive reference lists, plus the dedicated pieces for Impale, Bleed, Bash, Trip, and Stun Location.
