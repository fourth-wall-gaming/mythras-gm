Setting overlay for flying Alar (gathers the aerial rules scattered through combat):

- **Combat Style cap:** fighting while flying caps Combat Style at the character's Flight skill (analogous to the SRD mounted-combat cap by Ride). Flying in turbulence is likewise capped by Flight.
- **Aerial special effects:** when both combatants are flying, Trip becomes Tumble (forced 1d10m altitude loss + Flight roll to recover) and Bash knocks the target back/down 1m per 2 damage in three dimensions.
- **Diving charge:** an Alar diving 10m+ onto a target charges at Damage Modifier +2 steps; a miss demands an immediate Flight roll or the attacker tumbles past prone / loses 1d10m altitude.
- **Ranged wind:** light/moderate/strong wind makes ranged attacks Hard/Formidable/Herculean.
- **Wing wounds & falling:** a disabled wing drops a flying Alar (see Falling). A falling Alar with one working wing may attempt a Flight roll (Hard if injured) to halve effective fall distance, or fully arrest on a Critical.
