**Trip** (offensive or defensive). The target makes an opposed Brawn/Evade/Acrobatics roll or is knocked prone.

**Aerial addition (setting rule):** when both combatants are flying, Trip becomes Tumble -- the target suffers a forced 1d10m altitude loss plus a Flight roll to recover.
