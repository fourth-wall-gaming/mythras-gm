Resolved like melee but only shields can parry; otherwise the target must Evade (dive prone) or use cover. Force is compared versus parry size. Long range = half damage and -1 Force step. Aiming a full round reduces one situational/range grade.

See `combat/arms-and-armor` for ranged weapon ranges (e.g. Stormbow 15/100/200m, Sling-darts 10/150/300m, Dagger thrown 5/10/20m).
