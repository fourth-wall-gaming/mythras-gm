**Proactive (on your turn, costs 1 AP, one per round):** Attack, Brace, Cast Magic, Change Range, Charge, Delay, Mount, Move, Outmaneuver, Ready Weapon, Regain Footing, Struggle, Take Cover.

**Reactive (any time, vs a threat, costs 1 AP):** Parry, Evade, Interrupt (if Delaying), Counter Spell.

**Free (no AP):** Assess Situation (Perception), Drop Item, Signal, Speak (5 words-ish), Use Luck Point, Ward Location (passive block).

All ordinary characters have 2 AP per round; unused AP do not carry over.
