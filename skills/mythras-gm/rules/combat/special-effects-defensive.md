Available to a successful defender:

- **Arise** -- stand up from prone for free.
- **Blind Opponent** (crit) -- strike at the foe's eyes/visibility.
- **Damage Weapon** -- harm the foe's weapon.
- **Disarm** -- knock the foe's weapon away.
- **Enhance Parry** (crit) -- block all incoming damage.
- **Force Failure** -- foe fumbled; turn their attack into a failure.
- **Prepare Counter** -- set up a follow-up strike.
- **Scar Foe** -- leave a lasting mark.
- **Select Target** -- attacker fumbled; redirect the hit to a bystander.
- **Slip Free** (crit) -- escape a grapple or hold.
- **Trip** -- opposed roll or foe prone.
- **Withdraw** -- break engagement for free.
- **Circumvent Parry** (crit) -- bypass the foe's defense.
