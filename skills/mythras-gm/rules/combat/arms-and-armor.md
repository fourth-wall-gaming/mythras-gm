Armor: quilted windsilk 2 AP, lacquered bone-scale 4 AP, drake-leather 3 AP, Wardens' alloy harness 5 AP (torso only; +flight penalty). Total AP worn >=8 imposes movement penalties and the highest worn AP subtracts from Initiative. Flying with >12 total AP requires a Flight roll per scene; Alar generally armor only the torso and head.
