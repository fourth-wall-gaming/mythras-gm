Available to a successful attacker:

- **Bash** -- knockback (see dedicated piece).
- **Bleed** -- cutting; Endurance vs attack or lose 1 Fatigue/round (see dedicated piece).
- **Bypass Armor** (crit) -- ignore the location's armor.
- **Choose Location** -- pick the hit location instead of rolling.
- **Circumvent Parry** (crit) -- ignore the defender's parry.
- **Damage Weapon** -- harm the foe's weapon.
- **Disarm** -- opposed Combat Style; knock the weapon away.
- **Grip** (unarmed) -- seize and hold the target.
- **Impale** -- impaling weapons (see dedicated piece).
- **Maximize Damage** (crit, stackable) -- one damage die deals maximum.
- **Stun Location** -- bludgeoning (see dedicated piece).
- **Sunder** (2H) -- damage the armor itself.
- **Trip** -- opposed Brawn/Evade/Acrobatics or prone (see dedicated piece).
