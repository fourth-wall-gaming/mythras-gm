**Charge:** move at Run/Sprint into contact, then attack at Hard. Weapon Size counts as +1 step and the Damage Modifier is +1 step (bipeds).

**Diving attack (setting):** an Alar diving 10m or more onto a target counts as a charge with Damage Modifier +2 steps, but a miss requires an immediate Flight roll or the attacker tumbles past prone / loses 1d10m of altitude.
