**Humanoid (1d20):** 1-3 R Leg, 4-6 L Leg, 7-9 Abdomen, 10-12 Chest, 13-15 R Arm, 16-18 L Arm, 19-20 Head.

**Winged Alar / avianoid (1d20)** -- used for all Alar PCs and NPCs:

| 1d20 | Location |
|---|---|
| 1-2 | Right Leg |
| 3-4 | Left Leg |
| 5-7 | Abdomen |
| 8-10 | Chest |
| 11-12 | Right Wing |
| 13-14 | Left Wing |
| 15-16 | Right Arm |
| 17-18 | Left Arm |
| 19-20 | Head |

**Wing wounds:** A Serious Wound to a wing means no flight (failed Endurance opposed vs attack = wing useless). A flying Alar whose wing is disabled falls -- see Falling. A Major Wound to a wing while airborne is usually fatal at altitude.
