**Terrifying:** The creature's appearance, form, or aura forces viewers to make an unopposed Willpower roll:
- **Success**: shaken for one round, cannot act offensively.
- **Failure**: flee in terror.
- **Fumble**: collapse unconscious from shock.
- **Critical Success**: act unhindered.

The viewer makes no further checks for the remainder of the combat, even if more of the same creature type appear. Repeated exposure to a specific type of Terrifying monster may eventually make the skill check one or more grades easier.
