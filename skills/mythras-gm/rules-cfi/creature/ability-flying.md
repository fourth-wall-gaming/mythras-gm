**Flying:** The creature automatically succeeds in everyday moving and maneuvering whilst flying and need not roll their Fly skill unless attempting an unusually difficult task (flying against a powerful wind, evading a ranged attack in the air, or outmaneuvering an aerial foe). A flyer may substitute the Fly skill (base STR+DEX) for Evade when fighting whilst aloft.
