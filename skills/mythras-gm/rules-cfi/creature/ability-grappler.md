**Grappler:** If the creature successfully strikes in combat, it can immediately Grapple in addition to inflicting damage. If the creature's attack was parried (or the creature itself is parrying), then it is granted the Grip effect against the opponent's limb or the Pin Weapon effect against their weapon instead. The creature always uses its Brawn skill to oppose a victim breaking free.
