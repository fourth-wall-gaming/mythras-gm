**Life Sense:** In tune with the rhythms of nature, this creature can determine the vitality of any living creature by touch. On a successful Perception roll, it learns the target's current injuries, Fatigue level, and whether afflicted by any poison or disease. The creature is also aware of any form of life within Willpower skill x3 feet, which may make Stealth difficult.
