**Multi-Limbed:** Similar to Multi-Headed, the creature gains an additional Combat Action per extra pair of limbs (beyond the first) it can bring to bear during combat. Limbs normally used to hold up or move the creature do not count. These extra Combat Actions can only be used to perform actions with those specific limbs and are lost as each pair is immobilized or incapacitated.
