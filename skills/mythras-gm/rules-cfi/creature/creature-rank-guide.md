**Monster Rank** denotes the approximate challenge of the creature. It is similar but NOT equal to a character's Rank.

**Scaling rule:** Four Rank 1 creatures challenge four Rank 1 characters. Equivalent challenges:
- 12 x Rank 1/2 = 4 x Rank 1
- 4 x Rank 1 = 4 x Rank 1
- 2 x Rank 2 = 4 x Rank 1
- 1 x Rank 4 ~ 4 x Rank 1

**Rule of Thumb:** Choose monsters whose Rank is equal to the party's Rank, plus or minus one. This ensures variety (not always the same number of orcs) while keeping encounters appropriately challenging. Random dice results mean outcomes vary -- this guidance sets a reasonable starting point, not a guarantee.
