**Formidable Natural Weapons:** This creature can actively parry or deflect attacks using its natural weapons. This is due to a combination of fighting style and natural armament formed from resilient substances such as horn, chitin, or bone, which can shrug off damage from manufactured weapons. Creatures lacking this trait either rely on natural armor to turn blows or use Evade.
