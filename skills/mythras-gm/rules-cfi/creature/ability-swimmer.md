**Swimmer:** The creature automatically succeeds in everyday moving and maneuvering whilst swimming and need not roll their Swim skill unless attempting an unusually difficult task (swimming against a powerful tide, leaping up a waterfall). A swimmer may substitute the Swim skill for Athletics and Evade rolls whilst in water.
