**Cold-Blooded:** The creature does not need to eat frequently. One meal a week is sufficient; gorging on a large amount of meat removes the need to eat for a month.

Conversely, when exposed to temperatures below 60 degrees Fahrenheit, reflexes become muted: -6 to Initiative and loss of one Combat Action per round.

Below 40 degrees Fahrenheit, cold-blooded creatures become completely torpid, entering a helpless catatonic state.
