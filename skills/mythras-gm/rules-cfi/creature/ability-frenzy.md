**Frenzy:** When wounded or exposed to a particular substance (blood, for instance), the creature must succeed in a Willpower roll or enter a frenzy lasting CON rounds. During the frenzy, it must only spend Combat Actions on attacking or moving toward combat. Spellcasting, parrying, evading, and anything else is forbidden. In return, the creature no longer suffers pain or fatigue, is impervious to mental control, and is immune to all detrimental effects of Serious Wounds (a Major Wound still incapacitates). Once the frenzy ends, the creature automatically gains a Fatigue level of Exhausted.
