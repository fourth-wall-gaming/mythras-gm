**Basic Magic Items** -- prices marked * add cost of base weapon/armor.

| Item | Cost | Effect |
| :-- | :--: | :-- |
| +1 Amulet of Magica | 250 GP | Provides additional Magic Points drawable for spellcasting; a character draws from only one such item at a time; spent points don't regenerate naturally (must be refilled by a spellcaster). |
| +2 Amulet of Magica | 500 GP | As above, greater capacity. |
| +1 Armor | 500 GP* | Negates 1 additional point of damage. |
| +1 Ring of Magica | 250 GP | Functions identically to Amulet of Magica. |
| +2 Ring of Magica | 500 GP | Functions identically to Amulet of Magica, greater capacity. |
| +1 Ring of Protection | 500 GP | +1 AP while worn; +5% bonus to Resistance rolls. If worn with magical armor, only the armor bonus applies (Resistance bonus is unaffected). Does not stack with other Rings of Protection. |
| +5% Shield | 250 GP* | +5% to Combat Skill when Parrying; +5 AP and +2 HP on shield. |
| +5% Shield, +20% vs. Missiles | 500 GP* | +5% to Combat Skill when Parrying melee; +20% when Parrying missiles; +5 AP and +2 HP. |
| +10% Shield | 500 GP* | +10% to Combat Skill when Parrying; +10 AP and +4 HP. |
| +1 Weapon (melee) | 400 GP* | +1 damage; +10 AP and +4 HP to weapon. |
| +1 Weapon (ranged) | 500 GP* | +1 damage; +10 AP and +4 HP to weapon. |
| Bag of Holding | 500 GP | Pocket dimension; weighs 2 Things regardless of contents; stores up to 100 Things. |
| Elven Boots | 1,000 GP | Grants 95% Stealth regardless of terrain; no benefit beyond 95%. |
| Potion of Invisibility | 50 GP | Invisibility for 1d4+2 minutes per dose; 8 doses per typical potion; any attack ends invisibility instantly. |
| Potion of Levitation | 40 GP | Levitate vertically 10 ft/Round; can carry one person SIZ 20 or less; horizontal movement by pulling along a surface at 10 ft/Round. |
| Ring of (Spell), Rank 1 | 1,000 GP | Casts a single Rank 1 spell continuously (active when ring is worn); common spells: Detect Magic, Endure Heat or Cold, Feather Fall, Floating Disk, Invisibility, Levitation, Protection from Evil/Good, Slow Poison. Ring of Invisibility: wearer visible for 1d3+1 Rounds after attacking. |
| Wand of (Spell), Rank 1 | 1,000 GP | 90 charges; usable by all classes; 50% chance magic-user spell / 50% cleric spell; expends charges equal to normal Casting Cost per use; casts at Intensity and Magnitude 3; costs 0 Magic Points. |
| Rod of (Spell), Rank 1 | 1,500 GP | 60 charges; Intensity and Magnitude 6; only usable by a class capable of casting the spell. |
| Staff of (Spell), Rank 1 | 2,000 GP | 30 charges; Intensity and Magnitude 9; only usable by a class capable of casting the spell. |
