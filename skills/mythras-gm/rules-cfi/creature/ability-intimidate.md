**Intimidate:** The creature may intimidate opponents as a prelude to combat or to avoid it altogether: growls, snarls, lowering of horns, and so forth. Opponents must make an unopposed Willpower roll:
- **Success**: character holds his ground.
- **Failure**: character must spend the next round placing distance between themselves and the creature.
- **Fumble**: character flees at maximum speed.
- **Critical Success**: character is immune to further intimidation attempts by the creature or its brethren for the remainder of the encounter.

The effect continues for as long as the creature acts in a threatening manner, including making an attack.
