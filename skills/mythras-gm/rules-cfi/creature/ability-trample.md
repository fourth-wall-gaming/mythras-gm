**Trample:** The creature can trample beings with a SIZ of half or less than its own, using its Athletics skill to attack. This inflicts damage equal to twice the creature's base Damage Modifier and increases the Size of the attack by one step.

- If immobile, the creature may trample a prone opponent on its turn (costs an Action Point).
- If performed whilst the creature is moving or charging over an opponent, the trample is a Free Action.
