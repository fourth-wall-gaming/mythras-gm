**Engulfing:** The creature has an orifice which can swallow targets whole. The maximum size of an engulfed victim is half the creature's own SIZ (may be less depending on physiology). When engulfed, the victim suffers at least one damage roll based on the creature's bite attack before being swallowed. If still alive, a swallowed victim begins to suffocate, likely dying by asphyxiation before suffering noticeable damage from stomach acid. An engulfed victim lacks the mobility to cut their way out in any realistic timeframe.
