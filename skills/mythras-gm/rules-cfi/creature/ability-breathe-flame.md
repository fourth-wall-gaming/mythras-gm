**Breathe Flame:** The creature breathes flame over an area as a Combat Action. The flame covers a cone in front of the creature stretching CON x5 feet. At the furthest extent, the width of the cone equals its length. Any creature caught in the flame suffers fire-based damage to all hit locations; a character can attempt to Evade to halve this damage. Armor Points count as normal unless otherwise noted.

A creature can only breathe flame once in a specified time period (usually once per hour or once per day). Further attempts within this time require the creature to pass Endurance rolls with each roll becoming one grade harder, or suffer a level of Fatigue.
