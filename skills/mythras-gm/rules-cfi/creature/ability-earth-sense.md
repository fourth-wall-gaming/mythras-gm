**Earth Sense:** Some creatures are so in tune with the underground world that they can work perfectly well in total darkness. By sensing vibration and air pressure, a creature with Earth Sense can fight and make Perception rolls with no penalty as long as their target is no more than INS x5 feet away. If using Earth Sense above ground, the range is halved.
