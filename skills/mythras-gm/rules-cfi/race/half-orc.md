**Half-orcs** originate in wild frontier regions where orcs and humans conflict. They exhibit more orcish traits than human: taller than average human, large prominent teeth, extended lower jaw, greenish skin covered with coarse dark hair.

**Lifespan:** ~80 years.

**Personality:** Warlike by nature; little interest in refined pursuits.

**Culture:** No native lands. Most often live among orcish tribes; others move toward human lands. Generally not accepted by society; drawn toward violent careers and adventuring. Adopt the culture of whichever race raised them.

**Relations:** Poor terms with most races due to orcish blood; greatest enmity with elves and dwarves. Half-elves are most sympathetic (understand outcasts). Gnomes and halflings tend to be accepting (gnomes: half-orcs take jokes; halflings: curious natures).

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40, Language (Orc) +40
- Standard Skills: Choose from a human culture OR from the orc list: Athletics, Brawn, Endurance, First Aid, Locale, Perception; plus either Boating, Evade, or Ride
- Professional Skills: Choose from a human culture OR from the orc list: Craft (any), Healing, Intimidation, Lore (any), Musicianship, Navigate, Survival, Track

**Alignment:** Typically Lawful Evil if raised by orcs (or as per human parent).
- Lawful traits: Adherence to tradition, Calculating, Closed-Minded, Lack of adaptability.
- Evil traits: Abusive, Cruel, Domineering, Enjoys Harming Innocents, Hates Good, Merciless, Sadistic, Slaver, Spiteful.

**Typical Passions (raised as orc):** Loyalty to Clan Chieftain; Love (friend, sibling, or romantic lover); Hate (magic, creature, rival, or clan). Raised as human: choose from appropriate human list.

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 20 feet.
- **Darkvision:** See up to 60 feet in dim light as bright light (standard Perception rolls). See in darkness as dim light (Perception rolls Hard). Colors appear as shades of gray in darkness. Does not work in magical darkness.
- **Illiterate (if raised by orcs):** Cannot read or write. To learn: spend 1 Experience Roll + 1 month of training for basic literacy (half skill level in one known language). A further 1 Experience Roll + 1 month gives full literacy. New languages learned in play are assumed at full literacy. If raised by a literate culture, they are literate.
- **Survival Bonus (Specific, if raised by orcs):** All Survival rolls for area of origin are one grade easier.
