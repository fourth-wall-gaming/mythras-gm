**Primitive** humans live closest to the land in extended family groups. Dwellings range from caves to simple huts. They are typically hunter-gatherers with few or poorly developed farming systems. Cultural beliefs and superstitions can be strongly defined. Few primitive cultures have written language, though paintings and pictorial symbols serve as communication.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40
- Standard Skills: Brawn, Endurance, Evade, Locale, Perception, Stealth; plus one of: Athletics, Boating, First Aid, or Swim
- Professional Skills: Craft (any), Healing, Lore (any), Language (any), Musicianship, Navigate, Survival, Track

**Alignment:** Any -- one or two from Ethical Code (Lawful/Neutral/Chaotic) and one or two from Moral Code (Good/Neutral/Evil); or one or two True Neutral.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to Chief/Headman; Love (friend, sibling, or romantic lover); Hate (something that scares or intimidates you).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 20 feet.
- **Lucky:** +1 Luck Point.
- **Survival Bonus (Specific):** All Survival rolls for area of origin are one grade easier.
- **Illiterate:** Cannot read or write. To learn: spend 1 Experience Roll + 1 month of training to gain basic literacy (half skill level in one known language). A further 1 Experience Roll + 1 month gives full literacy. New languages learned in play are assumed at full literacy.
