**Barbarian** humans are tribal in nature, occupying distinct wilderness territories. Kinship and clan bonds are strong.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40
- Standard Skills: Athletics, Brawn, Endurance, First Aid, Locale, Perception; plus either Boating or Ride
- Professional Skills: Craft (any), Healing, Language (Any), Lore (any), Musicianship, Navigate, Seamanship, Survival, Track

**Alignment:** Any -- one or two from Ethical Code (Lawful/Neutral/Chaotic) and one or two from Moral Code (Good/Neutral/Evil); or one or two True Neutral.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to Clan Chieftain; Love (friend, sibling, or romantic lover); Hate (magic, creature, rival, or clan).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 20 feet.
- **Lucky:** +1 Luck Point.
- **Survival Bonus (Specific):** All Survival rolls for area of origin are one grade easier.
- **Exposure Tolerance:** Reduced effects from temperature extremes of origin area. Lightly outfitted: grace period = CON in hours before exposure sets in. Suitable clothing: double CON in hours. After that, must seek shelter or suffer Fatigue loss.
- **Illiterate:** Cannot read or write. To learn: spend 1 Experience Roll + 1 month of training to gain basic literacy (half skill level in one known language). A further 1 Experience Roll + 1 month gives full literacy. New languages learned in play are assumed at full literacy.
