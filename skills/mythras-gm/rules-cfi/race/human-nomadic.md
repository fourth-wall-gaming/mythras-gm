**Nomadic** humans have no fixed home, moving constantly to follow animal migrations, herd domesticated beasts, or drift across oceans. They waste nothing and subsist on what they find.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40
- Standard Skills: Endurance, First Aid, Locale, Perception, Stealth; plus two of: Athletics, Boating, Swim, Drive, or Ride (depending on primary travel mode)
- Professional Skills: Craft (any), Culture (any), Healing, Language (any), Lore (any), Musicianship, Navigate, Survival, Track

**Alignment:** Any -- one or two from Ethical Code (Lawful/Neutral/Chaotic) and one or two from Moral Code (Good/Neutral/Evil); or one or two True Neutral.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to Tribal Chieftain/Khan; Love (friend, sibling, or romantic lover); Hate (creature, rival, or tribe).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 20 feet.
- **Lucky:** +1 Luck Point.
- **Survival Bonus (Specific):** All Survival rolls for area of origin are one grade easier.
- **Literate:** Can read and write any language they can speak (interaction with many cultures teaches this).
