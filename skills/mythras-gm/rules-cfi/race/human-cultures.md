Humans must choose one of four cultures to define their background. All human cultures share:
- **Free Skills:** Customs +40, Language (Common) +40
- **Movement:** 20 feet
- **Lucky:** +1 Luck Point at character creation
- **Language note:** Humans are not limited in language selection during character creation if the choice can be justified.

| Culture | Key Identity |
|---------|-------------|
| Barbarian | Tribal, wilderness, strong kinship ties |
| Civilized | Urban, literate, bureaucratic, cosmopolitan |
| Nomadic | Migratory, scavengers, subsistence travel |
| Primitive | Hunter-gatherer, closest to nature, pre-literate |

See individual culture pieces for full skill lists, alignment, passions, and special rules.
