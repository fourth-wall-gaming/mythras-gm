**Half-elves** are the offspring of an elf and a human. Their appearance leans elven; they tend to be attractive. They possess their human parent's wanderlust and their elven parent's love of nature.

**Lifespan:** ~300 years.

**Society:** No communities of their own. Live wherever humans and elves co-mingle. Adopt the culture of whichever race raised them (human culture if raised by humans; elven culture if raised by elves). Often receive the greatest bigotry from elves despite being generally accepted by humans and elves alike.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40, Language (Elf) +40
- Standard Skills: Choose from a human culture OR from the elf list: Conceal, Influence, Insight, Locale, Perception, Stealth, Willpower
- Professional Skills: Choose from a human culture OR from the elf list: Art (any), Commerce, Craft (any), Courtesy, Language (any*), Lore (any), Musicianship, Survival
- *If raised by elves: gnome, halfling, goblin, hobgoblin, orc, gnoll only. If raised by humans: no language restriction.

**Alignment:** Typically Chaotic Good if raised by elves (or as per human parent).
- Chaotic traits: Curious, Freedom, Suspicious.
- Good traits: Admirable, Altruistic, Angelic, Charitable, Compunctions against harming innocents, Decent, Ethical, Forgiving, Friendly, Hates Evil, Helpful, Kind, Loves nature, Merciful, Optimistic, Positive, Vegetarian.

**Typical Passions (raised as elf):** Loyalty to King/Queen; Love (Something emotional); Hate (any enemy of grace and beauty). Raised as human: choose from appropriate human list.

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 20 feet.
- **Elven Chain:** Can cast Arcane Magic spells while wearing elven chain; casting difficulty is one level higher.
- **Literate:** Can read and write any language they can speak.
- **Resistance to Sleep and Charm:** Willpower rolls to resist Sleep and Charm spells (and all Enchantment/Charm school spells: Charm Person, Friendship, Hypnotism, etc.) are 1 grade easier (note: elves get 2 grades; half-elves get only 1). They may voluntarily drop this resistance.
- **Sharp Vision:** All Perception rolls to spot something are one grade easier.
- **Stealthy:** When wearing nothing more restrictive than light armor, Stealth tests are 1 grade easier.
