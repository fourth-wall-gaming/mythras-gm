**Gnomes** dwell in mines and burrows in rolling hills and woodland areas, away from humans. They are smaller than dwarves but less rotund.

**Appearance:** 3'4" to 3'7" tall. Brown or tan skin. White hair. Considerably larger noses than dwarves.

**Lifespan:** ~700 years.

**Personality:** Uncontrollable tendency for practical jokes; keen sense of humor (sometimes dangerous). Love of nature almost equaling that of elves. Love gems and jewelry; many are expert gem-cutters. Suspicious of larger races -- test strangers' intentions with humor. Expert tellers of tall-tales and wild stories.

**Culture:** Clannish, organized under a chief with cleric advisors for religious matters. Mining and crafting fine jewelry are primary activities. Love of games and practical jokes distinguishes them from dwarves. Good relations with dwarves, elves, and other woodland beings.

**Relations:** Best with dwarves (kin). Generally more accepting of others than dwarves. Suspicious of large races.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40, Language (Gnome) +40
- Standard Skills: Deceit, Evade, Insight, Locale, Perception, Stealth, Willpower
- Professional Skills: Commerce, Craft (any), Engineering, Language (any*), Lore (any), Mechanisms, Oratory, Survival
- *Language limits: dwarf, halfling, goblin, and kobold only at character creation.

**Alignment:** Typically Neutral Good.
- Neutral traits: Does not strongly lean toward Law or Chaos; Flexible; Freedom of action; Respectful of nature.
- Good traits: Admirable, Altruistic, Angelic, Charitable, Compunctions against harming innocents, Decent, Ethical, Forgiving, Friendly, Hates Evil, Helpful, Kind, Loves nature, Merciful, Optimistic, Positive, Vegetarian.
- Note: Choose either Respectful of nature OR Loves nature -- not both.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to Clan; Love (Something Materialistic); Hate (typically kobolds and goblins).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 15 feet.
- **Literate:** Can read and write any language they can speak.
- **Darkvision:** See up to 60 feet in dim light as bright light (standard Perception rolls). See in darkness as dim light (Perception rolls Hard). Colors appear as shades of gray in darkness. Does not work in magical darkness.
- **Magic Resistance:** Willpower Checks to resist Arcane Magic are one grade easier; two grades easier vs. illusions. No effect on Divine magic. Many gnome magic-users specialize in the School of Illusion.
- **Poison Resistance:** Endurance rolls related to poisoning are 1 grade easier.
- **Tunnel Sense:** Easy Perception roll detects underground phenomena within 10 feet: stonework pits, deadfalls, and traps; slope or grade; approximate depth underground; new tunnel or passage construction; shifting or sliding walls or rooms.
