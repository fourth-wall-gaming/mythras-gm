**Humans** are the most widespread and adaptive race in the setting.

- **Lifespan:** Close to 100 years, rarely exceeds naturally.
- **Culture:** Humans choose one of four cultures (Barbarian, Civilized, Nomadic, Primitive) instead of having a fixed racial culture.
- **Lucky:** All humans start with one additional Luck Point.
- **Movement:** 20 feet (all cultures).

Humans are considered the chosen race of many gods. Other races note human population growth outpaces demi-human decline. Humans can have contact with -- and be allies or enemies of -- any species.
