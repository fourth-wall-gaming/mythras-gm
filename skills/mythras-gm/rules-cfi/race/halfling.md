**Halflings** are peaceful, civilized, largely agrarian folk. Despite being smaller and weaker than most races, they compensate with nimbleness and, when pressed, surprising courage.

**Appearance:** 4'0" to 4'3" tall -- shorter than dwarves, taller than gnomes. Pointed ears. Normal range of skin, hair, and eye colors. Large, rugged, oversized and hairy feet. They shun shoes; their thick-soled feet protect against rocks and stones.

**Lifespan:** ~170 years.

**Personality:** Inquisitive, love secrets and stories. Generally unadventurous and prosaic; prefer not to interfere with the wider world. Nimble; cautious but not cowardly. Can fight fiercely for home and way of life.

**Culture:** Akin to Civilized. Live in small communities called shires (closely-knit villages and small towns), with extended families forming the community base. Industries: agriculture, pottery, weaving, leatherworking, textiles. Work metals rarely and mostly not for weapons/armor. Cultural unit: the Riding (three shires); three Ridings form a Borough.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40, Language (Halfling) +40
- Standard Skills: Dance or Sing, Evade, Insight, Locale, Perception, Stealth, Willpower
- Professional Skills: Art (any), Commerce, Craft (any), Courtesy, Language (any*), Lore (any), Musicianship, Streetwise
- *Language limits: dwarf, elf, gnome, goblin, and orc only at character creation.

**Alignment:** Typically Lawful Good.
- Lawful traits: Honest, Honorable, Reliable, Trusting, Trustworthy, Well-mannered.
- Good traits: Admirable, Altruistic, Charitable, Compunctions against harming innocents, Decent, Ethical, Forgiving, Friendly, Hates Evil, Helpful, Kind, Loves nature, Merciful, Optimistic, Positive, Vegetarian.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to (Shire, Riding, or Borough); Love (Something emotional); Hate (Heavy Industry).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 15 feet.
- **Literate:** Can read and write any language they can speak.
- **Magic Resistance:** Willpower Checks to resist Arcane Magic are one grade easier. No effect on Divine magic.
- **Poison Resistance:** Endurance rolls related to poisoning are one grade easier.
- **Stealthy:** When wearing nothing more restrictive than light armor, Stealth tests are 1 grade easier.
- **Exposure Tolerance (Feet):** Halflings suffer no adverse effects from exposure when barefoot, regardless of temperature. Exposure affects the rest of the body normally.
