**Dwarves** make their homes in mountain strongholds and underground tunnels. They are strong, tough, and untiring in battle.

**Appearance:** 4'8" to 4'10" tall, stocky. Hair: black, gray, dark brown, or occasionally red. Skin: ruddy or earth-colored. Male dwarves favor long beards (spiked, braided, or forked); some dwarven women also grow beards.

**Lifespan:** ~450 years.

**Culture:** Barbarian-like clan culture in underground halls and cities. Gender-equal: males and females share all duties. Individual needs subordinated to the clan. Acquisitive and materialistic -- jealously guard discoveries. Lone dwarves can become selfish without clan structure.

**Relations:** Best with gnomes (distant kin, though their humor is annoying). Acceptable relations with humans. Low tolerance of elves (ancient wars). Dislike halflings (lifestyle clash). Legendary hatred of orcs.

**Magic:** Distinct aversion to Arcane magic; resistant to it. Divine magic affects them normally. Dwarven magic-users are unheard of.

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40, Language (Dwarf) +40
- Standard Skills: Athletics, Brawn, Endurance, Evade, Locale, Perception, Willpower
- Professional Skills: Commerce, Craft (any), Engineering, Language (any*), Lore (any), Mechanisms, Oratory, Survival
- *Language limits: gnome, goblin, kobold, and orc only at character creation.

**Alignment:** Typically Lawful Good.
- Lawful traits: Closed-Minded, Hates Chaos, Honest, Honorable, Judgmental, No sense of humor, Obedience to legitimate authority, Organized, Predictable, Reliable, Trustworthy.
- Good traits: Admirable, Compunctions against harming innocents, Decent, Ethical, Friendly, Hates Evil, Helpful, Kind, Optimistic, Positive.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to Clan; Love (Something Materialistic); Hill Dwarves: Hate (Orcs, half-orcs, goblins, hobgoblins); Mountain Dwarves: Hate (Orcs, goblins, evil giants, dark elves).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 15 feet.
- **Darkvision:** See up to 60 feet in dim light as bright light (standard Perception rolls). See in darkness as dim light (Perception rolls Hard). Colors appear as shades of gray in darkness. Does not work in magical darkness.
- **Literate:** Can read and write any language they can speak.
- **Magic Resistance:** Willpower Checks to resist Arcane Magic are one grade easier. No effect on Divine magic.
- **Poison Resistance:** Endurance rolls related to poisoning are one grade easier.
- **Tunnel Sense:** Easy Perception roll detects underground phenomena within 10 feet: stonework pits, deadfalls, and traps; slope or grade; approximate depth underground; new tunnel or passage construction; shifting or sliding walls or rooms.
