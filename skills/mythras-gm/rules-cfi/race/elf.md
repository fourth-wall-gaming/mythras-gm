**Elves** inhabit woodland realms. Few leave for the adventuring life. They are graceful and slender, approximately human height.

**Appearance:** Fine bone structure, narrow tapering skulls, narrow pointed ears. Eye colors range from human tones to brilliant gem hues.

**Lifespan:** ~1,100 years. Long before this, many elves feel compelled to depart the realms of men -- destination unknown to outsiders.

**Society:** Frequently monarchical (king, queen, or both). Otherworldly and remote yet perceptive. Nature is paramount; they preserve their environments. Do not perceive time like short-lived races.

**Culture:** Akin to Civilized. Great store placed on learning, deeds, conduct, and integrity. Dignified manners expected; loutish behavior viewed with disdain. Loyalty to the monarch is paramount.

**Relations:** Reasonably well with halflings and gnomes. Poorly with dwarves (gruff nature). Suspicion of humans. Despise orcs and goblins (ancient animosity, endless wars).

**Skills:**
- Free Skills: Customs +40, Language (Common) +40, Language (Elf) +40
- Standard Skills: Conceal, Influence, Insight, Locale, Perception, Stealth, Willpower
- Professional Skills: Art (any), Commerce, Craft (any), Courtesy, Language (any*), Lore (any), Musicianship, Survival
- *Language limits: gnome, halfling, goblin, hobgoblin, orc, and gnoll only at character creation.

**Alignment:** Typically Chaotic Good.
- Chaotic traits: Curious, Freedom, Suspicious.
- Good traits: Admirable, Altruistic, Angelic, Charitable, Compunctions against harming innocents, Decent, Ethical, Forgiving, Friendly, Hates Evil, Helpful, Kind, Loves nature, Merciful, Optimistic, Positive, Vegetarian.

**Typical Passions:** Loyalty to King/Queen; Love (Something emotional); Hate (any enemy of grace and beauty).

**Special Rules:**
- **Movement:** 20 feet.
- **Elven Chain:** Elves can cast Arcane Magic spells while wearing elven chain; casting difficulty is one level higher.
- **Literate:** Can read and write any language they can speak.
- **Resistance to Sleep and Charm:** Willpower rolls to resist Sleep and Charm spells (and all Enchantment/Charm school spells: Charm Person, Friendship, Hypnotism, etc.) are 2 grades easier. Elves may voluntarily drop this resistance if they choose.
- **Sharp Vision:** All Perception rolls to spot something are one grade easier.
- **Stealthy:** When wearing nothing more restrictive than light armor, Stealth tests are 1 grade easier.
