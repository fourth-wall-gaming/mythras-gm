**Cultures and Races** determine a character's starting skills, alignment, passions, and special rules.

**Humans** choose a separate culture (Barbarian, Civilized, Nomadic, or Primitive) for additional granularity. Demi-human races are less widespread and have a single, unified culture subsumed under their race entry.

**Demi-human creation:** Use the race-specific characteristic dice (resulting in different values and ranges). All other steps -- Attributes, Class, etc. -- proceed normally.

**Applying bonuses:** Add Free Skill bonuses immediately. Add listed Standard Skill bonuses. Add Professional Skills to the Professional Skills section of the character sheet. Where a range of skills is offered (pick one or more), players choose to differentiate characters of the same racial background.

Each race also lists a typical **Alignment** and **Passions** (see Chapter 4).
