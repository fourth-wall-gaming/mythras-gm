Every CFI character is defined by seven Characteristics:

- **STR (Strength)** -- physical strength; how much one can lift, how hard one can hit. Component of Damage Modifier. At 0: cannot move or lift objects.
- **CON (Constitution)** -- health and hardiness. Component of Hit Points and Healing Rate. At 0: character dies.
- **SIZ (Size)** -- mass; indicates height and weight. Affects Hit Points and Damage Modifier. Larger mass increases force of a blow.
- **DEX (Dexterity)** -- agility, balance, reflexes. Important for Initiative. At 0: functional paralysis.
- **INT (Intelligence)** -- cognitive ability. Factor in Initiative. At 0: character becomes completely mindless.
- **POW (Power)** -- soul, spirit, inner drive, capacity for magic. Governs Magic Points and Luck Points. At 0: loses all independent will.
- **CHA (Charisma)** -- personality, independent of physical appearance. Affects Experience Modifier. At 0: cannot socially interact; ignored or driven away.
