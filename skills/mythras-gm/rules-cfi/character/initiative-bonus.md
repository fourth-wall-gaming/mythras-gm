**Initiative** = (DEX + INT) / 2 (average of the two Characteristics).

Initiative acts as a modifier to Initiative rolls in combat -- the higher the result, the faster the character responds. Additional factors such as armor may further modify Initiative. See the Combat chapter for full Initiative rules.
