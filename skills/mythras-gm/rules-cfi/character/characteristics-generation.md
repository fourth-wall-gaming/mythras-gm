**Base dice (humans):** Roll 3d6 for STR, CON, DEX, POW, CHA; roll 2d6+6 for SIZ and INT.

## Methods

**Dice Roll:** Roll each as shown in the Racial Characteristics Table and take the result as rolled.

**Dice Roll, Assign:** Roll as above, then swap values between Characteristics that share the same dice range (e.g. swap among STR/CON/DEX/POW/CHA which share 3d6; or swap SIZ and INT which share 2d6+6). Cannot swap across different dice-range groups.

**Dice Roll, High:** Roll one extra die for each Characteristic and drop the lowest result from each.

**Points Build:**
1. Start with the racial average for each Characteristic (shown in parentheses in the Racial Characteristics Table).
2. Spend 10 extra points (humans) or 6 extra points (demi-humans) to increase Characteristics.
3. May lower a Characteristic to gain extra points (e.g. reduce STR 11 to 9 to gain 2 points).
4. Characteristics cannot go below the minimum or above the maximum possible for their dice range.
