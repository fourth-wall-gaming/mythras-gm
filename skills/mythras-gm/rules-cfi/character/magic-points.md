**Magic Points = POW** (the character's Power score).

Magic Points are consumed when casting spells; the cost depends on the type of magic used. When Magic Points reach 0, the character cannot cast any spells until they recover. Availability of magic may be limited by campaign setting or class -- some characters will find this attribute superfluous.
