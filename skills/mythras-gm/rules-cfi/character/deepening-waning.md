**Deepening and Waning** -- Alignment, Passions, and Oaths change outside normal Experience Rolls when significant events occur.

- **Deepening:** A Passion increases when events strongly reinforce it.
- **Waning:** A Passion decreases -- or reverses -- when events undermine it. The GM decides whether to reduce or reverse; reversal flips the Passion (e.g., "Trust Chieftain" becomes "Distrust Chieftain" at the same percentage).

**Deepening and Waning Table**

| Depth of Event | Passion Modified by |
| :-- | :-- |
| Weak | 1d10 |
| Moderate | 1d10+5 |
| Strong | 1d10+10 |

The GM determines which row applies based on the strength of what occurred.
