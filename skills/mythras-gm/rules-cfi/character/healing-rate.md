**Healing Rate** determines natural recovery speed after injuries. The rate specifies Hit Points recovered per day, week, or month depending on the severity of the wound.

| CON | Healing Rate |
|:---:|:------------:|
| 6 or less | 1 |
| 7-12 | 2 |
| 13-18 | 3 |
| Each 6 points above 18 | +1 |
