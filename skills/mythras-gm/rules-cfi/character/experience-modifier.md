A character's **CHA** score may adjust the number of Experience Rolls available, reflecting relationships with peers and reputation in the community.

| CHA | Experience Modifier |
|:---:|:-------------------:|
| 6 or less | -1 |
| 7-12 | +0 |
| 13-18 | +1 |
| Each 6 points above 18 | +1 |
