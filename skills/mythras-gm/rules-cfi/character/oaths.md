**Oaths** are formal commitments sworn to an organization or order (e.g., a religious order for clerics, a thieves' guild for rogues). They are mechanically identical to Passions in every respect.

**Mechanical treatment:**
- An Oath is treated exactly like a Passion for all game mechanic purposes (augmentation, Standard rolls, Opposed rolls, resistance to manipulation).
- Starting value: use the Passion Table row for "An organization or group of people" = POW + INT, then apply a starting bonus (+40, +30, or +20 based on order of importance).
- Oaths can deepen or wane using the Deepening and Waning table.

**Required Oaths:**
- **Cleric:** Must take an Oath to their religious order. Maintaining the Oath is required to retain Class Abilities.
- **Rogue (thieves' guild):** Must take an Oath of Loyalty to their guild. Maintaining the Oath is required to retain guild-related Class Abilities.

**Taking an Oath during play:**
- A lord, chief, or authority calling for an Oath establishes a 'Loyalty to X' Passion immediately when sworn.
- Costs NO Experience Roll -- established at Base Value on the spot.
