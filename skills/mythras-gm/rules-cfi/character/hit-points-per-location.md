Add **CON + SIZ** and cross-reference the table below to find Hit Points for each location.

Humans have 7 locations: head, chest, abdomen, left arm, right arm, left leg, right leg.

All characters gain **+1 Hit Point in each location** at Rank 2 and again at Rank 4.

| Location | 1-5 | 6-10 | 11-15 | 16-20 | 21-25 | 26-30 | 31-35 | 36-40 | +5 |
|:---------|:---:|:----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:--:|
| Head     | 1   | 2    | 3     | 4     | 5     | 6     | 7     | 8     | +1 |
| Chest    | 3   | 4    | 5     | 6     | 7     | 8     | 9     | 10    | +1 |
| Abdomen  | 2   | 3    | 4     | 5     | 6     | 7     | 8     | 9     | +1 |
| Each Arm | 1   | 1    | 2     | 3     | 4     | 5     | 6     | 7     | +1 |
| Each Leg | 1   | 2    | 3     | 4     | 5     | 6     | 7     | 8     | +1 |

When a location's HP fall to a critical level, it becomes useless or worse. See Damage and Wounds for consequences.
