**Luck Points** represent fate, karma, or good fortune. They may be spent to:

- Re-roll unfavorable dice.
- Mitigate physical damage or other unfortunate circumstances.
- Gain an edge at a vital moment in combat.

Once spent, Luck Points are gone until the next game session (unless the GM makes an impromptu award).

| POW | Luck Points |
|:---:|:-----------:|
| 6 or less | 1 |
| 7-12 | 2 |
| 13-18 | 3 |
| Each 6 points above 18 | +1 |

**Special:** Humans start with one additional Luck Point above the table result. All characters gain +1 Luck Point each time they attain a new Rank.
