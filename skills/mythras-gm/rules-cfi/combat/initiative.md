**Initiative** is rolled once at the start of a fight and persists until a Special Effect or Action forces a re-roll.

**Roll:** 1d10 + Initiative Modifier.

Highest result acts first, then descending order. Ties: higher DEX goes first. If still tied, each roll again -- higher roll wins.

**Armor penalty:** The highest Armor Point value of armor *worn* by a character is subtracted as a penalty to their Initiative roll.

**Unused Action Points** do not carry over between Rounds.
