**Impale** (offensive; impaling weapons only).

Roll weapon damage twice, attacker chooses the better result.

**Leaving weapon in:** If the weapon penetrates armor and causes a wound, attacker may leave it in. The victim suffers a Difficulty Grade penalty to all future skill attempts based on creature SIZ and weapon size (see table below). Multiple impalements with same-sized weapons impose no additional penalty.

**Withdrawing weapon (melee only):** Ready Weapon Action. Unopposed Brawn roll (or Opposed if target resists).
- **Success:** Weapon removed; half normal damage (no Damage Modifier) to same location; armor does not reduce withdrawal damage.
- **Failure:** Weapon stays; may try again next Turn.
- Barbed weapons (harpoons) inflict normal damage on withdrawal.
- While impaled, the weapon cannot be used to Parry.

**Impale Effects Table (Difficulty Grade penalty):**

| Creature SIZ | Small | Medium | Large | Huge | Enormous |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1-10 | Formidable | Herculean | Incapacitated | Incapacitated | Incapacitated |
| 11-20 | Hard | Formidable | Herculean | Incapacitated | Incapacitated |
| 21-30 | No Effect | Hard | Formidable | Herculean | Incapacitated |
| 31-40 | No Effect | No Effect | Hard | Formidable | Herculean |
| 41-50 | No Effect | No Effect | No Effect | Hard | Formidable |
| Each +10 | Follow table progression | | | | |

**Ranged impale:** Impale Size column (separate from Force) determines the penalty grade.
