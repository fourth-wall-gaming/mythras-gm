**Uses per Short Rest (1 hour):**
- Rank 1: 1 use
- Rank 3: 2 uses
- Rank 5: 3 uses

**Turning Power:** 1d6+2, plus +2 per Rank beyond 1 (e.g., Rank 2 = 1d6+4). Record on character sheet.

**Range:** 30 feet. **Trigger:** Opposed skill test -- Channel vs. the highest Willpower among affected undead.

**Results (Good/Neutral clerics):**

- **Critical Success:** Twice Turning Power in Intensity undead affected. If cleric's Rank >= twice the Intensity of a non-sapient undead, it is destroyed outright.
- **Success:** Turning Power in Intensity of undead repulsed (lowest Intensity first). Repulsed undead must move and stay 20+ ft away for 12 Rounds (1 minute). Cannot attack the cleric's party within AoE unless forced within 10 ft. Cornered undead cower (attacks against them 1 grade easier). Sapient undead get a Hard Willpower roll each Round to break the effect. If cleric's Rank >= twice a non-sapient undead's Intensity, it is destroyed.
- **Failure:** No undead Turned.
- **Fumble:** No Turning, and undead equal to twice Turning Power in Intensity are drawn to target the cleric (lowest Intensity first).

**Evil clerics** may choose to **Command** (control) instead of Turn:
- **Success:** Command Turning Power in Intensity; can issue orders but undead are not fully subservient. Sapient undead will not throw away their unlife needlessly. No effect on Intensity 7+ undead.
- **Critical Success:** Command twice Turning Power in Intensity for 12 Rounds; OR Enslave Turning Power in Intensity (fully subservient until control broken). No effect on Intensity 7+ undead.

**Approaching within 10 ft** of Turned undead breaks the effect (for non-ranged attacks). Ranged attacks from 10+ ft do not break Turn on non-sapient undead; any attack breaks Turn on sapient undead.
