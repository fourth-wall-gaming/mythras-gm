**Sharpshooter** (offensive; Ranged weapons only).

Move the Hit Location struck by one step to an immediately adjacent body area. Common sense and physiology apply.

Examples:
- Humanoid leg hit -> can be moved to abdomen.
- Griffin chest hit -> can be moved to foreleg, wing, or head.
