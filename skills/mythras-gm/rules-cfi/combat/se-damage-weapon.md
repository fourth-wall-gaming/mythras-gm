**Damage Weapon** (offensive or defensive).

The character aims at the opponent's weapon rather than the wielder. Damage is rolled and applied against the **weapon's own Armor Points**. If the weapon's Hit Points are reduced to zero, it breaks.

When attacking: damage roll targets the defender's Parrying weapon.
When defending: damage roll targets the attacker's weapon.
