**Blind Opponent** (defensive; requires Defender Critical).

The defender throws sand, reflects sunlight off a shield, or uses another tactic to interfere with the attacker's vision. The attacker must make an Opposed Roll of Evade (or Combat Skill if using a shield) vs. the defender's original Parry roll.

If the attacker **Fails**, they suffer a situational modifier of **Hard or Formidable** for the next **1d3 Turns**, depending on the blinding method.
