**Trip Opponent** (offensive or defensive).

The character attempts to overbalance or throw the opponent to the ground.

**Resolution:** Opposed Roll -- character's original roll vs. the target's choice of Brawn, Evade, or Acrobatics.
- **Target Fails:** They fall prone.

**Quadruped exception:** Opponents with four or more legs may substitute their Athletics skill for Evade and treat the roll as one Difficulty Grade easier.
