**Accidental Injury** (defensive; requires attacker Fumble).

The defender deflects or twists the attack so that the attacker injures themselves. The attacker rolls weapon damage against themselves in a random Hit Location. If the attacker is unarmed, they tear or break something internal -- the damage roll **ignores armor**.
