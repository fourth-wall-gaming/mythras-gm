**Sunder** (offensive; two-handed weapons only; suitable weapon such as an axe required).

The attacker damages the armor or natural protection of an opponent.

**Damage resolution:**
1. Any weapon damage, after reductions for Parrying or magic, is applied against the Armor Point (AP) value of the protection at the struck location.
2. Surplus damage over the protection's AP reduces the AP value of that armor(ed) location (ripping straps, bursting rings, creasing plates, or tearing away hide/scales/chitin of monsters).
3. If any damage remains after the protection is reduced to zero AP, it carries over onto the Hit Points of the location struck.
