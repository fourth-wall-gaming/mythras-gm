**Bleed** (offensive; cutting weapons only).

The attack cuts open a major blood vessel.

**Trigger:** The blow must overcome Armor Points and injure the target.

**Resistance:** Defender makes an Opposed Roll of Endurance against the original attack roll.
- **Defender Fails:** They begin to bleed profusely.

**Bleed cadence:** At the start of each Combat Round, the bleeding recipient loses one level of Fatigue, continuing until they collapse and possibly die.

**Stopping the bleed:** A successful First Aid skill roll staunches the wound. However, once staunched, the recipient can no longer perform any strenuous or violent action without re-opening the wound.
