**Trigger:** Damage (before Parry or armor reductions) exceeds the recipient's SIZ.

**Effects:**
- Recipient must pass an **Easy Acrobatics** or **Standard Athletics** roll to avoid falling prone.
- Pushed back **5 feet for each 8 points of damage** (or fraction thereof) over SIZ.

**Mitigation:** Proactive use of the **Brace** Action (before the blow) can reduce or negate knockback. SIZ is treated as 50% bigger vs. Knockback/Leaping; doubled vs. Bash specifically.
