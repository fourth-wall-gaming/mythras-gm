**Starting abilities (Rank 1, all known by default):**

- **Combat Proficiency:** +5% to Combat Skill and Unarmed.
- **Weapon Specialization:** Choose one weapon (or shield):
  - *Melee:* +5% Combat Skill with that weapon (does not aid Rank Advancement) + one bonus Parry per exchange (no Action Point cost).
  - *Ranged:* Auto-fire first before Initiative roll if unsurprised with readied weapon; aiming within Close Range takes 1 Turn (not a full Round) and grants 1 grade easier; reload time reduced by 1.
  - *Shields:* +10% Combat Skill with all shields (does not aid Rank Advancement); all shields count 1 Size category larger for Parrying damage.

**Rank 1 Ranked Abilities (1 XP each):**
- **Forceful Strike** (Brawn + Combat Skill 50%+): Reduce attack 1 grade -> Damage Modifier +2 grades.
- **Just a Scratch** (Endurance 50%+): Once/day after 1-hour rest, regain Healing Rate HP in one location (no effect on Major Wounds).
- **Mounted Combat** (Ride 50%+): Combat Skill not capped by Ride; mounted charge no penalty to hit; ignore first Ride penalty in combat.
- **Second Wind** (Endurance 50%+): Once/day after 1-hour rest, recover up to 3 lost Fatigue levels.
- **Skirmishing** (Athletics + Combat Skill 50%+): Fire ranged weapon while using Hustle (Run only, not Sprint); attack % cannot exceed Athletics.
- **Weapon Precision:** Use STR+DEX for Damage Modifier with small weapons if better than STR+SIZ; modifier cannot exceed weapon damage die. Covers clubs, daggers, shortswords, rapiers, main gauche, garrotes, knives, unarmed, darts, slings, short bows, javelins.
- **Additional Weapon Specialization** (Combat Skill 50%+): Take another specialization.

**Rank 2 Auto-progression:**
- **Weapon Master** (Combat Skill 70%+, prior Specialization): One weapon only. Critical chance doubled; melee weapon +1 Damage Modifier grade; ranged weapon +1 Damage Modifier grade vs. Close Range targets.

**Rank 2 Ranked Abilities (2 XP each):**
- **Brute Strength:** Once/day, Brawn 1 grade easier for one Action; lose 1 Fatigue after.
- **Sweeping Strike** (Forceful Strike + Combat Skill 70%+): Single melee attack roll vs. two targets in reach and line of sight; effects resolved separately; cannot sweep past a friendly unit; once per Round.
- **Additional Weapon Specialization** (Combat Skill 70%+).

**Rank 3 Auto-progression:**
- **High Master** (Weapon Mastery + Combat Skill 90%+):
  - *Melee:* Free Action riposte after a successful Parry at 1 grade harder; weapon treated as 1 size larger for Parrying.
  - *Ranged:* Shift Hit Location result to adjacent location if target within Close Range (as Sharpshooter).

**Rank 3 Ranked Abilities (3 XP each):**
- **Defensive Reflexes** (Unburdened, max light armor): Once per combat, re-roll a failed Parry or Evade without spending a Luck Point.
- **Greater Sweeping Strike** (Sweeping Strike + Combat Skill 90%+): As Sweeping Strike but vs. all enemies in reach and line of sight; once per Round.
- **Additional Weapon Specialization** (Combat Skill 90%+).

**Rank 4 Auto-progression:**
- **Grand Master** (High Mastery + Combat Skill 110%+):
  - *Melee:* +1 additional Damage Modifier grade (total +2 grades from Specialization).
  - *Ranged:* Reload time reduced by 1 more; weapon with 0 load can be readied as Free Action.

**Rank 4 Ranked Abilities (4 XP each):**
- **Greater Brute Strength:** Brute Strength usable twice/day; first use no Fatigue cost, second costs 1 Fatigue.
- **Additional Weapon Specialization** (Combat Skill 110%+).

**Rank 5 Auto-progression:**
- **Legendary Master** (Grand Mastery + Combat Skill 130%+):
  - *Melee:* Weapon treated as 2 Size grades larger for Parrying (Large -> Enormous, etc.).
