Each costs 1 Action Point. May be taken at any time during the Round in response to an imminent threat.

| Action | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Counter Spell** | Dismiss or counter an incoming spell. Must have Casting Time of 1 Turn (or be prepared with Hold Magic). Negates the entire spell on success, including multi-target/AoE. |
| **Evade** | Opposed Evade vs. attacker; full avoidance if won, leaves you prone. Within 10 ft of AoE edge: full avoidance on success instead of half damage. |
| **Interrupt** | Delaying characters only. Halts opponent's Turn to act; opponent resumes after if still possible. Also enables attack-of-opportunity vs. characters passing within reach. |
| **Parry** | Defend using Combat Skill (weapon, shield, or body). Reduces or blocks damage based on weapon Size. |
