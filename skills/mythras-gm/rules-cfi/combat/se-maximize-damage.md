**Maximize Damage** (offensive; requires Attacker Critical; stackable).

Replace one weapon damage die with its maximum value.

- 1d6 damage -> treat as 6.
- 2d6 damage -> treat one die as maximum: 1d6+6.

Stack to maximize additional dice. Does **not** affect the Damage Modifier (rolled normally).

Usable with natural weapons as well.
