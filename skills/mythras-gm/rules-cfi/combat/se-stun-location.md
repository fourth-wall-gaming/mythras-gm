**Stun Location** (offensive; bludgeoning weapons only).

The attacker uses a bludgeoning weapon to temporarily stun the body part struck.

**Trigger:** The blow must overcome Armor Points and injure the target.

**Resistance:** Defender makes an Opposed Roll of Endurance against the original attack roll.
- **Defender Fails:** The Hit Location is Incapacitated for a number of Turns equal to the damage inflicted.

**Location effects:**
- **Torso:** Defender staggers winded; can only defend (no offensive actions).
- **Head:** Defender is briefly insensible and unable to act.
