**Bypass Armor** (offensive; requires Attacker Critical; stackable).

The attacker finds a gap in the defender's armor. If the defender has both natural armor and worn armor, the attacker must specify which is bypassed. **Stack twice** to bypass both.

Magic physical protection is treated as worn armor for this purpose.
