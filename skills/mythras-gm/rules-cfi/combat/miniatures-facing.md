**Scale:** 1 inch = 5 feet or 10 feet (choose one, use consistently).

**Facing:** At end of movement a figure faces one side of its square (or optionally a diagonal).

- **Attacking:** Into any front or side square.
- **Evading an attack from front or side:** Standard difficulty.
- **Evading an attack from back square:** Formidable difficulty, and requires a successful Perception roll just to know the attack is coming.

**Terrain:**
- **Obstacles** (walls, pits, closed doors): completely block movement; must circumvent.
- **Difficult terrain** (rubble, shallow water, low fences, tall grass): costs 2x movement to enter (5-ft square = 10 ft of movement). Minimum movement 5 ft. Passing through at faster than Base Move requires Formidable Athletics or Standard Acrobatics or fall prone.
