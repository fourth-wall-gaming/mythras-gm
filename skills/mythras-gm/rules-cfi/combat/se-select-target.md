**Select Target** (defensive; requires Attacker Fumble).

The defender maneuvers or deflects the blow to hit an adjacent bystander instead. Requirements:
- New target must be within reach of the attacker's melee weapon, or along the line of fire for ranged.
- The new victim is taken completely by surprise -- the attack **automatically hits** with no chance to Evade or Parry.
- The accidental victim suffers **no Special Effect** from the attack.
