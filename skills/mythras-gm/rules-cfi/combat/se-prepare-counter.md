**Prepare Counter** (defensive; stackable).

The defender reads the opponent's patterns and prepares a counter against one specific Special Effect (noted in secret).

If the opponent attempts to use that Special Effect during the fight, the defender **instantly substitutes** the attacker's effect with one of their own, which **succeeds automatically**.

Stack to prepare counters against additional Special Effects.
