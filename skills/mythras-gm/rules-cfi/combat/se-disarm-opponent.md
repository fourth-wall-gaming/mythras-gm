**Disarm Opponent** (offensive or defensive).

Opponent makes an Opposed Roll of their Combat Style vs. the disarmer's original roll.

- **On failure:** Weapon flung Damage Modifier x3 feet away. If no Damage Modifier, weapon drops at the disarmed character's feet.
- Works only on creatures up to **twice the attacker's STR**.

**Weapon size modifiers:** Each step the disarmer's weapon is larger = one grade harder for opponent. Each step smaller = one grade easier.
