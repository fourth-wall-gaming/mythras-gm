**Surprise** occurs when an unexpected attack is launched against opponents unaware of the attacker's presence or intention (ambush, treacherous attack on an unsuspecting ally).

**Effects on the surprised target:**
- Suffers a -10 penalty to Initiative.
- Until their Initiative arrives, they are considered flat-footed and **cannot defend** themselves.
- The **first attack**, if successful, gains **one bonus Special Effect**.
- For the **remainder of the Round**, they may not perform any offensive Action.
