**Entangle** (offensive or defensive; requires entangling weapon such as whip or net).

Immobilizes the location struck:
- **Arm:** Cannot use what it holds.
- **Leg:** Target cannot move.
- **Head/chest/abdomen:** All skill rolls one grade harder.

**Follow-up:** On the wielder's next Turn, may spend 1 Action Point for an automatic Trip Opponent attempt.

**Breaking free:** On the victim's Turn, Opposed Brawn roll to yank free, OR win a Special Effect and choose Damage Weapon, Disarm Opponent, or Slip Free.
