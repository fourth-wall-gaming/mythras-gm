**Charge Action:** Move at Run (3x) or Sprint (5x) into melee. No minimum distance -- momentum matters, not distance.

**Attack modifier:** Hard difficulty on the attack roll.

**Bonuses:**
- Attacker's weapon Size increases by 1 step.
- Bipedal attacker's Damage Modifier increases by 1 step.
- Quadruped (or more legs): Damage Modifier increases by 2 steps.
- Mounted: may substitute mount's Damage Modifier for own.

**Counter-charge:** Instead of Parrying or Evading, recipient can simultaneously counterattack. The wielder of the **longer weapon** strikes first. If the receiving weapon can be set (e.g., a spear), the receiver may use the **charger's Damage Modifier** instead of their own.

**After the charge:** Character may stop in engagement or sweep past (breaking engagement immediately).
