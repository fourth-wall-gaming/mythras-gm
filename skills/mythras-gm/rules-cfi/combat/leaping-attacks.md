**Leaping attacks** can be launched from ambush, at the end of a charge, or by creatures capable of leaping without a run-up.

**Resolution:** Opposed Roll of leaper's **Athletics** vs. defender's **Brawn or Evade**.
- Quadruped defenders: difficulty one grade harder.
- **Leaper wins:** Defender automatically knocked **prone**, attacker astride them. No damage from the leap.
- **Defender wins:** Defender weathers or sidesteps impact.
- Level of success difference grants Special Effects as normal.

**Prone victim** cannot regain footing until attacker leaves or they win a Special Effect permitting Arise.

**Limit:** Only works on opponents up to twice the attacker's SIZ. Defender who uses **Brace** proactively is harder to knock down (treated as up to 50% larger SIZ).
