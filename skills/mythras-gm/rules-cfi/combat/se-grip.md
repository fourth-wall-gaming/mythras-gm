**Grip** (offensive; Unarmed only).

Requires an empty hand (or limb capable of gripping) within Unarmed reach. The attacker holds the opponent, preventing them from disengaging.

**Breaking free:** On the victim's Turn, Opposed Roll of either Brawn or Unarmed vs. whichever of the two skills the gripper prefers.
