**Bash** (offensive; shields or bludgeoning weapons only).

The attacker deliberately bashes the opponent off balance.

**Knockback distance** (based on damage rolled, before armor/parry reductions):
- Shields: 5 feet per 3 points of damage rolled.
- Bludgeoning weapons: 5 feet per 5 points of damage rolled.

**SIZ limit:** Bash works only on creatures up to twice the attacker's SIZ.

**Obstacle collision:** If the recipient is forced backward into an obstacle, they must make an Acrobatics or Athletics roll at Hard difficulty or fall/trip over.
