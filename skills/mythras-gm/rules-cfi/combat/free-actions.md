Free Actions cost no Action Points and may be performed at any time during the Round.

| Action | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Assess Situation** | Perception roll (unengaged). Success reveals tactical changes. Hard while Running; Formidable while Sprinting. |
| **Drop Item** | Drop held object instantly. |
| **Signal** | Gesture or signal to allies (if unengaged and they can perceive it). |
| **Speak** | Short phrases only (fitting within 5 seconds). |
| **Use Luck Point** | Re-roll a result or invoke another Luck Point benefit. |
| **Ward Location** | Dedicate a weapon to passively block one Hit Location. Damage to that location is auto-reduced as per weapon Size. Shield can cover multiple locations; crouching behind a shield doubles locations covered. Block ends when the dedicated weapon attacks or actively Parries. |
