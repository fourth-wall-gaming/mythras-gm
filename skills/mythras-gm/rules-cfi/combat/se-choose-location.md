**Choose Location** (offensive).

**Melee:** Attacker may freely select any Hit Location normally within reach.

**Ranged:** Requires an Attacker Critical, UNLESS the target is within Close Range AND is either stationary or unaware of the attack -- in which case a standard success suffices.
