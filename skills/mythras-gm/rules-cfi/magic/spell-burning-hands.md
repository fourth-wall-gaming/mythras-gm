**Burning Hands** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 120-degree Cone, 5 ft. long
- **Resist:** Evade

Flames shoot from caster's hands in a 120-degree arc in front, affecting all targets in that cone. Damage applies to all Hit Locations. Worn armor does not protect; natural armor and magical AP reduce damage normally. A successful Evade (going prone) means no damage. Artful Dodger Ability allows Evade without going prone.

Large creatures may have fewer locations affected: SIZ 21-30 take damage to head, chest, abdomen, and arms; SIZ 31+ take damage to abdomen and legs (adjust per shape and position). Chance of igniting flammable materials = damage rolled x5%.
