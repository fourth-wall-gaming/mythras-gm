**Darkness** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 10 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 10 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates total magical darkness that nullifies normal vision and special racial sight (Infravision). All mundane light sources (sunlight to candlelight) are nullified. Countered by Light (and vice versa), but a Light spell already in effect when Darkness is cast has no effect on it. Other magical lights (Dancing Lights, Fairy Fire, detection glows) are also nullified.
