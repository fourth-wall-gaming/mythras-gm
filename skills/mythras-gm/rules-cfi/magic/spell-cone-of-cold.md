**Cone of Cold** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 90-degree cone, 10 ft./Intensity long
- **Resist:** Evade

Cone of blistering cold from caster's hand. Damage applied to each Hit Location (rolled once). Worn armor offers only half protection; natural armor and magical AP reduce damage normally. Evade (going prone) = no damage; Artful Dodger Evade = damage halved (unless within 5 ft. of edge). Chance of coating victim in ice = rolled damage x5%; if iced, victim may spend 1 AP per Round for a Brawn roll to break free.
