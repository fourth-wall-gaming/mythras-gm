**Spiritual Hammer** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 3 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 30 ft. Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** Parry or Evade

A hammer-like force of spiritual energy appears and attacks a target designated by the caster. Each Turn the caster may spend one Action Point to direct the hammer while maintaining Concentration. During Concentration, the caster may move no faster than a walk, and may Evade or Parry without breaking Concentration.

The hammer can be moved anywhere within the caster's field of vision. If attacking from behind, the target cannot defend to their full ability. The hammer deals 1d8+1 damage using the cleric's Combat Skill. Worn armor provides only half its Armor Points; magical Armor Points and natural armor work normally. It may be parried or evaded but cannot be harmed by direct attacks.

The hammer counts as a magical weapon for striking creatures immune to non-magical weapons. Each Intensity beyond the first grants a cumulative +1 Magic Bonus (max +5), which helps penetrate creature immunities but does not add to damage.
