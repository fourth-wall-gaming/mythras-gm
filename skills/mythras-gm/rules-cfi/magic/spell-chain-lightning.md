**Chain Lightning** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 50 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** Evade

Releases a 5-ft. wide bolt of electricity from the caster's fingertips. Strikes one visible target in range for damage, then arcs to nearest object or creature (which takes the damage -1), then arcs again (damage -2), and so on. Can arc back to allies or caster. Subsequent arc targets need not be visible. Each arc has its own 50-ft. range. Continues until damage reaches 0 or no more valid targets exist (stops if grounded by metal cage, pool of water, etc.).

Each creature takes damage to a single Hit Location. Worn armor offers no protection; natural armor and magical AP reduce damage normally. Evade (going prone) = no damage; Artful Dodger Evade without prone = damage halved. Ignite flammable materials = damage rolled x1%.
