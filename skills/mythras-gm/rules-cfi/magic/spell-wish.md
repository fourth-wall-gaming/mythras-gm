**Wish** (Conjuration, Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** See Below
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3 MP + 1 or more EXP Rolls
- **Range:** Unlimited
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** See Below

Can permanently and significantly alter reality. Casting costs 3 Magic Points and 1 or more EXP rolls depending on the desired result. Each successive casting within a 1-month span doubles the EXP required. Casting Time is the time needed to verbally specify the Wish. Upon completion, the caster loses Fatigue equal to the EXP expended and ages 5% of their normal lifespan (5 years for a human).

Results are taken literally and are open to GM interpretation. Greedy desires have a 10% chance of backfiring. A Wish cannot override decrees of deity-like entities.
