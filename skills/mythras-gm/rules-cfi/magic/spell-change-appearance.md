**Change Appearance** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 2d6 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Alters the caster's appearance to any humanoid or person-shaped bipedal form. Height and weight change is limited to +/-5 SIZ. Can become fat or thin, tall or short, alter gender. Hair, clothing, and equipment may be changed. Facial features may be changed to perfectly imitate a specific person. Subject to disbelief as per other Illusion spells. Does not alter the caster's scent; if touched, still feels the same as original form and clothing.
