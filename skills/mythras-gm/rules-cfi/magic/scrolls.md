Scrolls allow casters to access additional spells beyond those they can memorize. Types:

**Cursed Scrolls:** Arcane or Divine. Activated by reading the name or title (or casting Read Magic on an Arcane cursed scroll). Curse takes effect immediately.

**Protection Scrolls:** Readable and usable by **anyone** regardless of Class. Requires a single **Cast Magic Action** to activate. Contents depend on creator:
- Cleric Protection Scrolls: Demons, Devils, Possession, Undead.
- Magic-User Protection Scrolls: Demons, Elementals, Lycanthropes, Magic, Petrification.

**Spell Scrolls:** One spell per scroll (any Rank). Same casting time as normal. **No MP expenditure** required (cost paid by creator). Each use destroys the spell from the scroll.

**Access by Class:**
- **Mage scrolls** (other than own creations): Require **Read Magic** before use. Exception: Thieves with the Ability to Use Arcane Scrolls may use without Read Magic.
- **Cleric scrolls:** Do not need Read Magic. Anyone may read the contents; but only a cleric can cast them.
- **Protection scrolls:** Usable by anyone.

**Casting from a scroll:** A caster may automatically cast any scroll spell of appropriate Class and sufficient Rank with no skill roll. If attempting a spell of **higher Rank**, a skill roll is required (see Scroll Casting Difficulty). Fumble: spell miscast, typically affects reader or allies, or produces opposite effect.
