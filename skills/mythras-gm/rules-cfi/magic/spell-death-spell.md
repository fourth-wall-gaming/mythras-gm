**Death Spell** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Cost:** 3 +1/Intensity, +2 EXP
- **Range:** 30 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 15 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Endurance

Kills a variable number of creatures SIZ 40 or less in the Area of Effect. Roll 1d6 per Intensity to determine creatures affected. SIZ 1-20 always affected before larger ones. Large (SIZ 21-40): Easy Resistance roll, but each counts as 5 human-sized creatures. SIZ 41+: unaffected. No effect on lycanthropes, undead, or other planar creatures. Only Wish can restore those killed.
