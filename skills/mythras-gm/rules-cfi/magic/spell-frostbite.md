**Frostbite** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Endurance

Works on living, organic tissue. Inflicts numbness and pain in one extremity (fingers, toes, buttocks, nose and ears, etc.). Caster must touch a specific extremity or roll randomly if cast at range. If not resisted: sensory numbness and lingering pain for Duration; all skill tests using that location become one Difficulty Grade harder. No direct damage. Large (SIZ 21-40): Easy resistance; Huge (SIZ 41+): Very Easy.
