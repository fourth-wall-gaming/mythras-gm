**Wall of Fire / Circle of Fire** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3 / Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** See Below
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** See Below

Conjures a wall or circle of blazing purple or reddish-blue flame. Duration lasts while Concentration is maintained, or 1 Round per Intensity once Concentration ceases.

**Wall of Fire:** Stationary. 20 ft. long per Intensity; 10-20 ft. high (chosen at casting). Range 180 ft. Cannot be conjured horizontally.

**Circle of Fire:** Moves with the caster. 10 ft. radius + 5 ft. per Intensity beyond the first; 10-20 ft. high (chosen at casting). Range 0; must encircle the caster.

**Heat damage** from the side facing away from the caster:
- Within 10 ft.: 1d6 per Round
- 10-20 ft.: 1d4 per Round

Damage applied at end of each Round; if in both zones in one Round, greater damage applies.

**Passing through the flames:** 2d6 damage + 1 per Intensity beyond the first, applied to each Hit Location. Worn armor provides no protection; magical Armor Points and natural armor reduce damage normally. A successful Evade roll negates damage on the Round the spell springs into being. Fire-using creatures take half damage; cold-using creatures take double. Flames have a chance to ignite flammable materials equal to the damage rolled x5%.
