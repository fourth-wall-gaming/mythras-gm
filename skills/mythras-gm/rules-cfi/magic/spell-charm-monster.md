**Charm Monster** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 1 Week/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Willpower

Enthralls all living creatures SIZ 20 or less in the Area of Effect, OR 1 creature of any SIZ. Large creatures (SIZ 21-40): Resistance roll is Easy. Huge creatures (SIZ 41+): Resistance roll is Very Easy. Otherwise functions as Charm Person.
