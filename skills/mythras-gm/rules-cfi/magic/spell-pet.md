**Pet** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Animal
- **Resist:** Willpower

Allows caster to take mental control of a small creature. Both the creature's SIZ and INS may not exceed half the caster's CHA. Loyal pets of the caster need not resist the spell. While concentrating, caster may access the animal's senses (sight, smell, hearing, etc.). The animal can be sent to scout, fetch, or perform complex tasks.
