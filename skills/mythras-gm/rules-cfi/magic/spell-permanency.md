**Permanency** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +2 EXP
- **Range:** See Below
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Cast immediately after another spell to make that spell permanent. The first spell is "held back" after casting; then Permanency is cast as an additional Action Point, after which the selected spell becomes permanent. Costs 2 EXP plus the normal cost of the original spell. Dispel Magic cast during Permanency dispels both spells.

Personal spells (affect caster only) that can be made permanent: Avert, Coordination, Darkvision, Deflect, Detect Evil, Detect Invisibility, Detect Magic, Glamour, Know Languages, Might, Preserve, Protection (Self), Protection from Evil, Read Magic, Tongues.

Spells that may be made permanent on others/objects/areas (* = objects/areas only): Chill, Cleanse, Dancing Lights*, Fear, Invisibility, Polish*, Preserve, Tidy*, Magic Mouth*, Wall of Fire*, Web*.
