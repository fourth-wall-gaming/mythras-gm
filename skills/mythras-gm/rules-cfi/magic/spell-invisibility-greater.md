**Invisibility, Greater** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 3 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Functions as Invisibility but no action by the subject (including melee/ranged attacks, spell casting) ends the Duration early. Note there are visible clues when actions occur (visual distortion). Anyone with line of sight may make a Formidable Perception roll after the action (or possibly before if delayed) to discern the invisible subject's location. Attacks at one grade of difficulty may be made in the same Round by those who perceive the location.
