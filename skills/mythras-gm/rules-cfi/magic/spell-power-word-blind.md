**Power Word - Blind** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 20 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 15 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Endurance

One target of caster's choice within line of sight and range is struck blind; all others in 15 ft. radius who fail Endurance roll are also blinded. Curable by Cure Blindness or Dispel Magic.

| Target Rank | Target SIZ | Duration |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 0-1 | 1-20 | 1d4+1 x10 minutes (permanent if 1-3 creatures in area) |
| 2 | 21-40 | 1d4+1 minutes |
| 3 | 41-60 | 1d4+1 Rounds |
| 4 | 61-80 | 1d3 Rounds |
| 5 | 81+ | 1 Round |
