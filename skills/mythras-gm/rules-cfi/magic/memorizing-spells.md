Knowing a spell is not sufficient -- it must be **committed to memory** before it can be cast.

**Prerequisites:**
- The caster must first sleep for at least 8 hours (need not be continuous; total must reach 8 hours).
- A calm, quiet area for concentration.

**Process:**
- 15 minutes of calm study and concentration (Arcane) or prayer and devotion (Divine) per spell, regardless of Rank.
- **Arcane:** The caster must have access to their own spell book. A spell from another's spell book requires a successful **Read Magic** casting first.
- **Divine:** The caster prays for the deity to grant the spell. Any spell of the caster's current Rank or lower may be memorized (e.g., a Rank 3 cleric may memorize any spell of Rank 1, 2, or 3).

**Limits:** The Rank Tables for each Class specify the maximum number of spells of the current Rank that may be held in memory simultaneously. To memorize a new spell when at the limit, the caster must first forget one -- this also takes 15 minutes.

**Persistence:** Casting a spell does not expunge it from memory; it may be recast as long as MP remain. Even unconsciousness does not erase memorized spells. Death and resurrection typically causes all memorized spells to be forgotten (except by powerful life-giving magic).
