**Know Languages (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** 5 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Sapient Being
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows understanding and reading of almost any non-magical language. Does not include the speech of non-sapient animals, only the true languages of sapient beings. Caster must touch the object or creature to comprehend. Grants no ability to speak or write the language.

**Reverse - Confuse Languages:** Makes one creature unintelligible to anyone, or makes written text indecipherable. May also counter Know Languages as it is being cast.
