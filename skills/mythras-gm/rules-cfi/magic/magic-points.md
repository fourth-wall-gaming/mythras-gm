**Magic Points (MP)** are derived from the POW Characteristic and represent stored magical energy. When MP reach 0, no spells or magical abilities dependent on MP can be used.

**Recovery:**

- **Full night's sleep:** Restores all MP (uninterrupted, safe rest). Camping in an occupied dungeon or similarly dangerous/distracting environment restores only half.
- **Short Rest -- study or prayer:** 1 MP per 15 minutes of studying the spell book (Arcane) or praying (Divine), during a Short Rest.
- **Potion of Magica:** Grants MP as detailed in the Common Magic Items table.

MP do not recover while the character is active in any other way.

**Ongoing spells lock MP:** MP spent on active ongoing spells are unavailable for recovery until those spells are dismissed or expire (see Enduring Magic Costs). Permanent-duration spells do not lock MP.
