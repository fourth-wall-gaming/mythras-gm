**Glue** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 24 Hours
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 2 Items or 1 Broken Item
- **Resist:** N/A

Cements two solid inanimate objects together (e.g., cartwheel to axle, door to frame) for 24 hours. Cannot be parted unless something actively wrenches them apart. Spell has a Brawn skill equal to caster's POW x5; fails when a superior Brawn defeats it in an Opposed Roll. When spell concludes or fails, items part unharmed.
