**Free Action** (Abjuration, Enchantment)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 10 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows subject to act normally regardless of spells or effects that hinder movement or penalize attacks. Negates terrain penalties (mud, snow). Underwater: subject moves at normal land speed; swung weapons (swords, axes, etc.) attack with full effect; does not grant water breathing. Negates movement and action penalties from: Entangle, Slow, Hold, Paralysis, Web, and similar.
