**Cure Fatigue (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance or Evade for reverse)

Restores 1d3+1 lost levels of Fatigue instantly, even restoring consciousness.

**Reverse - Cause Fatigue:** Imparts 1d3+1 levels of Fatigue on targets failing an Endurance roll. Caster's Channel roll serves as the hit roll (no separate attack roll needed). Neither worn nor natural armor protects; magical Armor negates the spell.
