**Divination** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 10 Minutes
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows clerics to receive specific information from their deity about some anticipated activity within one week. Unlike Augury, provides specific information. Channel skill roll starts at Formidable; each Intensity decreases difficulty one grade. Maximum Intensity 5; roll of 96%+ always fails. GM should make this roll secretly (hostile magic could provide false information).

| Intensity | Cost | Channel Difficulty |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | Formidable |
| 2 | 6 | Hard |
| 3 | 9 | Standard |
| 4 | 12 | Easy |
| 5 | 15 | Very Easy |
