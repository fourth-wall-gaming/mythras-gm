**Protection from Cold** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 15 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Complete protection from natural cold. When cast on self: also protects from magical cold (white dragon breath, Cone of Cold, etc.), absorbing 12 points per Intensity. Once the field is depleted, any excess from that attack is halved before being applied. When cast on others: complete protection from natural cold; magical cold attacks are halved.

| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |

| 2 | 4 | 30 min. | 24 points |

| 4 | 6 | 60 min. | 48 points |
| +1 | +1 | +15 min. | +12 points |
