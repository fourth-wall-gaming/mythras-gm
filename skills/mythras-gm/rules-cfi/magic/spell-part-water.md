**Part Water** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 4
- **Casting Time:** 10 Minutes
- **Duration:** 10 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 60 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Parts water or other liquid creating a trough: 100 ft. wide, 10 ft. deep per Intensity, 120 ft. long per Intensity. Can be cast underwater to create an air corridor with the same dimensions. May be ended early by caster or until Duration expires. Used offensively on a water elemental: deals 10 damage per Intensity; surviving elemental must Willpower resist or flee caster for 3d4 minutes.
