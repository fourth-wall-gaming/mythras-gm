**Command** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Minute
- **Cost:** 3
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

On the subject's next Turn (if they fail to resist), the single-word command is followed literally, using one Action Point for the action. The command cannot directly injure the target, though secondary effects may harm them (e.g., "Fall" to someone on a cliff face). Target must understand the language spoken.

Example commands: Crawl, Die (collapse comatose 1 Round), Fall (prone), Fumble, Sleep, Flee, Fly (flap arms and run in circles), Go (walk facing direction), Halt, Leave, Rest (sit down), Run, Scream, Sink (stop swimming), Surrender (drop weapons).
