The Magic Points spent on all currently **active, ongoing** magic are **locked** -- they cannot begin recovering until the relevant spell is dismissed or expires.

- Example: A caster who has charmed several retinues with Charm Person (Duration: weeks) will have those MP unavailable for recovery for the entire spell duration.
- **Permanent-duration spells are an exception:** They do not affect the caster's MP total at all (the cost was paid at casting and is not ongoing).

This rule means that maintaining many long-duration spells can severely deplete a caster's effective MP pool even after rest.
