**Armor** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3 +1/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Grants an invisible field of magical force equal to scale armor: 4 Armor Points to each Hit Location. Has no effect on someone already wearing armor; supersedes natural armor if it offers superior protection. If target has more than 4 points of natural armor, increases AP by 1 in all locations instead. Does not hinder movement or spell casting. Persists until dispelled or until caster takes damage exceeding the threshold below in a single attack. Caster may drop spell voluntarily (must do so to sleep).

| Intensity | Cost | Sustained Until Damage From 1 Attack |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | 8 damage |
| 2 | 4 | 9 damage |
| 3 | 5 | 10 damage |
| 4 | 6 | 11 damage |
| +1 | +1 | +1 damage threshold |
