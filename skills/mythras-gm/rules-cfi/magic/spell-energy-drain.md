**Energy Drain** (Evocation or Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Endurance

On a failed Resistance roll, imposes two levels of permanent Fatigue loss. Cumulative with normal Fatigue loss. Cure Fatigue spells/potions do not affect Energy Drain Fatigue. Only Restoration spells/scrolls can permanently cure the lost Fatigue. Armor has no effect.
