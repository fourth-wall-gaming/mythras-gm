**Fumble** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 1 Round/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 10 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Willpower

All opponents in the Area of Effect become clumsy: all physical skill rolls are one grade harder; all failures result in a Fumble. Even simple physical actions (picking up dropped items, climbing stairs) require an Athletics roll with failure = Fumble. A successful Resistance roll avoids effects for the current Round; if the victim remains in the area next Round, must resist again.

May alternatively target a specific creature: on a failed roll, suffers effects for the entire Duration with no area effect. On success against a targeted creature, victim suffers the effect of Slow instead.
