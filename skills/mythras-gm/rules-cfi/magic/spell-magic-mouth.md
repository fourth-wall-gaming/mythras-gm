**Magic Mouth** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** See Below (permanent until triggered, then ends)
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Area:** 1 Object
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates a mouth on any inanimate object (pillar, statue, chest, wall). Appears and speaks when triggered by a specified event. Phrase: max 25 words, spoken at roughly 1 word/second. Cannot speak words of power or cast spells. Trigger: anything discernible non-magically from spoken word to specific person type entering a room; trigger range is 25 ft. per Intensity. Cannot target creatures with Intelligence or Instinct. Duration is permanent until triggered, then ends.
