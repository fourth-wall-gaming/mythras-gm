**Dancing Lights** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 100 ft. Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates one of: 1d4 flickering lights resembling lanterns/torches (similar illumination), 1d4 glowing spheres like will-o-wisps, or 1 vaguely humanoid flickering flame shape. Directed by caster without Concentration at any desired speed within the Area of Effect. If lights move out of Area of Effect or Duration expires, they vanish.
