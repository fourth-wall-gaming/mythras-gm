All spells have three component types:

- **Somatic:** Gestures and body movements required for casting.
- **Verbal:** Words, invocations, and vocal sounds.
- **Material:** Physical substances consumed during casting.

**Generic components** (no specific component named in the spell description): Assume the caster has enough on their person to cover an average adventure (roughly one month of adventuring). Cost is negligible and need not be tracked.

**Specific components** (named in the spell description): Must be purchased and tracked individually. If the caster is separated from specific material components, they **cannot cast any spells** until the situation is resolved.
