**Find Familiar** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 2d12 Hours
- **Duration:** Special
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity, +1 EXP
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 1/2 Mile Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** N/A

Requires a brazier with charcoal and 100GP of incense. Even if successfully cast, only 50% chance of a suitable familiar answering. Each additional Intensity extends range another half mile and increases chance by 5% (max 95%). May only be attempted once per year. Caster must sacrifice 1 Experience Roll to create the spiritual link.

While familiar and master are within 120 ft., caster may use the familiar's Magic Points. Caster gains telepathic link and special Abilities as per the familiar type (d100 roll). In case of familiar's death, caster loses 5 Experience Rolls (future EXP must pay the balance if none available).

Common familiar examples: Bat (+10% hearing Perception, fight blind), Cat (Night Vision, +10% hearing/stealth), Crow (verbal communication, +10% Perception), Hawk (+20% Perception, 2x vision range), Owl (Night Vision, +10% night Perception, +10% Willpower), Raven (verbal communication, +10% Commerce/Perception).
