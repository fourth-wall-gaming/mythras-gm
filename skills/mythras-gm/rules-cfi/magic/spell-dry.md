**Dry** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Removes all extraneous moisture from an object or person; moisture slicks off as a puddle or evaporates in vapor. Used to dry off after rain, protect equipment from rot, and various other purposes. Caster can affect an object up to POW x2 in SIZ.
