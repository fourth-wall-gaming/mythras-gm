**Reincarnation, Arcane** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 10 Minutes
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity, +1 EXP
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** See Below (Willpower for non-evil souls)

Cast on corpse dead less than 24 hours per Intensity. No effect on full-blooded elves. Non-evil souls may Willpower resist (Opposed Roll; GM may waive for heroic reasons). Evil souls: must overcome the tormentor's Willpower. On success: a fully formed adult body appears in 1d6x10 minutes. Form is determined randomly. Former Class and Rank retained. All Characteristics randomly determined for new form EXCEPT INT, POW, and CHA which remain unchanged. Former racial Abilities lost; new racial Abilities gained. Character remembers most of former life (Willpower roll first week for significant facts). Only Wish can restore the original form later.

New form table: 01-20 Human; 21-60 Demi-Human (Dwarf, Elf, Gnome, Half-Elf, Halfling); 61-95 Humanoid/Monster (Bugbear, Gnoll, Goblin, Hobgoblin, Kobold, Orc, Ogre, Troll); 96-00 GM's Option.
