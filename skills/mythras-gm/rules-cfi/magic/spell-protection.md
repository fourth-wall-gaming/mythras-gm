**Protection** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Trigger
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

The first time the character would normally take damage that penetrates protective clothing or armor, Protection triggers and reduces damage taken by 1d3 points, then dissipates. Stacks with all other forms of protection magic. Only protects against physical damage; not fire, choking, etc. The Mage version may only be cast on oneself.
