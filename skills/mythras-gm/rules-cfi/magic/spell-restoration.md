**Restoration** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 3 Minutes
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

A victim previously drained of life force (by Energy Drain or similar) regains one previously lost Fatigue level. At Intensity 1, works if the drain occurred within the last 24 hours; each additional Intensity extends that window by 24 hours. Multiple drained levels require multiple castings.

Regardless of the Fatigue restored, also restores all mental capacities lost to magic, insanity, or dementia.

To reverse magical aging: in addition to normal cost, spend 1 EXP per 1% of the recipient's typical lifespan to be restored (1 EXP = 1 year for a human). Cannot reduce the subject's current age below their actual age.
