**Holy Word (R)** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3, +1 EXP
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 30 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Caster must be on their own plane. Extra-planar evil entities are sent back to their plane and cannot return for at least 24 hours. Non-planar Evil creatures suffer additional effects based on Rank or SIZ (whichever is more beneficial to target):

| Target Rank | Target SIZ | Effect | Skills Penalty | Movement |
| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-: | :-: |
| 0-1 | 1-20 | Kills | - | - |
| 2 | 21-40 | Paralyzes 1d4x10 Minutes | - | - |
| 3 | 41-60 | Slows 2d4 Rounds (as Slow spell) | Hard | 1/2 |
| 4-5 | 61+ | Deafens 1d4 Rounds | Formidable | 1/4 |

Creatures previously deafened or unable to hear are unaffected by the table effects; extra-planar entities are still banished.

**Reverse - Unholy Word:** Functions identically but only against creatures of Good Alignment.
