**Detect Magic** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1, Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 90-degree Arc, 60 ft.
- **Resist:** N/A

Reveals a glowing blue aura surrounding any magic item or spell effect within a 90-degree arc to 60 ft. Detects approximate Intensity/Magnitude (d100: 01-25 = -1d3; 26-75 = exact; 76-00 = +1d3). 10% chance per Intensity of discovering specific magic type (necromancy, elemental, etc.). Aura projects through 3 ft. wood, 1 ft. stone, 1 inch solid metal. No more than one 60-degree arc per minute.
