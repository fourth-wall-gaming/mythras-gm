**Coordination** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Trigger
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Enhances manual dexterity and agility for a single predetermined task. When cast in preparation, permits the recipient to re-roll one skill check where coordination is required (Acrobatics, Lockpicking, etc.). May choose the better of two rolls. Spell is expended upon use.
