**Protection from Fire** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 15 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Complete protection from mundane heat (torches, campfires, burning buildings). When cast on self: also protects from fire-based magical damage (red dragon breath, Burning Hands, Heat Metal), absorbing 12 points per Intensity. Once field is depleted, excess from that attack is halved before being applied. When cast on others: complete protection from mundane fire; magical fire attacks are halved.
