**Protection from Evil (R)** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1, Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Three defensive layers:
1. Evil creatures suffer -10% penalty to Combat Style or Unarmed; recipient gets +10% bonus to Resistance rolls vs. evil creatures.
2. Summoned/conjured creatures and beings not native to the material plane (demons, devils, elementals, etc.) cannot make physical contact. Natural attacks cause recoil. Weapons held by such creatures may still strike.
3. Mental control attempts (Charm, Command) and possession (Magic Jar) automatically fail to penetrate.

Ends if protected individual makes any melee attack or tries to impose protective field against an affected creature.

**Reverse - Protection from Good:** Second and third benefits operate against good opponents; first benefit operates against good creatures.
