**Polymorph Self** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 20 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Caster assumes form of any other corporeal creature SIZ 1-40; each Intensity allows +/-10 SIZ change. Transformation takes 1 Round. Gains creature's breathing type (if different), natural attacks, Hit Points, Damage Modifier, physical skills; retains mental and social skills (INT, CHA based). Loses speech unless new form can speak. If new form can speak, can still cast spells. Cannot gain magical abilities of the new form.

On reverting voluntarily: Minor Wounds gained in new form disappear; Serious Wounds become Minor Wounds. Major Wounds carry over. No healing when transforming to animal form. If Dispel Magic forces reversion: no healing benefit. If slain: reverts to natural form.
