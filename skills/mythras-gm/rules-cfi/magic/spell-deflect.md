**Deflect** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Wards the recipient against tiny impacts of foreign material (raindrops, cloud of midges, flying grains of sand). Protection is limited to objects smaller than a child's fingernail; cannot prevent normal missile weapons from striking the character.
