**Preserve** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Turn
- **Duration:** 1d3 Months
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Sterilizes organic matter (animal and vegetable) preventing bacterial decay for 1d3 months. If material is later smoked, pickled, or salted it is preserved indefinitely. Can halt decay that has begun but cannot reverse it. Caster can affect organic matter equal in Things or SIZ to their POW.
