**Wizard Sight** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** 1 Minute/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates a small invisible eye (SIZ 1; no larger than 1 inch) that the magic-user can see through for the Duration. The eye is corporeal and cannot pass through solid matter.

**Vision:** Normal vision to 60 ft.; Infravision to 10 ft.

**Movement:**
- Scanning (obvious threats, floor and lower walls): 20 ft. per Round (240 ft. per minute)
- Thorough (full walls and ceiling): 10 ft. per Round (120 ft. per minute)

The caster must maintain Concentration; if broken, the eye remains inert until Concentration is re-established. Any gaze attacks that would normally affect the caster still do so through the eye. The eye is considered independent for blindness, darkness spells, and similar effects.
