**Appraise** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Turn
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Item
- **Resist:** None

Allows immediate assessment of quality of physical goods with combined ENC in Things or SIZ up to caster's POW. Determines whether identical-looking items are of the same quality, or if one is flawed or superior. Does not work on organic things, only inanimate objects. Does not reveal the nature of flaws or enhancements, only that they exist. Cannot identify or detect magic.
