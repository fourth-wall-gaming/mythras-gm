**Endure Heat/Cold** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 90 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Protects one creature from natural extremes of heat or cold (sub-zero temperatures, desert heat). Immune to mundane extremes including Heat Metal. Any magical heat or cold attack (red dragon breath, Cone of Cold) automatically dispels the protection regardless of element protected against. However, the protected individual ignores the first 10 points of damage in the Round the spell fails (if attack matches protection type). Area effect spell damage is reduced before being applied.
