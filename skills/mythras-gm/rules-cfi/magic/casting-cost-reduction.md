Higher-Rank casters learn more efficient magical channeling. For **each Rank above 1**, reduce the **final Casting Cost** (after all Intensity calculations) by **2 MP per Rank of difference** between the caster's Rank and the spell's Rank.

- This reduction applies to the **total final cost**, not per Intensity.
- Minimum final cost: **1 MP** (cannot be reduced below 1).

**Examples:**
- Rank 3 caster, Rank 2 spell: -2 MP reduction.
- Rank 3 caster, Rank 1 spell: -4 MP reduction.
- Rank 5 caster, Rank 1 spell: -8 MP reduction.
