**Sleep** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 5 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Area:** 10 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Willpower

Causes magical slumber in one living creature per Intensity in a 10 ft. radius circle. Closest creatures resist first, then next closest, etc. (randomly determine ties). Each creature rolls to resist until the spell's Intensity worth of creatures have been affected, or none remain in the area. Creatures in heightened alertness or actively in combat when the spell is cast find the resistance roll one grade easier.

Large creatures (SIZ 21-40): resistance roll is Easy. Huge creatures (SIZ 41+): resistance roll is Very Easy. Smaller creatures are always affected before larger ones.

While sleeping, creatures are helpless and normal noise will not wake them; they must be shaken/slapped awake or struck (which grants a Surprise attack). Awakening a creature takes 1 Combat Round.
