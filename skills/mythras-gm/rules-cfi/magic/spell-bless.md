**Bless (R)** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 5 Minutes
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 25 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Willpower

All allies within the Area of Effect radiate the Alignment of the caster (Good or Evil; no neutral blessings). Affected allies receive +5% to all skill and Resistance rolls. Weapons deal +1d3 damage to intelligent undead and outer-plane creatures with opposing alignment (demons, devils, angels, etc.). Animated undead (skeletons, zombies) are subject to this bonus even though not inherently evil.

**Reverse - Curse:** Enemies receive the inverse of all benefits above (-5% to skills, damage penalty instead of bonus). Damage penalty cannot reduce damage below 1.
