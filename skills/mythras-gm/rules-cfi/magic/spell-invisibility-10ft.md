**Invisibility, 10 ft. Radius** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 6 Hours/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target, 10 ft.
- **Resist:** N/A

Functions identically to Invisibility but extends to all within 10 ft. of the recipient when the spell is cast. Invisible subjects can see each other (but does not grant ability to see other invisible creatures). If any subject moves out of the 10 ft. area, their invisibility is lost. Moving back into the area later does not restore it. Attacks by anyone other than the original recipient disrupt only their own invisibility. If the original recipient attacks, all become visible. Those not present when cast do not benefit.
