**Mirror Image** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 3 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 5 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates 2d4 duplicates of the magic-user within 5 ft., exactly mimicking caster's movements. The entire area shimmers, making exact caster location impossible to determine. Hitting an image destroys it; hitting the caster does not remove images. Injuries to the caster are duplicated on remaining images. To determine if caster is struck: determine total potential targets (caster + images), roll nearest die size (e.g., 5 total = roll 1d6 re-rolling 6; caster is hit on 1). Alternatively use face-down markers. Images vanish at end of Duration.
