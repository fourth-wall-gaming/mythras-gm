**Enchant Weapon** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** 5 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Weapon
- **Resist:** N/A

Grants a normal weapon a temporary +1 Damage Modifier and allows it to strike creatures needing a +1 or better magic weapon. May enchant 2 small weapons (half a Thing each, like daggers/arrows) per casting, otherwise only 1 weapon. An existing magic weapon may have its bonus increased by +1 (total max +3). At +3, weapon Size increases by one category for damage purposes. Each +1 also gives the weapon +5 Armor Points and +2 Hit Points. May be combined with Enchant Item and Permanency for lasting effect.
