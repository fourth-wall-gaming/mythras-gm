**Classic Fantasy Imperative** provides two magical disciplines. Both use the same core rules for Magic Points, casting, memorization, and recovery.

- **Arcane:** Spells are researched, copied from spell books or scrolls, and recorded in the caster's own spell book. Arcane casters use the **Arcane Casting** skill.
- **Divine:** Spells are granted through prayer to a deity. Divine casters learn all spells of a new Rank automatically upon reaching that Rank. Divine casters use the **Divine Channeling** skill.
