**Speak with Monsters** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 4
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 1 or more Monsters
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows the caster to understand and be understood by any type of creature capable of any form of communication, including telepathic, pheromonal, and tactile forms. The caster speaks in their own language and the creature understands the intent in its own form of communication. The GM should determine the creature's reaction based on its Alignment relative to the caster's. The caster may switch between creatures of the same type throughout the Duration.
