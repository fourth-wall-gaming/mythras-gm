**Augury** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Minutes
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows the caster to determine whether a specific action to be taken within the next 30 minutes will be beneficial or detrimental. Requires a focal point (dragon claws or small bones, a pearl, or pure gold worth at least 100GP that has been crushed or melted). Maximum Intensity 4. GM should make this roll secretly.

| Intensity | Cost | Channel Roll Difficulty |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | Formidable |
| 2 | 6 | Hard |
| 3 | 9 | Standard |
| 4 | 12 | Easy |

Results on success: Yes (beneficial), No (harmful), Yes and No (both), Nothing (neither). A Fumble reveals the opposite of reality.
