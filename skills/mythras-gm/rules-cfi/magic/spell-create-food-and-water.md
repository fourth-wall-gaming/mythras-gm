**Create Food and Water** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** See Below (food lasts 24 hours)
- **Cost:** 3 +1/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates food to feed 3 creatures SIZ 20 or less OR 1 creature SIZ 21-40 per Intensity (creatures SIZ 41+ require 1 Intensity per meal). Food resembles normal food, bland but nutritious. Shelf life of 24 hours regardless of storage. After spoiling, can be made edible again by Purify Food and Water (extending safe period by another 24 hours). Also creates drinking water functioning as the Rank 1 spell Create Water.
