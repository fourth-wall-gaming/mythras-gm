**Slow** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 2 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Willpower

Halves the Movement Rate of one creature per Intensity. Those affected must be within the Area of Effect when the spell is first cast but may move freely thereafter. Affected creatures also suffer -4 to Initiative and must halve their total Action Points (including reactive uses). Cancels Haste. Does not stack with itself or similar magical effects.
