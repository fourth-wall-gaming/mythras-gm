**Flesh to Stone (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

On a failed Willpower Resistance roll, victim and all carried/worn gear transform into solid stone (suspended animation, not death). Can only be restored by the reversed spell or Wish. Damage while stoned carries over upon restoration. Large (SIZ 21-40): Easy resistance; Huge (SIZ 41+): Very Easy.

**Reverse - Stone to Flesh:** Restores a victim (no resistance needed). Also transforms non-living stone: up to 10 cubic ft. per Intensity may become soft flesh; creates a tunnel of flesh 3 ft. wide x 10 ft. long per Intensity; transforms statues or stone golems to flesh (stone golems may Willpower resist). Stone-turned-flesh retains Hit Points but has no Armor Points.
