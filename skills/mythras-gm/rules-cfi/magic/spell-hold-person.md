**Hold Person** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Cleric 2, Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 2 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 360 ft
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Willpower

Immobilizes 1 humanoid creature per Intensity within the Area of Effect. Successful Resistance roll completely negates for that target. Large humanoids (SIZ 21-40): Easy resistance; Huge (SIZ 41+): Very Easy. Held creatures are completely helpless; only Dispel Magic releases them early.
