**Feather Fall** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** Instant
- **Duration:** 1 Minute/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 5 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Reduces weight of several creatures/objects within Area (combined SIZ no more than 20 per Intensity) to that of a feather. Fall speed reduced to 10 ft. per Round (120 ft./min.); no falling damage while Duration lasts. Effectively immune to knockback greater than 5 ft. If cast on a flying creature, requires Hard Fly rolls each Round to remain aloft; success = half movement; failure = tumble out of control. Instant Casting Time allows reactive use to intercept slow-propelled projectiles (arrows, hurled spears), causing them to float harmlessly to the ground.
