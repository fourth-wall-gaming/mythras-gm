**Chant** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 30 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

All within the Area of Effect receive +5% per Intensity to all skill rolls and +1 per Intensity damage bonus. Enemies within the same area suffer the inverse penalties. Chant must be maintained throughout; casting other spells is impossible. Dispel Magic has no effect on ongoing Chant since the chanting itself is not magical; Silence nullifies it. Stacks with both Bless and Curse.
