**Silence** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 360 ft
- **Area:** 15 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** See Below

Creates complete silence in the Area of Effect. No sound is possible: conversations cease, spells with verbal components cannot be cast, etc. If cast on an object or creature, the area of silence moves with the target. Cast into open air, it remains immobile.

Duration depends on the target:
- Willing target: 2 Minutes/Intensity
- Unwilling target: 2 Rounds/Intensity

An unwilling creature may resist with Willpower. A successful resistance roll means the spell instead affects the area the creature occupies and remains immobile.
