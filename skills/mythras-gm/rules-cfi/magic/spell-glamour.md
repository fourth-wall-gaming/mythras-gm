**Glamour (R)** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Makes the target alluring; nature of glamour chosen when cast (beauty, sultrier voice, perfumed scent). No mechanical bonuses granted but ensures the recipient gains an audience, engages the attention of a desired contact, or distracts allowing accomplices to act.

**Reverse - Repugnance:** Twists appearance so target causes distaste (large wart, nasal voice, pungent odor). People turn away and make excuses to leave the victim's presence.
