**Detect Lie (R)** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 1 Minute/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Deceit

Determines whether one being is knowingly lying or withholding the truth. Does not reveal the truth, only that answers are inaccurate. Target may resist with a Formidable Opposed Roll of their Deceit vs. the original casting roll.

**Reverse - Conceal Lie:** Prevents detection of lies by magical means for 6 hours per Intensity.
