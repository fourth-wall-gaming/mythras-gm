**Cure Minor Wounds (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3 (or 1 for minor ailments)
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance or Evade for reverse)

Effects depend on the ailment: Minor Wound: heals 1d4+1 Hit Points to injured location. Serious/Major Wound: heals only 1 Hit Point but stabilizes the location, stopping all bleeding. Minor complaint (headache, cold, warts): lifts one effect for 1 Magic Point.

**Reverse - Cause Minor Wounds:** Reduces touched location by 1d4+1 HP; only 1 point to a Serious/Major location. May cause a minor malady for 1 MP. Channel roll serves as hit roll. Cannot sever location.
