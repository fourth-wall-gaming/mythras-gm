Most offensive spells allow the target a chance to resist. The target makes an **Opposed Roll** of one of the following (as specified in the spell):

- **Endurance** -- physical toughness
- **Willpower** -- mental fortitude
- **Evade** -- physical dodging (requires a Combat Action available to throw themselves clear)

The target compares their result against the caster's **Arcane Casting** or **Divine Channeling** roll.

**When the caster's skill is unknown** (Permanent-duration spells, traps, magic items), use a default casting skill:
- Rank 1 spell: 55%
- Each additional Rank: +20%
- Example: Rank 3 spell resisted as if the caster had 95% skill.

Lower Magnitude spells are always easier to resist (Magnitude is still based on Maximum Intensity regardless of chosen Intensity).
