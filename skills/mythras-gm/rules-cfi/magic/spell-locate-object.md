**Locate Object (R)** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2, Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Minute/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 60 ft./Intensity
- **Resist:** N/A

Caster is directed to a known or familiar item within the Area of Effect by sensing its direction while slowly turning. Generic items (bed, chest, stairs, gold): spell directs toward nearest instance. Specific unique items: caster must have an accurate mental image from having seen it in person within 30 feet or via magical means. Cannot locate living or non-living creatures.

| Intensity | Cost | Duration | Search Radius |
| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | 1 | 1 min. | 60 ft. |
| 2 | 2 | 2 min. | 120 ft. |
| 3 | 3 | 3 min. | 180 ft. |
| 4 | 4 | 4 min. | 240 ft. |
| +1 | +1 | +1 min. | +60 ft. |

**Reverse - Obscure Object:** Conceals one item from magical detection (crystal balls, Detect Magic, etc.) for the same Duration.
