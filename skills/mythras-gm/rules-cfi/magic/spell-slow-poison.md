**Slow Poison** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Hour/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Slows the spread of poison or venom; does not neutralize the toxin. The toxin's Onset Time does not begin until after the spell's Duration expires.

If cast on a victim after symptoms have already set in, the symptoms cease until the Duration expires, then continue as normal.

If the victim has already died of poisoning, Slow Poison cast within 10 minutes of death holds them in a coma for 1 hour per Intensity. If cured of the poison during this window, the victim may be revived by First Aid or magical healing.

Example: cobra venom has Onset Times of agony after 1d6+4 minutes and asphyxiation after 1d6+6 hours. At Intensity 1, Slow Poison delays Onset by 1 hour, after which the normal sequence resumes.
