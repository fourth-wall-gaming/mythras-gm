**Polish** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Instantly buffs one object (up to caster's POW in Things or SIZ) to a high sheen, making it glossy, shiny, and highly desirable even if the quality is sub-par. The sheen fades naturally over time.
