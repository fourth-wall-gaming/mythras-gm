**Plane Shift** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** Willpower (for unwilling targets)

Caster and up to 7 others (one per additional Intensity beyond 1) travel to another plane. All must be touching and forming a circle. Alternatively, caster may transport one creature independently to a plane of their choice (unwilling creatures may Willpower resist; success negates spell). Precise arrival is seldom as intended: roll 1d6 for direction, 1d10x10 for distance in miles. Requires rods of rare metals attuned to each destination plane (rare; value up to 10,000GP each).
