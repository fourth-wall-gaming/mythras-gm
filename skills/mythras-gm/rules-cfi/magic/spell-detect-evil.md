**Detect Evil (R)** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1, Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 5 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 90-degree Arc
- **Resist:** N/A

Detects strong evil emanations from supernatural evil creatures (demons, vampires) or mundane beings of evil or vile intention (GM's determination). Does not detect traps, poison, cursed items, etc. Can see through 3 ft. solid wood, 1 ft. stone or dirt, 1 inch solid metal. No more than one 60-degree arc per minute may be scanned.

**Reverse - Detect Good:** Functions identically but detects good emanations.
