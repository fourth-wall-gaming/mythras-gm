**Ironhand** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 10 Minutes
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Allows recipient to hold anything that would otherwise cause damage through the hands (extremely hot/cold items, acid dripping items). Can grasp burning brands, lift boiling cauldrons, reach through steam vents. Stops damage from conducting through the skin of the recipient's hands only. Does not protect anything worn on the hand (rings, gloves suffer the source effects).
