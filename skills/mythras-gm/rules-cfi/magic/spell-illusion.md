**Illusion** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration; See Below
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 50 ft. Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** Disbelieve

Creates a visual illusion of anything desired (from open meadow to a dragon) if it fits within the Area of Effect. No effect on other senses (no sound, odor, temperature, texture). Lasts while caster concentrates or until an opponent touches or enters it (causes the illusion to unravel). Caster may spend 1 Magic Point to maintain illusion after physical contact by directing it to react appropriately. The illusion does no actual damage but can convince minds of injury or temperature extremes (objects do not combust or freeze unless caster concentrates). Additional visual effects may be added as Free Actions.
