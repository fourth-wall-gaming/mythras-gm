**Fireball** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch (Range varies by Intensity)
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Evade

Two-Turn casting: first Turn forms a growing ball of fire in hand; second Turn completes incantation and the ball is hurled, detonating with a low roar. Deals 1d6 per 2 Intensity (or fraction) to all victims; damage rolled once and applied to all Hit Locations. Worn armor offers no protection; magical AP and natural armor reduce normally. Evade (going prone) = no damage; Artful Dodger = damage halved (unless within 5 ft. of edge). Ignites flammable materials = damage x5%.
