**Continual Light** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2, Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Semi-Permanent (see below)
- **Cost:** 3
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 60 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** See Below

Creates a globe of full-daylight brightness. Semi-permanent: temporarily negated by magical darkness, but returns when darkness ends. May be permanently dismissed by caster at any time. Permanently negated by Darkness or Dispel Magic at Intensity greater than the Magnitude of Continual Light. Creatures with daylight aversion suffer normal penalties within the area. If cast on a creature, Willpower roll to resist (success = spell affects area behind creature, not creature). If centered on creature's eyes and Willpower fails: partial blindness, all sight-dependent rolls are one grade harder; only removed by Cure Blindness and Deafness or Dispel Magic at Intensity greater than Magnitude. Effects are immobile but can be cast on a portable object.
