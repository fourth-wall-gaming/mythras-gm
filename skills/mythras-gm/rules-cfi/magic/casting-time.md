Spell casting time is fixed regardless of Intensity unless the spell notes otherwise.

| Casting Time | Proactive/Reactive | When It Goes Off |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Instant | Either (Proactive or Reactive) | Immediately; may be cast as a Free Action |
| 1 Action | Proactive only | Same Turn it is cast |
| 2+ Actions | Proactive only | Last Turn of the required Actions |
| Minutes / Hours | N/A | Used out of combat; no detailed tracking needed |

**Multi-Turn casting:** Action Points are spent each Turn. The casting skill roll is made on the **final Turn**. The MP cost is only expended once the full Casting Time is complete.
