**Charm, Mass** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 2 Rounds
- **Duration:** 1 Week/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 40 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** Willpower (difficulty varies by SIZ)

Functions like Charm Person and Charm Monster. May enthrall all creatures within the Area of Effect of any SIZ. Resistance by size: SIZ 1-20 (Hard roll), SIZ 21-40 (Standard roll), SIZ 41+ (Easy roll).
