**Identify** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** 1 Minute/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Item must be worn or held appropriately (helm on head, amulet over neck, weapon held, ring on finger). Successful Arcane Casting roll grants one Arcane Knowledge roll to identify one magical property. One attempt per minute of Duration. Also identifies activation words and charge counts for items with charges or Magic Point matrices. Cursed items activate when worn; successful Arcane Casting roll allows a Willpower roll to avoid curse effects (does not dispel curse but allows removal). Requires pearl worth at least 100GP ground into dust and consumed. Adding powdered Luckstone makes all properties auto-identified without further rolls.
