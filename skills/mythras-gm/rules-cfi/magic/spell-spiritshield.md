**Spiritshield** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Creates a shield around the recipient that deters spirits and ghosts from attacking or possessing them. Any spirit or ghost wishing to attack or possess the recipient must win an Opposed Test of their Willpower against the caster's Piety skill.
