**Gate** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5, Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 2 Rounds
- **Duration:** See Below (entity stays as long as it chooses)
- **Cost:** 3 +1/Intensity, +3 EXP
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** Willpower

Requires the caster to know the true name of the entity to be summoned (a planar creature: demon, devil, angel, demigod, god). Automatically gains the entity's attention. If entity succeeds Resistance roll, they typically do not come in person (something less powerful comes through instead). Costs the caster the listed Magic Points plus 3 EXP and ages them 5% of their typical lifespan (5 years for humans). Gate stays open as long as the entity remains; two-way travel is possible while open.

| Intensity | Cost | Entity Level |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | Sub-Demon or Sub-Devil |
| 2 | 4 | Minor Demon or Devil |
| 3 | 5 | Lesser Demon or Devil |
| 4 | 6 | Greater Demon or Devil |
| 5 | 7 | Demon/Devil Prince or Duke, Arch Angel, or Demi-god |
| 6 | 8 | Demon Lord, Arch-Devil, or Lesser Deity |
| 7 | 9 | Greater Deity |
| 8 | 10 | Ruling Deity |
