**Mage Lock** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Portal
- **Resist:** N/A

Permanently seals any lockable object (door, chest, tome) of up to 25 HP per Intensity unless battered open. Dispel Magic at Intensity greater than Magnitude: opens for 1 Round only. Knock: temporarily opens for 10 minutes, then Mage Lock resumes. A magic-user of higher Rank than caster may pass freely without breaking the enchantment (and may hold it for others). Unlike Secure Portal, not automatically bypassed by extra-planar creatures or material-passing spells.

| Intensity | Cost | Max Portal HP | Examples |
| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | 25 | Shackles, padlocks, typical wooden chest/door |
| 2 | 4 | 50 | Wooden reinforced chest/door |
| 3 | 5 | 75 | Iron chest, door, or portcullis |
| 4 | 6 | 120 | Small castle gate |
| 5 | 7 | 150 | Large reinforced castle gate |
