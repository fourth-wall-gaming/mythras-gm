**Prayer** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** 10 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 60 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Blesses caster and all allies within Area of Effect (+5% to all skill and Resistance rolls, +1 to all damage); enemies receive -5% penalty and -1 damage reduction. May stack with Chant (if same cleric or another of same religion) for +10%/+2 vs. -10%/-2.
