**Heal (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 4
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3 +1/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Creature
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance or Evade for reverse)

Each Intensity of Heal cures ONE of: any disease (any POT), blindness or deafness, a mental disorder from injury or magic, OR heals one Hit Location of up to 12 HP. Cannot restore a severed limb (requires Regenerate). No time limit on injuries treated (unlike lesser healing spells). To heal an individual with burns on all body locations would require 7 Intensity (one per location).

**Reverse - Harm:** Inflicts 12 points of magical injury to one Hit Location on a failed Resistance roll; or inflicts a disease at Cause Disease's POT +20. Channel roll = hit roll. No armor protection; magical AP reduce damage. Each additional Intensity affects another contiguous Hit Location. No effect on undead, creatures only harmed by iron/silver/magic, or non-corporeal beings.
