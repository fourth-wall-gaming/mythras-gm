**Fireball, Delayed Blast** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 50 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Evade

Functions as Fireball except: inflicts 1d8 per 2 Intensity (fraction thereof) and can be set to detonate at any time between instantly and 5 minutes after casting. Travels to designated spot and remains inert (a tiny floating cinder) until detonation time.

| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |

| 2 | 4 | 100 ft. | 1d8 |
| 3 | 5 | 150 ft. | 2d8 |
| 4 | 6 | 200 ft. | 2d8 |
| 5 | 7 | 250 ft. | 3d8 |
| 6 | 8 | 300 ft. | 3d8 |
| +1 | +1 | Continue | Continue |
