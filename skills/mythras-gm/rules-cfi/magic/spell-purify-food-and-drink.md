**Purify Food and Drink (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 5 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Removes poison, rot, and contaminants from an average-sized meal of food and/or water per Intensity within the area. Also destroys malignant properties of unholy water.

**Reverse - Contaminate Food and Drink:** Spoils food, makes pure water undrinkable, and corrupts sacred properties of holy water.
