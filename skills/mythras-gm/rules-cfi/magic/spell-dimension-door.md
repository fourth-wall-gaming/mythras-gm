**Dimension Door** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Instantly jumps up to 100 ft. per Intensity without crossing intervening space, with no chance of error. Destination may be visible or specified by direction/distance. Arriving in mid-air causes falling damage (unless saved by Feather Fall). Jumping into a solid object traps caster in Astral Plane. All physically carried equipment travels along. Living matter of no more than SIZ 20 may accompany caster. Caster (and any transported beings) require a full Round to recover; may only defend during this time.
