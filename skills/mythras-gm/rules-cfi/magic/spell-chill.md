**Chill (R)** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Dramatically reduces the temperature of a small object (ENC no greater than 1 Thing) down to the temperature of ice water. Does not freeze the object or damage its structure; merely makes it very cold.

**Reverse - Heat:** Dramatically increases temperature of a small object up to boiling water temperature. Useful for mulling wine, cooking, warming a bed. Does not affect living tissue, though it can affect clothing and armor (somewhat uncomfortable but does not hinder wearer).
