**Enchant Item** (Enchantment)

- **Rank:** Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** See Below (1d8+2 days)
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3 + See Below
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Item
- **Resist:** N/A

Requires item of finest construction (at least 100GP in embellishments) and a laboratory/workshop. Caster must touch the item throughout the 1d8+2-day casting period (must remain within arm's reach during rest/sleep). No other spell or magic may be performed during casting.

Once successfully cast, caster has 24 hours to imbue the item with a desired spell (from memory or scroll). Imbuing costs normal Magic Points for the desired Intensity and takes 1d12 hours per spell Rank; an Arcane Casting roll is required at the end. Multiple spells may be imbued (each within 24 hours of the previous).

Each imbued spell may be cast only once unless followed by Permanency. It is not possible to imbue non-spell magical effects.
