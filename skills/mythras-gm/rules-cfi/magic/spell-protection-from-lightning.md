**Protection from Lightning** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 15 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

When cast on self: protects from electrical-based damage (blue dragon breath, shocking grasp, electrical traps), absorbing 12 points per Intensity. Once field is depleted, excess from that attack is halved before being applied. When cast on others: electrical attack damage is halved.

| Intensity | Cost | Duration | Self: Electrical Absorption |
| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | 15 min. | 12 points |
| 2 | 4 | 30 min. | 24 points |
| 3 | 5 | 45 min. | 36 points |
| 4 | 6 | 60 min. | 48 points |
| +1 | +1 | +15 min. | +12 points |
