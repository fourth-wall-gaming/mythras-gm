**Power Word - Kill** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity, +3 EXP
- **Range:** 20 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 10 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Endurance

Kills creatures in the Area of Effect who fail Endurance. Each casting automatically costs the caster 1 Fatigue level. Intensity required depends on target Rank or SIZ (whichever is more beneficial to target):

| Target Rank | Target SIZ | Intensity to Kill |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 0-1 | 1-20 | 1 Intensity; or 4 creatures SIZ 20 or less |
| 2 | 21-40 | 2 Intensity |
| 3 | 41-60 | 3 Intensity |
| 4-5 | 61+ | No effect |

Creatures SIZ 61+ and Rank 4+ are unaffected. Unlike Death Spell, victims may be raised by Wish, Raise Dead, Resurrection, or Reincarnation.
