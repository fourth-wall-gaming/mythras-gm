Each spell is defined by these standard trait fields:

**Spell Name (R):** "(R)" marks a Reversible spell. The caster chooses normal or reversed version when memorizing. Both versions may be memorized simultaneously (as separate spell slots).

**(School):** One of eight Schools of Magic. Relevant to Arcane casters. Spells of the School of Enchantment note "(Charm)" as a reminder that elves and half-elves are typically immune. Arcane spells do not list a Sphere.

**Rank:** The spell's Rank per Class. A spell may be different Ranks for Cleric vs. Mage.

**Sphere:** Divine spells only. 16 Spheres: All, Animal, Astral, Charm, Combat, Creation, Divination, Elemental, Guardian, Healing, Necromantic, Plant, Protection, Summoning, Sun, Weather. A parenthetical Sphere indicates the reversed version's Sphere. Deities access specific Spheres of Influence.

**Cost:** MP to cast. Formats: "1" (fixed), "1, +1/additional Intensity", "3/Intensity", or similar. "(+1 EXP)" = additional Experience Roll sacrifice.

**Area:** Area of effect. Types: Caster, 1+ Targets, Cone, Path, Radius, Wedge. Radius = distance from center to edge (so 20' radius = 40' across). Area "0" = caster or touched only.

**Casting Time:** Instant | 1+ Actions | Minutes | Hours. Not typically affected by Intensity.

**Duration:** Fixed or variable (e.g., "5 Minutes/Intensity"). Special values: Instant (ends immediately), Permanent (lasting effect), Concentration (ends when caster stops concentrating), Trigger (delayed up to 4 hours unless defined as Permanent until triggered).

**Range:** Touch | 0 (caster only) | set distance. Variable ranges scale with Intensity (e.g., "20'/Intensity" at 3 Intensity = 60').

**Resist:** Disbelief | Evade | Endurance | Willpower. Evade requires a Combat Action available. Disbelief is situation-dependent (see Illusions).

**Limiting traits:** When multiple traits scale with Intensity, the caster may limit some while maximizing others. Static traits (e.g., Fireball's 20' radius) may not be altered.
