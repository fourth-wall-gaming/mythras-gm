**Calm** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Cleric 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Dampens the Passions of a SIZ 1-20 humanoid, ensuring a lovesick suitor does not press, a frightened rival does not scream, or that weapons are not drawn in anger. A calmed person is not otherwise mentally affected; assault or threats still permit defense and attack, but the target does so in a calm and level-headed manner.
