**Cure Serious Wounds (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance or Evade for reverse)

Heals one Hit Location of 1d6+2 points of injury, provided the injury is no more severe than a Serious Wound. Limited effect on Major Wounds: heals 2 Hit Points and stabilizes the injury (prevents death).

**Reverse - Cause Serious Wounds:** Reduces a single Hit Location by 1d6+2 HP. Only causes 2 points to a Major Wound location. Channel roll serves as hit roll. Cannot sever but can crush or mutilate. No armor protection; magical AP reduce damage.
