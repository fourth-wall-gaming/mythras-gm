**Befuddle** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Turn
- **Duration:** 30 Minutes
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Causes confusion in a corporeal target. The subject has difficulty thinking straight and cannot initiate any constructive activity until the spell ends. Target can still act in self-defense. Any attack or threatening action instantly breaks the spell, whether or not directed at the befuddled target.
