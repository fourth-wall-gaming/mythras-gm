**Dispel Magic** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2, Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 360 ft
- **Area:** 15 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** N/A

Cast against a specific target or area. All magic within 15 ft. of target point is susceptible. Ends ongoing spells but does nothing to magical creatures or undead. Eliminates all magic with Magnitude less than spell's Intensity. Starts with most powerful magic first; if it fails to eliminate any spell, the spell ends and no more magic is eliminated. Magic items and Charm-type spells must be targeted individually. May be used defensively to neutralize incoming spells via the Counter Spell Reactive Action.
