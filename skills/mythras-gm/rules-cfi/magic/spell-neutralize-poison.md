**Neutralize Poison (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance)

Cancels poison/venom on a creature; can cure someone who "died" of poisoning if used within 10 minutes of death. 1 Intensity per 30 Potency. May neutralize venom of a poisonous creature (snake, scorpion) for 24 hours (same cost). May purify 5 lbs. of solids or 2 gallons of liquid per Intensity.

| Intensity | Cost | Effect |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | Cure POT 1-30 poison; neutralize POT 1-30 creature; treat 5 lbs./2 gal. |
| 2 | 4 | Cure POT 31-60; neutralize POT 31-60 creature; treat 10 lbs./4 gal. |
| 3 | 5 | Cure POT 61-90; neutralize POT 61-90 creature; treat 15 lbs./6 gal. |
| 4 | 6 | Cure POT 91-120; neutralize POT 91-120 creature; treat 20 lbs./8 gal. |

**Reverse - Inflict Poison:** Creates a contact toxin in a victim. POT = Intensity x30. Onset: 1d2+1 Rounds to Agony, then Asphyxiation (usually death). Duration: 1d3x10 Minutes. Only Slow Poison or Neutralize Poison can treat it.
