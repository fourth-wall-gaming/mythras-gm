Arcane spellcasters (mages) record all known spells in **spell books** (also called grimoires).

- Spells are written in a shared system of ancient script, detailed notations, and magical formulas used by all Arcane casters regardless of language, culture, or species.
- Each caster adds personal variations that make the writings incomprehensible to others without **Read Magic**.
- A mage can always read their **own** spell book without any roll.
- Once a spell in another's book has been successfully deciphered with Read Magic, no further deciphering is needed.

**Practical notes:**
- A mage deprived of their spell book can only cast spells currently in memory.
- As a mage's collection grows, they may maintain a **traveling spell book** with commonly used spells and keep the full library safe at home.
- Multiple volumes may be maintained; there is no stated limit on the number of books.
