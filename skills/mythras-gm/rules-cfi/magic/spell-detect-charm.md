**Detect Charm (R)** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target per Minute
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows caster to discern if selected creatures are under Charm effects. Requires 1 minute of Concentration to scan each creature.

**Reverse - Hide Charm:** Shields one creature from magical charm detection for 6 hours/Intensity.
