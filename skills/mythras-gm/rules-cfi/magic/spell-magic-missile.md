**Magic Missile** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 1-5 Targets
- **Resist:** See Below (cannot be evaded)

Missiles of magical energy dart from caster's fingertips and strike unerringly. Number of missiles based on caster's Arcane Casting skill (2nd missile at 60%, 3rd at 70%, 4th at 80%, 5th at 90%); Intensity must equal number of missiles desired. Maximum Intensity 5. Cannot be evaded; target in total cover cannot be targeted. Shield spell automatically deflects Magic Missiles if aware and facing caster. Each missile: 1d6+1 damage; worn armor provides no protection; natural armor and magical AP reduce normally; no damage to inanimate objects. Multiple missiles can target one creature or several; each strikes a random Hit Location.

| Intensity | Cost | Range | Missiles (with sufficient skill) |
| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | 1 | 100 ft. | 1 |
| 2 | 2 | 200 ft. | 2 (if 60% skill) |
| 3 | 3 | 300 ft. | 3 (if 70% skill) |
| 4 | 4 | 400 ft. | 4 (if 80% skill) |
| 5 | 5 | 500 ft. | 5 (if 90% skill) - max |
