**Shield** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 5 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates an invisible mobile disk of force hovering in front of the magic-user. The shield intercepts attacks coming through the caster's front facing. No skill test required beyond casting; the spell automatically interposes.

| Type of Attack | Difficulty to Strike Caster |
| :-- | :-- |
| Magic Missiles only | Impossible (automatically blocked) |
| Hurled attacks (thrown axes, spears, rocks) | Herculean |
| Device-propelled attacks (arrows, crossbow bolts, sling stones) | Formidable |
| Other physical (swords, bites, claws) | Hard |
| Evadeable spells (Fireball, Lightning Bolt, Cone of Cold) | Normal difficulty; no damage on successful Evade, half on failure |
