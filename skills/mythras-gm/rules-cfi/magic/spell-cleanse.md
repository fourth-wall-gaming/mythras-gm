**Cleanse (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** Willpower

Rids an object, person, or small area of dirt, grease, grime, bad smells, etc. Does not organize or tidy, merely cleans to spotless condition. Can clean an area equal to POW x5 square feet (a 5-ft. square is 25 sq. ft.).

**Reverse - Dishevel:** Immediately covers affected objects in grime, dust, cobwebs, etc. Can make new objects look old and weathered or prevent well-turned-out people from being recognized.
