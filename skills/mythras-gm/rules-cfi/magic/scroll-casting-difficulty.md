When casting a scroll spell above the caster's current Rank:

| Difference | Required Roll |
| :-- | :-- |
| Spell is caster's Rank or lower | No skill roll required |
| Spell is 1 Rank higher | Successful skill roll |
| Spell is 2 Ranks higher | Successful **Hard** skill roll |
| Spell is 3 Ranks higher | Successful **Formidable** skill roll |
| Spell is 4 Ranks higher | Successful **Herculean** skill roll |

**Failure:** Spell not cast; erased from scroll.
**Fumble:** Spell miscast -- typically affects the reader and/or allies, or has the opposite effect of what was desired.
