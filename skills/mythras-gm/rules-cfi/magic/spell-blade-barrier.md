**Blade Barrier** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 4
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 3 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 5-30 ft. radius
- **Resist:** See Below

Creates a barrier of whirling blades around a fixed point. Any creature passing through takes 1d8 points of damage to 1d4+2 random Hit Locations. Worn armor offers only half protection; magical AP reduces damage normally. Cannot be Parried or Evaded (blades are too fast and densely packed). A shield may passively protect covered Hit Locations normally.

May be cast defensively (protecting allies) or offensively (trapping enemies). Creatures within 5 feet of the edge as it forms may make an Evade roll to escape; regardless of success they end up prone just outside. Success = no damage; failure = full effect.
