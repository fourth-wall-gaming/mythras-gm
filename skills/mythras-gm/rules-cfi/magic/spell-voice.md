**Voice** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Cleric 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** See Below
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Amplifies the recipient's intonation and delivery so their speech becomes compelling: all who can hear the speaker are forced to listen. The voice also carries through the loudest background noise (howling gales, waterfalls, battlefield clashes) to a Range of 30 x the recipient's CHA in feet.
