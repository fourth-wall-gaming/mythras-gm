While performing magic (any discipline), characters are restricted to:

- Moving at a **maximum of walking pace**.
- Performing **Free or Reactive Actions** only.

Characters **cannot** attack, run, or perform any other skill while preparing a spell.

At the GM's discretion, Reactive Actions such as **Parrying** or **Evading** may make completing the spell more difficult due to disruption of somatic gestures or mental focus.
