**Web** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 20 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Evade, plus See Below

Long strands of sticky webbing shoot from the caster's hand, anchored floor-to-ceiling or wall-to-wall to block passage, or sprayed onto opponents. A successful Evade roll (costing 1 Action Point) puts a target outside the Area of Effect. A failed roll leaves the victim immobilized with 1d3 Hit Locations entangled.

Effects by entangled location:
- Arm: cannot use what it is holding
- Chest, abdomen, or leg: all physical skill rolls one grade harder (except Brawn to break free)
- Head: causes Asphyxiation

On subsequent Turns, the victim may spend an Action Point to attempt to break free: Opposed Roll of Brawn vs. the web's Stickiness (equal to the caster's Arcane Casting skill). The web has 6 Armor Points and Hit Points equal to 3x Intensity. Fire damage ignores the web's Armor Points but webs are not flammable by normal means. Cutting tools used to free a victim are subject to the same entanglement process.
