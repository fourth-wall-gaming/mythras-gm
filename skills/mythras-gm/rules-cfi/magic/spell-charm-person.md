**Charm Person** (Enchantment (Charm))

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Week/Intensity (max 4 weeks)
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 360 ft
- **Area:** 1 living humanoid SIZ 1-20
- **Resist:** Willpower

One living (undead unaffected) sapient humanoid SIZ 1-20 that fails Resistance ceases hostilities and offers to help and protect the caster. Subject acts based on what it thinks is best to help/protect, never obeying suicidal orders. Any hostile act by caster or allies against the charmed subject breaks the enchantment. Caster must be able to speak with the charmed one for complex requests. After initial roll, 1 week passes before another Resistance roll may be made. Caster's Magic Point total is reduced by casting cost for the Duration (Enduring Magic Costs).
