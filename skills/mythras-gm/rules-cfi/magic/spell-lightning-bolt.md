**Lightning Bolt** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 50 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 10 ft. wide path
- **Resist:** Evade

Two-Turn casting: first Turn forms electrical arcing between fingers; second Turn fires a lightning blast in a straight 10 ft. wide line. Damage 1d6 per 2 Intensity (fraction thereof), rolled once and applied to all Hit Locations. Both worn and natural armor offer no protection; magical AP reduce damage normally. Evade (prone) = no damage; Artful Dodger = damage halved (unless within 5 ft. of edge). Ignites flammable materials = damage x1%. May shatter or destroy inanimate objects.
