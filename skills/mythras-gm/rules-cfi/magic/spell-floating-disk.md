**Floating Disk** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 30 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Area:** 3 ft. wide disk
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates a shimmering force disk 3 ft. in diameter that always remains 3 ft. above ground. Carries any goods not exceeding 20 Things per Intensity (roughly 6 SIZ per Intensity, as 1 SIZ = 3 Things). Concave shape prevents items from rolling off. If not directed, stays within 5 ft. of caster (moves at 15 ft. Movement Rate). Can be directed to any point within 60 ft. by concentrating. Cannot rise above 3 ft. If caster allows it to go beyond 60 ft., it vanishes, dropping all cargo.
