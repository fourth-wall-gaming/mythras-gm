**Know Alignment (R)** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1, Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Round/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

On a failed Resistance roll: first Round reveals ethical code (Law/Neutrality/Chaos); further concentration for another Round reveals moral Alignment (Good/Neutrality/Evil); each additional Round reveals one other random Passion. Strength revealed: 01-29% Weak, 30-69% Average, 70-99% Strong, 100%+ Very Strong.

**Reverse - Obscure Alignment:** Makes target's Alignment and Passions unreadable for 24 hours (including Know Alignment and Detect Evil/Good).
