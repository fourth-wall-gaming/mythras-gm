**Resurrection (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5
- **Casting Time:** 10 Minutes
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity, +2 EXP
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Willpower for non-evil souls)

Raises any creature dead no more than 20 years per Intensity, even if only a tiny remnant (e.g., a finger bone) remains. The creature is restored to full Hit Points and all lost limbs are regrown. No period of rest is required; the subject is fully functional immediately. No effect on a creature that died of old age.

Non-evil souls may resist with an Opposed Willpower roll. Evil souls: the caster must overcome their tormentor's Willpower (assume 20+ points above the deceased's if unknown).

The cleric ages 3% of their typical lifespan (3 years for a human) per casting.

**Reverse - Destruction:** Must touch the victim. Target resists with Willpower modified by Rank or SIZ (whichever benefits the target):

| Target Rank | Target SIZ | Willpower Modifier |
| :-: | :-: | :-: |
| 0-1* | 1-20 | Formidable |
| 2 | 21-40 | Hard |
| 3 | 41-60 | Standard |
| 4 | 61-80 | Easy |
| 5 | 81+ | Very Easy |

*Includes characters without a Class.

Failed Resistance roll: victim is slain and reduced to dust; only Wish can restore them. Successful roll: victim suffers -1d4 Hit Points to the touched location. Armor offers no protection. Destruction does not age the caster.
