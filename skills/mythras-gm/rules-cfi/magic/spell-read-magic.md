**Read Magic** (All)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

First spell taught to apprentice magic-users. Allows reading of magical writings created by another spellcaster. Caster's own magical writings are always legible to them. Reading magical script does not activate contained spells (except cursed scrolls, which are activated). When identifying a scroll or spell book: 1 minute per spell Rank (Rank 1 takes 1 min., Rank 2 takes 2 min., etc.). Once a set of inscriptions is identified, need not cast Read Magic again for the same document; separate scrolls or books require new castings. Usable by all Schools of Magic.
