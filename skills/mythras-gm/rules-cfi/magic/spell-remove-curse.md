**Remove Curse (R)** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2 / Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Removes the effects of any curse afflicting a creature or item. Does not destroy the cursed nature of an item (weapon, shield, armor), but allows a creature afflicted by such an item to be rid of it. Some curses require a minimum Intensity noted in their write-up. Either Cure Disease or Remove Curse may cure lycanthropy, requiring Intensity 12 in either case.

**Reverse - Bestow Curse:** Inflicts a curse on one enemy. Duration 30 minutes/Intensity. Target resists with Opposed Willpower. Roll 1d100 for curse type:

- 01-25: All skills using STR, DEX, or CON one grade more difficult; Initiative -4; Damage Modifier reduced 2 steps.
- 26-50: All skills using INT, POW, or CHA one grade more difficult; Initiative -4.
- 51-75: Combat Skills and Resistance rolls 2 grades more difficult.
- 76-00: All failed skill rolls treated as Fumbles.
