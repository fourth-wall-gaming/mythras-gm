**Control Weather** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5, Mage 4
- **Casting Time:** 10 Minutes
- **Duration:** 1d6 Hours/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3 +1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 1d4 square miles/Intensity
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows caster to alter the current weather in a significant way. After casting, 1d4x10 minutes must pass for effects to fully manifest. May alter Precipitation, Temperature, and Wind by up to +/-2 grades (as per conversion tables).
