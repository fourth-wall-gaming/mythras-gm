Spells with Duration based on Intensity do not need to be cast at maximum Intensity. Instead, the caster may cast at Intensity 1 and **extend** duration period by period:

- At the start of each new Duration period, the caster decides whether to **maintain** the spell or let it end.
- To maintain: spend the required MP, and the spell continues for another Duration period.
- The caster must **concentrate throughout** the Duration, even for spells that do not normally require Concentration.
- If Concentration lapses for a normally non-Concentration spell: the spell finishes out its current Duration period then ends.
- If Concentration lapses for a Concentration spell: the spell ends immediately.
- Maximum maintained Intensity is still capped at the spell's **Magnitude** (= skill/10).

**Example:** Detect Magic has Duration 2 Minutes/Intensity. Cast at Intensity 1 for 2 minutes; caster may spend 1 MP to extend another 2 minutes, maintaining Concentration throughout. If the caster stops concentrating after the second period starts, the spell runs out its 2 minutes and ends.
