**Levitate** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 10 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 60 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower (for unwilling subjects)

Target of up to 20 SIZ (+10 per additional Intensity) is levitated off the ground. Other creatures may be moved vertically at 5 ft. per Combat Round; caster levitating themselves moves up to 10 ft. per Round. No horizontal movement granted (subject may pull along wall or cliff if within reach). Concentration only required when changing elevation. Unwilling subjects allowed Willpower Resistance roll.
