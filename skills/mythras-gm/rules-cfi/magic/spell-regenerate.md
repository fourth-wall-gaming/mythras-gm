**Regenerate (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 4
- **Casting Time:** 3 Minutes
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Creature
- **Resist:** N/A (Parry or Evade for reverse)

Touch one creature to regenerate a dismembered Hit Location. Lost limbs grow back at 1 HP per 10 minutes until fully healed. If severed location is present and touching the victim: recovery time reduced to 1 minute. Cannot clone an individual (only portion attached to brain/CNS benefits). Each Intensity regenerates a separate Hit Location.

**Reverse - Wither:** Causes a touched location to shrivel and eventually fall off. The location loses 1 HP per 10 minutes; becomes unusable at 0 HP; falls off when fully negative. Only negated by Regenerate.
