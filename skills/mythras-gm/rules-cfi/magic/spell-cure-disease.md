**Cure Disease (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance or Evade for reverse)

Cures any mundane or magical disease with Potency < Intensity x30. Cures parasitic afflictions: green slime or rot grubs (Intensity 1), lycanthropy (Intensity 12+, must be cast within 72 hours). Provides no future immunity.

| Intensity | Cost | Effect |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | Cure POT 30 disease, green slime, or rot grubs |
| 2 | 6 | Cure POT 60 disease |
| 3 | 9 | Cure POT 90 disease |
| 4 | 12 | Cure POT 120 disease, lycanthropy (if within 72 hrs) |

**Reverse - Cause Disease:** Inflicts a magical disease on a target. Intensity x30 must equal or exceed the Potency of chosen disease. Recovery limited to Remove Disease or Cure. Healing skill has no effect on magically inflicted diseases.
