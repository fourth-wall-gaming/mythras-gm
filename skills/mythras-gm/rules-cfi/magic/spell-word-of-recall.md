**Word of Recall** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 4
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** The caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Instantly teleports the cleric to a pre-designated sanctuary (an arrival point no larger than 10 ft. radius). There is no range limit, including travel between planes.

**Mishap chances:**
- Within one plane: no mishap
- Crossing 1 plane: Fumble on 91-00
- Crossing 2 planes: Fumble on 81-00
- Each additional plane reduces the safe range by 10 (Fumble on 71-00, 61-00, etc.)

This replaces the caster's normal Fumble chance. A Fumble when crossing planes means the cleric is irrevocably lost. Fumbles within a single plane follow normal rules.

The cleric may also transport 3 additional objects per Intensity, each no larger than SIZ/9 Things. Transported objects may be treasure, equipment, or living beings. Living beings transported must be covered by sufficient Intensity to account for their carried equipment.
