**Exorcism** (Abjuration)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 1d10x100 Minutes (random; cannot be interrupted)
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 10 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Drives out a spirit or supernatural force possessing a corporeal being. Also works against domination by objects/magic items, spells, curses, and charms. Exorcises possessions with Intensity up to half the spell's Intensity. Belligerent discorporate entities may engage in Spirit Combat targeting nearby creatures; if they win, they may possess the loser. If they lose, they return to their plane of origin. The random Casting Time cannot be interrupted without disrupting the ritual.
