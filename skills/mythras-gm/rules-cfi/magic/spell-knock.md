**Knock (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 1 Portal
- **Resist:** N/A

Opens one door, chest, shackles, chains, or secret door: locked, stuck, Held, or Mage Locked. Portal can have up to 25 HP per Intensity. Max 2 different aspects affected per casting (e.g., a door that is barred AND locked requires 2 castings). Against Mage Lock: enchantment suspended for 10 minutes, then reverts. No effect on portcullises or gates. Even welded apertures break free.

| Intensity | Cost | Max Portal HP | Examples |
| :-: | :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | 1 | 25 | Shackles, padlocks, typical wooden chest or door |
| 2 | 2 | 50 | Wooden reinforced chest or door |
| 3 | 3 | 75 | Iron chest or door |

**Reverse - Lock:** Closes and locks one portal; up to 2 functions (lock, bolted, barred) may be set.
