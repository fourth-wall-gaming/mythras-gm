**Flame Strike** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 100 ft. +20 ft./additional Intensity
- **Area:** 5 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** Evade

A 30-ft. high column of flame streaks down from above onto a designated point. Deals 1d8 per 2 Intensity (fraction thereof) to all victims in the Area of Effect; damage rolled once and applied to all Hit Locations. Worn armor offers half protection; magical AP and natural armor reduce damage normally. Evade (going prone, rolling out) = no damage; Artful Dodger Evade = no damage, may freely move outside Area. Ignites flammable materials = damage x5%.
