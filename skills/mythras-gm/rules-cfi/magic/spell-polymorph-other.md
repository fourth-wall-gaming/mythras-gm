**Polymorph Other** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity (+1 EXP)
- **Range:** 15 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** Willpower

Alters form of one corporeal creature; each Intensity changes SIZ by up to 5 points. Target gets Willpower roll to resist. Large (SIZ 21-40): Easy; Huge (SIZ 41+): Very Easy. Transformation takes 1 Round. Second Willpower roll to retain personality (failure = stunned 1d10 min.; Fumble = death from mental shock). Doubling/halving SIZ or changing environment makes personality roll one grade harder. Grants natural attacks, movement modes, breathing type, Hit Points, Damage Modifier; does NOT grant magical abilities. Each day subject must repeat personality roll until failed, at which point mentality fully changes. Only Wish restores lost identity. All worn/carried equipment melds into new form.
