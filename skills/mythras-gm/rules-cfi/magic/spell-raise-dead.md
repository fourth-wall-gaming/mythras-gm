**Raise Dead (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 3
- **Casting Time:** 1 Minute
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity, +1 EXP
- **Range:** 100 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Willpower for non-evil souls who may resist returning)

May be cast on corpse dead less than 24 hours per Intensity. Non-evil souls may resist with Opposed Willpower roll (GM may waive for heroic reasons). Evil souls: caster must overcome the tormentor's Willpower (20+ points above deceased's Willpower). On success: subject returns to life with full HP. Severed limbs remain lost (must be physically reattached beforehand). Other maladies (poison, disease) must be treated separately. Subject reduced to Incapacitated Fatigue for 24 hours per day dead (Cure Fatigue ineffective during this period). No effect on creature that died of old age.

**Reverse - Slay Living:** Kills one touched victim. Target resists with Willpower (Hard for Rank 0-1/SIZ 1-20; Standard for Rank 2/SIZ 21-40; Easy for Rank 3/SIZ 41-60; Very Easy for Rank 4-5/SIZ 61+). Failed roll: killed outright. Success: treated as Cause Serious Wounds. No armor protection.
