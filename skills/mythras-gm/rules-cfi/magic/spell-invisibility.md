**Invisibility** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 6 Hours/Intensity (max 24 hours)
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Subject and all carried gear become invisible to normal vision. Does not prevent detection by hearing, touch, smell, Life Sense, Sonar, etc. Lasts for full Duration unless dispelled or subject makes an attack (spell ends after attack, subject reappears and attack recipient benefits from Surprise rules). Non-combat actions (opening doors, talking, climbing, running, pickpocketing, spell casting without directly harming enemies) do not end the spell. Many such actions may still allow Perception checks to detect indirect evidence.
