**Secure Portal** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Minute/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 60 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 1 Portal
- **Resist:** N/A

Holds shut one door, portcullis, gate, or similar aperture of wood, stone, or metal as if securely locked. The portal may be battered down but will not otherwise open for the Duration. Caster may end the spell at any time. Negated by Dispel Magic (cast at greater Intensity) or Knock (any Intensity). Automatically bypassed by extraplanar creatures (demons, devils, angels, elementals) that can pass through solid matter. Does not affect Blink or similar spells.
