**Sun Ray** (Evocation, Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 1d4+1 Rounds
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** 5 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** See Below

The cleric may summon a brilliant beam of natural sunlight using a Cast Magic Action at any point during the Duration. The beam has a 5 ft. radius.

**Blinding:** Creatures in the Area of Effect must Evade or be blinded for 1d3 minutes. A failed roll also blinds Dark Vision users for 2d4 minutes and suppresses their Dark Vision out to 20 ft. beyond the area for 1d4+1 minutes. Creatures harmed by natural sunlight are blinded permanently on a failed roll, or for 2d6 Rounds on success.

**Undead damage:** Undead in the Area take 2d6 damage to each Hit Location; no armor protection, but magical Armor Points reduce damage normally. Undead within 5 ft. of the edge may Evade for no damage. Undead within 20 ft. of the Area take 1d6+1 damage (no damage on a successful Evade). Sunlight-vulnerable undead (vampires) are destroyed if they fail to Evade.

**Fungi:** Subterranean fungi and fungoid creatures suffer the same effects as undead but cannot make a Resistance roll.
