**Light (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 1, Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Hour/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** See Below

Creates a globe of light at a chosen spot (object or thin air); may be dismissed at any time. If cast on a creature: Willpower roll to resist (success = spell affects area behind creature). If centered on creature's eyes and Willpower fails: partial blindness for Duration (all sight-dependent skill/Resistance rolls are one grade harder). Effects immobile but can be cast on a portable object.

The divine version may be reversed to create Darkness (functions as the Arcane Darkness spell but with Duration of 30 minutes per Intensity).
