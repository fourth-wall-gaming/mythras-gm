**Water Breathing (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2 / Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 6 hours/Intensity + 1d4 hours
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 or more Targets
- **Resist:** N/A

Grants the ability to breathe water for the Duration. Multiple recipients may be touched while casting; in that case the total Duration is divided by the number of subjects. The exact Duration is rolled secretly by the GM so recipients do not know precisely how much time remains.

The recipient retains their normal breathing capability as well (a fish given Air Breathing can still breathe underwater).

| Intensity | Cost | Variable Effect |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 3 | 6 hours + 1d4 hours total |
| 2 | 4 | 12 hours + 1d4 hours total |
| 3 | 5 | 18 hours + 1d4 hours total |
| 4 | 6 | 24 hours + 1d4 hours total |
| +1 | +1 | Continue progression |

**Reverse - Air Breathing:** Allows a creature otherwise unable to breathe oxygen to do so for the Duration.
