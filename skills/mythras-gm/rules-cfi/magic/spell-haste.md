**Haste** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 3 Rounds/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 180 ft
- **Area:** 20 ft. Radius, 1 Target/Intensity
- **Resist:** N/A

One creature per Intensity within the area gains double Movement Rate, +4 Initiative bonus, and 2 Actions per Turn for each AP used. Does not increase spell casting speed or Duration. Spell casters under Haste do not cast faster and cannot benefit from the extra Action in the same Turn spells are cast. Ages each target 1% of normal life expectancy (approximately 1 year for humans) regardless of Duration. Cancels Slow (no aging in that case). Does not stack with itself or similar magic.
