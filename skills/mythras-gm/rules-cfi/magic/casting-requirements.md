All spellcasters use ritual words and gestures to focus Concentration. The following requirements must ALL be met:

1. The spell must have been previously **memorized**.
2. The caster must have the **requisite MP** remaining (insufficient MP means the spell simply does not work).
3. The caster must be able to **gesture or symbolize** with at least 1 free hand.
4. The caster must be able to **make appropriate vocal sounds** to invoke the spell.
5. The caster must be able to **accurately perceive or sense** the target (for targeted spells).
6. The caster must possess the required **generic material spell components**.

Being denied even one of these requirements prevents casting any spell, unless the specific spell notes otherwise.

**Skill roll:** On the **last Turn** of casting, the caster makes an **Arcane Casting** or **Divine Channeling** skill check. Failure typically means the spell simply does not work, or at worst the memorized spell is forgotten (see Casting Cost for Failure/Fumble details).
