A **phantasm** is a manifestation created within the minds of one or more specific living creatures. They cannot manifest as quasi-reality.

- Most phantasms instill intense emotion (typically fear and terror).
- Only the originally targeted creatures experience the phantasm; people who enter the area later see nothing unusual.
- Phantasms **cannot be touched** and cannot be dispelled by physical contact.

**Duration and Area interactions:** A phantasm with a 60' radius and 10-minute Duration cast on a party will remain in their minds throughout the Duration even if they leave the area. A new group entering the area sees nothing.

**Disbelief:** The GM secretly makes the Disbelief roll as soon as victims are subjected to the spell (to not hint at its nature). Phantasms are **2 Difficulty Grades harder** to Disbelieve than illusions because they are rooted in the psyche.

**Dispel Magic:** Must target a single character suffering the phantasm's effects and overcome the spell's Magnitude to remove it from that victim's mind.
