**Earthquake** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5, Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 1 Minute
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 50 ft./Intensity
- **Area:** 10 ft. Radius/Intensity
- **Resist:** Evade

Failing to resist causes the victim to fall prone at minimum. In vulnerable locations (building, forest, cliff), failing Evade also means struck by falling debris. Worn armor offers half protection; natural armor and magical AP reduce damage normally.
