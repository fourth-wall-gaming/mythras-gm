**Fear** (Illusion)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 5 ft. x 20 ft. Cone
- **Resist:** Willpower

A wave of terror causes those failing Resistance to flee directly away from caster at sprinting speed for Intensity x2 Rounds. Those who Fumble their resistance roll drop all unsecured held items. Large (SIZ 21-40): Easy resistance; Huge (SIZ 41+): Very Easy resistance.
