**Fire Shield** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Caster
- **Resist:** N/A

Creates wispy flames around the caster that protect from one elemental type (chosen when memorized). Give off no heat; light at half-torch brightness.

**Chill Shield (blue or green flames):** Reduces fire/heat damage to half; successful Resistance roll negates all. Roll is one grade easier; Evade does not render defender prone.

**Warm Shield (violet or blue flames):** Reduces cold damage to half; successful Resistance roll negates all. Roll is one grade easier; Evade does not render defender prone.

Both variants: Melee attacks using the protected element type cause the caster's full damage to be reflected back to the attacker. Worn armor of attacker does not protect; natural armor and magical AP reduce reflected damage. Creatures with Innate Magic Resistance may dispel Fire Shield with a successful Willpower roll on first strike.
