**Fly** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** 30 Minutes/Intensity, +1d6x10 Minutes (rolled secretly by GM)
- **Cost:** 3+1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

Grants one creature up to 10 SIZ per Intensity the gift of flight at 30 ft. Movement Rate (15 ft. climbing, 60 ft. diving). All speed-related skill penalties apply to flying speed. Spell casting and combat limitations apply as per ground movement. Exact Duration is unpredictable (GM rolls secretly). If Fly skill roll required, may substitute Athletics or Acrobatics at two grades of difficulty; frequent flyers may develop Fly skill at STR+DEX base.
