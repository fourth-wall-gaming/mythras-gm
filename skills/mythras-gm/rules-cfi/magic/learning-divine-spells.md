Divine casters may memorize any spell of their Rank or below, with no restrictions on selection. Upon attaining a new Rank, a Divine caster automatically gains access to **all spells of that Rank**. No Experience Roll expenditure, research time, or spell book is required. A cleric simply prays for the deity's blessing when memorizing spells.
