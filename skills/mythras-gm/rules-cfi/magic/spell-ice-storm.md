**Ice Storm** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 3
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below (Instant or 1 Round/Intensity)
- **Cost:** 3, +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** See Below

Choose one variant when casting:

**Hail Stones (Instant):** Large hail balls rain in 60 ft. radius. 1d10 per 2 Intensity; each die rolled individually and applied to random locations. Fire-using creatures: +3 per die; cold-using creatures: half damage. Worn armor = half protection; natural armor/magical AP reduce normally. Evade only effective if within 5 ft. of cover or edge (success = half damage each roll).

**Driving Sleet (1 Round/Intensity):** Creates driving sleet in a 60 ft. wide x 120 ft. long path (designated by caster). Blinds those affected. Ground ices over: movement halved; fliers must make Formidable Fly roll or land; ground walkers must succeed at Athletics each Round or fall prone (two grades harder if moving faster than a walk). Ice lasts minimum 10 minutes after spell ends. Extinguishes small fires. Negates Heat Metal.
