**Might** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 0, Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Trigger
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** None

Adds caster's POW to recipient's Brawn skill for a single predetermined act of physical brute force. Spell is expended on use. Does not increase the character's Damage Modifier for combat damage.
