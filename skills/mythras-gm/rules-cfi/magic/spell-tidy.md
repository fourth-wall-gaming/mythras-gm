**Tidy** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 20 ft
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Immediately restores up to the caster's POW in items, each no larger than 2 Things, to a neat, tidy, and orderly state within the spell's Range. Items larger than 2 Things are shifted to a more orderly position but require manual intervention to tidy properly.
