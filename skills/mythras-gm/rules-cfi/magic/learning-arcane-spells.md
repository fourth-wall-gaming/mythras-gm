Arcane spellcasters learn new spells through three methods. The caster must be of sufficient Rank to cast the spell in question.

### On Rank Increase (Free)
Arcane casters automatically learn **3 new spells** each time they gain a new Rank. No Arcane Knowledge roll or Experience Roll expenditure is required. These may be inscribed into the spell book following the normal scribing process.

### Through Research
- **Cost:** Experience Rolls equal to the spell's Rank, plus 1 week of study per spell Rank.
- Example: a Rank 2 spell requires 2 EXP rolls and 2 weeks of study.
- Investment may be spread across multiple sessions.
- The mage is assumed to be slowly scribing the spell during the research period.

### From Spell Books or Scrolls
- A casting of **Read Magic** is required before the mage can copy or memorize a spell from another's spell book or from a scroll.
- If Read Magic **Fails**, no second attempt from that source is allowed -- only the spell's Rank and name are discerned.
- Once successfully deciphered, no further Read Magic is needed for that spell.
- **No Experience Rolls** are required -- the research was done by the original author.
- A spell may be deciphered even if the caster is currently below its Rank; it simply cannot be memorized or cast until the caster attains the required Rank.

A mage can always read their own spell book without Read Magic.
