**Cure Major Wounds (R)** (Necromancy)

- **Rank:** Cleric 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Round
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A (Endurance or Evade for reverse)

Heals a single Hit Location suffering a Major Wound (crushed or mutilated). No effect on dismembered limbs (requires Heal if limb present, or Regeneration). If injured within Intensity hours: heals 1d8+4 Hit Points instantly. If injured earlier: requires full rest (Healing Rate days), with rolled HP averaged over recovery period. Interrupted recovery leaves location maimed; only Heal can fix it.

**Reverse - Cause Major Wounds:** Reduces one Hit Location by 1d8+4 Hit Points. Channel roll serves as hit roll. Cannot sever location but can crush or mutilate. Neither worn nor natural armor protects; magical AP reduce damage normally.
