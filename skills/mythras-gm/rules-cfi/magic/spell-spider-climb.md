**Spider Climb** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 2 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** N/A

The subject can move along vertical surfaces and ceilings with a Movement Rate of 10 ft. per Round, even while hanging upside down. All hand and footwear must be removed. Handling very small objects (negligible ENC) is impossible due to hands sticking to everything, which also makes casting spells with material components typically impossible under this spell. Pulling an object free from the individual, or pulling a resisting individual free from a surface, requires a Formidable Brawn roll.
