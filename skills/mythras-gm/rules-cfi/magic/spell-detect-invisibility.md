**Detect Invisibility** (Divination)

- **Rank:** Mage 2
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** 5 Minutes/Intensity
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** Line of Sight
- **Resist:** N/A

Grants the ability to see any objects or beings that are invisible, hidden, ethereal, astral, or out of phase. May also detect those hidden or concealed by mundane means. Unlike other Detect spells, limited to line of sight and does not require a minute to scan.
