**Remove Fear (R)** (Abjuration (Charm))

- **Rank:** Cleric 1
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** See Below
- **Cost:** 1/Intensity
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Area:** 1 Target
- **Resist:** See Below

Banishes fear from one individual per Intensity, granting an additional Willpower roll at one grade easier. When cast on a subject not yet suffering fear, wards against it for 10 minutes with the same grade-easier bonus. The caster cannot use this reverse-to-remove an active fear on themselves (fear prevents spell casting).

| Intensity | Cost | Variable Effect |
| :-: | :-: | :-- |
| 1 | 1 | 30 ft Range; 1 subject |
| 2 | 2 | 60 ft Range; 2 subjects |
| 3 | 3 | 90 ft Range; 3 subjects |
| 4 | 4 | 120 ft Range; 4 subjects |
| 5 | 5 | 150 ft Range; 5 subjects |

**Reverse - Cause Fear:** Touch attack. On a failed Willpower Resistance roll, the victim flees in terror at Sprint for 1d4 Rounds, direction must be away from the caster. Countered by Remove Fear (and vice versa).
