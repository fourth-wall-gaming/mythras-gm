**Meteor Shower** (Evocation)

- **Rank:** Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Cost:** 3/Intensity
- **Range:** 20 ft/Intensity
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** Evade

Two micro-meteors per Intensity streak from caster's outstretched hand, arcing toward a target on a downward trajectory. First meteor always strikes targeted location; additional meteors scatter randomly (roll 1d8 for direction); each has a 30 ft. explosive radius. Each meteor deals 1d10 fire damage. Damage combined and applied to all Hit Locations where blasts overlap (overlapping = more damage). Worn armor: no protection; magical AP and natural armor reduce damage normally. Evade (prone) = no damage; Artful Dodger = damage halved (unless within 5 ft. of edge). All within 60 ft. of any impact must pass Athletics or fall prone. Ignites flammable materials = damage x5%.
