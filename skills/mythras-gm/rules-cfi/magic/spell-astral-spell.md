**Astral Spell** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Cleric 5, Mage 5
- **Casting Time:** 2 Actions
- **Duration:** See Below (until caster wills it to cease)
- **Cost:** 3 +1/additional Intensity
- **Range:** Touch
- **Area:** See Below
- **Resist:** N/A

Allows caster and up to 5 others (one per additional Intensity beyond 1) to enter the Astral Plane. All must be touching and forming a circle when cast. Physical bodies are left in the plane of origin. Travelers do not typically bring clothing or equipment, except items that radiate a magical aura (these transform into astral equivalents). Duration lasts until caster wishes it to end. May be ended prematurely by Dispel Magic. Killing the caster's physical body also ends the spell early, stranding any companions.
