**Ignite (R)** (Transmutation)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 10 ft
- **Area:** 1 Small Fire
- **Resist:** N/A

Two uses (chosen when cast):

**Ignite:** Causes a small flammable inorganic object or hand-sized area to burst into flame. Normally used to light candles, torches, or lanterns from afar. Can start a camp or cooking fire in adverse conditions (damp kindling, high winds). Once burning, flames may spread unless quenched.

**Extinguish:** Immediately quenches small flames. Works on candles, lanterns, torches, or small cook fires. Will not work on magical fires or larger conflagrations (pyres, burning houses, dragon flames).
