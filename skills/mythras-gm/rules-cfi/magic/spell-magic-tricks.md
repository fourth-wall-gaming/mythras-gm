**Magic Tricks** (Conjuration)

- **Rank:** Mage 0
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Duration:** Concentration
- **Cost:** 1
- **Range:** 0
- **Area:** 30 ft. Radius
- **Resist:** None

Entertainment magic using small cantrip-level effects: puffs of wind to flicker candles, pulling a coin from behind a child's ear, causing a flower to quickly bloom, card tricks, eerie ethereal music, etc. Cannot cause even a single point of damage or any distraction in combat. Can be used to entertain a crowd while others sneak past.
