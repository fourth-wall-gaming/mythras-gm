The Language and Native Tongue skills are not rolled directly; their value acts as a static **fluency cap** on verbal interactions.

| Language % | Conversational Fluency |
|:-:|:--|
| 01-25% | Understands only a few simple words; cannot form sentences; cannot read |
| 26-50% | Understands and communicates in fragmentary simple sentences (e.g. "Where is Temple?"); reads at elementary level |
| 51-75% | Fluent enough for general conversation; reads at high school level |
| 76%+ | Eloquent expression; reads at college level |

**Literacy note**: literacy does not necessarily imply alphabetic writing -- carvings, knotwork, hieroglyphs, and ideograms all count.

**Dialect understanding**: similar dialects sharing a common linguistic basis can be understood at least one Difficulty Grade harder, potentially reducing the effective fluency tier.
