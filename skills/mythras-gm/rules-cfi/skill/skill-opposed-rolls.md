**Opposed Rolls** resolve contests with a clear win/lose outcome (e.g. Stealth vs. Perception, Deceit vs. Insight).

**Resolution**:
1. Both participants roll against their respective skills.
2. The winner is the one with the **higher Level of Success** (Critical > Success > Failure > Fumble).
3. If both achieve the same Level of Success, the winner is the one with the **higher dice roll still within the success range**.

**Both Fail**: The GM either re-rolls the contest to establish a winner or describes an outcome that suits the nature of the failure.

**Common opposed pairs** (examples):

| Activity | Skills |
|:--|:--|
| Bargaining | Commerce vs. Commerce; Influence vs. Commerce |
| Chasing | Athletics vs. Athletics; Ride vs. Ride |
| Deceiving | Deceit vs. Insight; Sleight vs. Perception |
| Hiding/Sneaking | Stealth vs. Perception; Stealth vs. Track |
| Pulling Rank | Influence vs. Willpower; Deceit vs. Willpower |
| Test of Strength | Brawn vs. Brawn; Brawn vs. Endurance |
| Test of Wills | Willpower vs. Willpower; Seduction vs. Willpower |
| Swaying a Crowd | Oratory vs. Willpower |
