**Differential Rolls** are like Opposed Rolls but produce a graded advantage rather than a binary win/lose. Primarily used in combat resolution.

**Resolution**: Both participants roll and suffer results as if tested individually. No outright winner -- the participant with the higher Level of Success gains an advantage equal to the **difference in success levels** (requires at least one Standard success).

| | Antagonist Critical | Antagonist Success | Antagonist Failure | Antagonist Fumble |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| **Protagonist Critical** | No Benefit | Protagonist +1 Level | Protagonist +2 Levels | Protagonist +3 Levels |
| **Protagonist Success** | Antagonist +1 Level | No Benefit | Protagonist +1 Level | Protagonist +2 Levels |
| **Protagonist Failure** | Antagonist +2 Levels | Antagonist +1 Level | No Benefit | No Benefit |
| **Protagonist Fumble** | Antagonist +3 Levels | Antagonist +2 Levels | No Benefit | No Benefit |

If both score the same Level of Success, there is no additional effect.
