If the highest-skilled participant in an **Opposed or Differential Roll** has a skill value **above 100%**:

1. Calculate the excess: `excess = skill - 100`.
2. Subtract that excess from **all** participants' skill values, including the leader's own.
3. Apply any circumstance modifiers **before** identifying who has the highest skill.

**Effect**: The leader retains their relative advantage while everyone's absolute roll targets are reduced proportionally. Opponents can never have their effective skill reduced below a functional minimum.
