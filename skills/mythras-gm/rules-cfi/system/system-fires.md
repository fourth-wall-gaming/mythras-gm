**Fire Intensity Table:**

| Intensity | Examples | Time to Ignite (Rounds) | Damage per Round |
|:---------:|:---------|:----------------------:|:----------------:|
| 1 | Candle | 1d4 | 1d2 |
| 2 | Torch | 1d3 | 1d4 |
| 3 | Campfire | 1d2 | 1d6 |
| 4 | Room-filling conflagration | 1d2 | 2d6 |
| 5 | Volcanic lava | Instant | 3d6 |

**Hit Locations affected:**

- Intensity 1-2: 1 Hit Location (the one touching the source).
- Intensity 3-4: nearest 1d4+1 Hit Locations.
- Intensity 5: all Hit Locations simultaneously.

Ignited flammable materials burn until physically extinguished; damage ignores Armor Points and applies to any flesh beneath. Spread: fire spreads to a number of Hit Locations per Round equal to its Intensity.

**Fire-Based Attacks (single round):**

- Chance to ignite flammable materials: damage rolled x5% for fire; damage rolled x1% for electrical.
- Once ignited, material burns for **1d3-1 damage per Round** (ignoring AP, affects flesh too). Natural armor protects normally.
- To smother flames: drop prone + successful Athletics roll (costs 1 Action Point; attempt each Turn). Help from others: one grade easier per helper. If a damage roll yields 0, that location's fire self-extinguishes; each self-extinguished location makes smothering one grade easier.
