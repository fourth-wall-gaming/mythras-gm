**Natural Healing Rate:** The Healing Rate determines how many Hit Points are recovered per time period:

| Wound Type | Recovery Period |
|:-----------|:---------------|
| Minor Wound | Healing Rate HP per Day |
| Serious Wound | Healing Rate HP per Week (until HP >0, then per Day) |
| Major Wound | Healing Rate HP per Month |

**Restrictions:**

- No strenuous activity while healing; otherwise Healing Rate is reduced by 1d3 for that period.
- **Major Wounds** do not begin natural healing until treated with a successful Healing skill roll or a Cure Wounds spell.
- Untreated Major Wounds that are not treated within days equal to **one-twentieth of the Healing skill** become maimed (permanently reduce the location's Hit Points).

**Magical Healing:**

- Cure Minor Wounds: most effective on Minor Wounds.
- Cure Serious Wounds: most effective on Minor and Serious Wounds.
- Only the most powerful magic can instantly reverse Major Wounds.
- Any healing spell, regardless of power, immediately stabilizes any wound -- stopping bleeding and preventing immediate death -- even if it cannot fully cure the injury. (This applies only to physical trauma, not suffocation, poison, etc.)
