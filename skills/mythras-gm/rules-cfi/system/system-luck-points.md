**Luck Points** differentiate heroes from ordinary NPCs. They replenish to their normal value at the start of each new session.

**Gaining Luck Points:** All characters gain +1 Luck Point upon reaching Rank 2, and +1 at every Rank thereafter.

**Spending Luck Points:** Only one Luck Point per Action. Cannot be spent on Experience or Training rolls.

| Option | Effect |
|:-------|:-------|
| **Cheat Fate** | Re-roll or swap any dice roll (yours or opponent's) -- e.g., a 75 becomes a 57. |
| **Desperate Effort** | Gain 1 additional Action Point when all APs are exhausted. |
| **Mitigate Damage** | Downgrade a Major Wound to a Serious Wound (location HP set to 1 below Major Wound threshold). |
