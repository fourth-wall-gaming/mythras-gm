**Falling Damage** (Armor Points do NOT reduce; Damage Modifier applies):

| Fall Height | Damage |
|:------------|:-------|
| 5 feet or less | No damage |
| 6-15 feet | 1d6 to one random location |
| 16-30 feet | 2d6 to two random locations |
| 31-45 feet | 3d6 to three random locations |
| 46-60 feet | 4d6 to four random locations |
| Each additional 15 feet | +1d6 damage |

**Falling Objects:**

A falling object deals 1d6 damage per 6 points of SIZ (round fractions up), plus the damage from the Falling Distance Table (including reductions for smaller objects).
