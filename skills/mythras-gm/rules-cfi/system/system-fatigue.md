**Accruing Fatigue:** For strenuous activity, the GM calls for an Athletics, Brawn, or Endurance roll. Each Failed roll accrues 1 level of Fatigue. Asphyxiation and blood loss add Fatigue through their own rules.

**Fatigue Levels Table:**

| Fatigue Level | Skill Grade | Movement | Initiative | Action Points | Recovery Period |
|:-------------|:-----------:|:--------:|:----------:|:-------------:|:---------------:|
| Fresh | -- | -- | No Penalty | -- | -- |
| Winded | Hard | No Penalty | No Penalty | No Penalty | 15 minutes |
| Tired | Hard | -5 feet | No Penalty | No Penalty | 3 hours |
| Wearied | Formidable | -5 feet | -2 | No Penalty | 6 hours |
| Exhausted | Formidable | Halved | -4 | -1 | 12 hours |
| Debilitated | Herculean | Halved | -6 | -2 | 18 hours |
| Incapacitated | Herculean | Immobile | -8 | -3 | 24 hours |
| Semi-Conscious | Hopeless | No Activities | No Activities | No Activities | 36 hours |
| Comatose | No Activities | No Activities | No Activities | No Activities | 48 hours |
| Dead | Dead | -- | -- | -- | Never |

**Recovering from Fatigue (physical exertion):** Complete rest for the Recovery Period divided by the character's Healing Rate removes one Fatigue level. Asphyxiation Fatigue recovers faster (1 level per minute); blood loss Fatigue recovers slower (1 level per day, starting the day after bleeding stops).
