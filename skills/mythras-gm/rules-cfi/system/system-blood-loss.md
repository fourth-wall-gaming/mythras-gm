**Blood Loss** from external or internal wounds accrues Fatigue levels (same mechanism as Asphyxiation). Without medical treatment, it typically results in death.

**Recovery:** Unlike Asphyxiation, blood loss Fatigue effects are enduring. The character recovers 1 Fatigue level per day, starting the day **after** bleeding stops.

**Heroic Last Actions:** When a key character suffers a Major Wound, the GM may allow one heroic last Action (e.g., cast a final spell, strike the foe, tourniquet a wound). Requirements:
- The character must have working limbs applicable to the action.
- Must spend 1 Luck Point.
- Gets one attempt, then immediately collapses into unconsciousness.
