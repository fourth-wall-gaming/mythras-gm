**Aging** begins at Early Middle Age (40 for humans). On entering each new Age Band, the character makes both an Endurance roll and a Willpower roll at the Difficulty Grade shown.

A Failed Endurance roll causes physical aging; a Failed Willpower roll causes mental aging. Each failed roll reduces one Characteristic by 1d3 (see table). If any Characteristic reaches 0, the character dies.

Once Dotage is reached, Aging rolls are made yearly regardless of race.

**Physical Aging (failed Endurance)**

| 1d6 | Characteristic |
|:---:|:--------------|
| 1-2 | STR |
| 3-4 | CON |
| 5-6 | DEX |

**Mental Aging (failed Willpower)**

| 1d6 | Characteristic |
|:---:|:--------------|
| 1-2 | INT |
| 3-4 | POW |
| 5-6 | CHA |
