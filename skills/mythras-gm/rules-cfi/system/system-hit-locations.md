**Humanoid Hit Location Table (roll 1d20):**

| 1d20 | Hit Location |
|:----:|:------------|
| 1-3 | Right Leg |
| 4-6 | Left Leg |
| 7-9 | Abdomen |
| 10-12 | Chest |
| 13-15 | Right Arm |
| 16-18 | Left Arm |
| 19-20 | Head |

Some attacks (spells, breath weapons, etc.) may strike multiple locations simultaneously. Individual locations may be armored separately.
