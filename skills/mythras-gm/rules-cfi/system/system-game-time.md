**Combat Round:** 5 seconds of real time. Used for action-by-action resolution (combat, chases, etc.).

**Exploration Round:** 5 minutes of real time.

- Movement: 3x Base Movement Rate (60 feet for human-sized characters; 45 feet for smaller demi-humans).
- Assumes caution, awareness of traps, and someone mapping.
- Enemies detect party at one grade harder (unless light sources reveal party in darkness).
- Perception rolls made without penalty.
- No mapper: treat as 1 minute long instead.
- Moving at combat speed: Perception rolls one grade harder; enemies lose the detection penalty.

**Skill Time:** A few minutes to a few hours. Used for deliberate tasks with a definite result (e.g., picking a lock: ~5 minutes; observing guard patrols: several hours).

**Narrative Time:** GM narration and player discussion. Compresses or stretches to match story needs.

**Daily-use items:** Reset after a full night's sleep (even if used just before going to bed).
