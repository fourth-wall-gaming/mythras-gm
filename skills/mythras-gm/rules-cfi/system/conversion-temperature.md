**Temperature Table**

| Temperature (F) | Type    | Risk (exposure interval) |
|:---------------:|:-------:|:------------------------|
| < 2 F           | Glacial | Seek Immediate Warmth   |
| 3-14 F          | Freezing| 15 Minutes              |
| 13-32 F         | Cold    | Hourly                  |
| 33-50 F         | Chill   | Daily                   |
| 51-68 F         | Cool    | --                      |
| 69-86 F         | Warm    | --                      |
| > 86 F          | Hot     | Hourly                  |

**Rules**

- Wearing suitable clothing grants a grace period of CON in hours before exposure sets in.
- After the grace period, the character must seek shelter or begin suffering Fatigue loss.
- Wet characters shift the Risk Rate one step cooler.
- Light or moderate gale force winds shift Risk Rate one step cooler.
- Strong gales and storms shift Risk Rate two steps cooler.
