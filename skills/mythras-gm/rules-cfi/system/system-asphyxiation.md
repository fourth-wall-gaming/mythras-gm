**Holding Breath:**

- Prepared (lungs full): hold for Endurance skill value in seconds.
- Unprepared, passive situation: halved.
- Unprepared, strenuous activity: one-fifth.

**Each round once breath period expires,** make an Endurance roll:

| Result | Effect |
|:-------|:-------|
| Critical Success | No further deterioration |
| Success | Accrue 1 level of Fatigue |
| Failure | Accrue 1d2 levels of Fatigue that Round |
| Fumble | Accrue 1d3 levels of Fatigue that Round |

**Recovery:** If asphyxiation ends before death, regain 1 Fatigue level per minute. The GM may impose an enduring Fatigue level for lung damage (smoke, water inhalation).
