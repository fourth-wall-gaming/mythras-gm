## Cleric

Clerics are the militant arm of a religious order. They travel the land spreading the word of their pantheon, bless allies with healing and protection, smite enemies with offensive divine spells, and drive away undead through faith.

**Common Species/Culture:** Any.
**Important Characteristics:** STR, INT, POW, CHA.

## Alignment and Oath

- Alignment: Any, as appropriate to pantheon or deity.
- Clerical Oath: 30% + INT + POW (mandatory).
- Must tithe 10% of all wealth to the order.
- Serves all deities of a pantheon that share compatible Alignment; opposes or respects (but does not worship) opposing-Alignment deities.

## Class Skills

- **Standard:** Combat Skill (Cleric), First Aid, Influence, Insight, Locale, Sing, Willpower.
- **Professional:** Channel and Devotion (specific pantheon/deity) automatically; plus 3 from: Bureaucracy, Courtesy, Culture (any), Healing, Lore (Alchemy), Lore (Religion), Language (any), Lore (any), Oratory.

## Armor and Weapon Proficiency

Proficient with all armor (light and heavy) and all shields except tower shields. Arcane spell casting is NOT hindered by armor. Combat Skill includes all basic weapons.

## Starting Abilities (Rank 1, free at character creation)

- **Spellcaster:** Masters of Divine magic. No spell book needed -- clerics access ALL spells of their rank or below. Casting requires 1+ Cast Magic Actions (brief prayers, somatic gestures with holy symbol) and a successful Channel roll.
- **Starting Spells:** Access to all Rank 0 cleric spells and all of current rank or lower. Pray for any of them when choosing memorized spells. See Memorizing Spells rules.
- **Turn Undead:** May turn undead within 30 feet. Evil clerics may choose to control instead. Usable once at Rank 1, twice at Rank 3, three times at Rank 5. Regain all uses after a Short Rest (1+ hour).

Also, during character creation choose one Rank 1 Ranked Ability for free (if prerequisites met).

**Turning Power:** 1d6+2, +2 per rank beyond 1. (Example: Rank 2 = 1d6+4.)

**Turning Procedure:** Opposed test: cleric's Channel vs. highest Willpower undead in area.
- Critical Success: Twice Turning Power in Intensity affected. Non-sapient undead of Intensity <= half cleric's Rank are destroyed. Evil clerics may Enslave Turning Power in Intensity instead.
- Success: Turning Power in Intensity affected (lowest first). Affected must stay 20+ feet away for 12 Rounds. If forced within 10 feet, Turn is broken. Sapient undead get Hard Willpower each Round to break effect. Non-sapient undead of Intensity <= half cleric's Rank are destroyed. Evil clerics command Turning Power in Intensity instead.
- Failure: Nothing turned.
- Fumble: Draws twice Turning Power in Intensity of undead to target the cleric specifically.

## Ranked Abilities

**Rank 1 (1 EXP roll each):**
- **Powerful Concentration:** Req. Devotion 50%+. Willpower tests to maintain Concentration on spells are 1 grade easier.
- **Greater Turning:** Req. Willpower 60%+. May re-roll Turning Power; new roll must be used.
- **Holy Smite:** Req. Devotion and Willpower 50%+. Increase Damage Modifier by 1 grade vs. undead, demons, devils. Once/day per Rank attained.
- **Mental Strength I:** Req. Devotion 60%+. Once/day: Willpower checks vs. mind-targeting magic 2 grades easier; Disbelieve illusions/phantasms 1 grade easier.
- **Weapon Precision:** Calculate Damage Modifier for small weapons using STR+DEX instead of STR+SIZ if better. Cap at weapon's normal damage die. Covers clubs, daggers, garrotes, knives, shortswords, main gauche, rapiers, unarmed; also darts, slings, short bows, javelins.

**Rank 2 (2 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic (not exceeding racial max).
- **Defensive Reflexes I:** Must be Unburdened and in no more than light armor. Once per combat: re-roll a failed Parry or Evade without spending a Luck Point.
- **Extra Turning:** Req. Devotion and Willpower 80%+. Double Turn uses between rests (2 at Rank 1, 4 at Rank 3, 6 at Rank 5).
- **Mental Strength II:** Req. Mental Strength I and Devotion 80%+. As Mental Strength I but twice/day.
- **Ranged Touch (X):** Req. Willpower 80%+. Give a Touch-range spell a 30-foot range instead; treat as 1 rank higher for memorization.

**Rank 3 (3 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Improved Holy Smite:** Req. Holy Smite + Devotion and Willpower 90%+. Damage Modifier increased by 2 grades (vs. undead/demons/devils).
- **Mental Strength III:** Req. Mental Strength II and Devotion 100%+. As Mental Strength I but three times/day.

**Rank 4 (4 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes II:** Req. Defensive Reflexes I. As Defensive Reflexes I but twice per combat.
- **Mental Strength IV:** Req. Mental Strength III and Oath/Devotion 120%+. Four times/day.

**Rank 5 (5 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Greater Holy Smite:** Req. Improved Holy Smite + Devotion and Willpower 130%+. Damage Modifier increased by 4 grades vs. undead/demons/devils.
- **Mental Strength V:** Req. Mental Strength IV and Oath/Devotion 140%+. Five times/day.
