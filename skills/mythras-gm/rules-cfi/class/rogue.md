## Rogue

Rogues are experts in subterfuge -- stealth, traps, lockpicking, and skills society views with suspicion. Not all rogues are thieves; some use infiltration skills for the greater good. They survive by wits rather than brute force.

**Common Species/Culture:** Any.
**Important Characteristics:** DEX, INT, CHA.

## Alignment and Oath

- Alignment: No restriction.
- Thieves' Guild Oath (optional): 30% + INT + POW. Required only if joining a Thieves' Guild.
- Guild strictures: loyalty to members, abide by guild rules, act in guild's best interest, keep secrets, pay dues (~100 gold/year) and a cut of guild-assisted jobs (~20%). In return: shelter, fences (pay ~1/4 sale value), guild services.
- Joining after character creation: 100 gold per current Rank + 1 month training.

## Class Skills

Rogues have more skill choices than standard (but no extra Skill Points).

- **Standard:** Athletics, Combat Skill (Rogue), Deceit, Evade, Insight, Perception, Stealth.
- **Professional:** Lockpicking and Mechanisms automatically; plus 2 from: Acrobatics, Acting, Commerce, Disguise, Intimidation, Seduction, Sleight, Streetwise. Guild members also receive Language (Thieves' Cant) at +40 at character creation.

## Armor and Weapon Proficiency

Proficient with all armor (light and heavy) and all shields except tower shields. Many rogue Abilities are penalized in heavy armor or when encumbered. Combat Skill includes all basic weapons plus hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords.

## Starting Abilities (Rank 1, all granted automatically)

- **Climb Walls:** If Unburdened and in no more than light armor: ignore 1 Difficulty Grade to Athletics rolls when climbing. Armor Penalty to Base Movement when climbing may be ignored.
- **Hide in Shadows:** If in a shadowed area, all Perception rolls to spot you are 1 Difficulty Grade harder.
- **Sneak Attack:** Requires small/medium melee weapon or Ranged Weapon within 30 feet. Target must be generally humanoid with vital organs. Must strike from outside victim's field of vision.
  - Bonus Special Effect: Choose Location (must be head, chest, or abdomen for full benefits).
  - In chosen location: ignore negative Damage Modifier; Bypass Armor and Maximize Damage do not require Critical Success.
  - Surprise not required but attack must be from outside field of vision. Parrying/Evading requires a Perception roll first (Hard if target distracted); defending vs. flank attack is Formidable, vs. rear attack is Herculean.
  - May only repeat Sneak Attack on same target if they remain distracted or unaware.
  - Sneak Attack bonus damage by rank: Rank 2: +1d2 melee (no range bonus); Rank 3: +1d4 melee, +1d2 ranged; Rank 4: +1d6 melee, +1d4 ranged; Rank 5: +1d8 melee, +1d6 ranged.
- **Subterfuge:** Lockpicking, Mechanisms, and Sleight are all 1 Difficulty Grade easier.

During character creation, also choose one Rank 1 Ability for free (if prerequisites met).

## Ranked Abilities

**Rank 1 (1 EXP roll each):**
- **Agile:** Req. Evade or Acrobatics 60%+. Must be Unburdened and in light armor or less. Add +4 to Initiative total.
- **Artful Dodger:** Req. Evade 50%+. Must be Unburdened and in light armor or less. May use Evade to dodge melee attacks without falling prone. Vs. ranged: only fall prone on Failed roll.
- **Great Hearing:** Req. Perception 50%+. All hearing-related Perception rolls 1 grade easier.
- **Sharp Eyed:** Req. Perception 50%+. All vision-related Perception rolls 1 grade easier.
- **Skirmishing:** Req. Athletics and Combat Skill 50%+. May launch ranged attack while running (Hustle Action, not sprinting). Attack percentage may not exceed Athletics skill.
- **Swashbuckling:** Req. Combat Skill 50%+. Must be Unburdened and in light armor or less. May attack and evade while jumping or swinging into/out of combat, ignoring Athletics skill cap.
- **Unarmored Defense:** Req. Artful Dodger + Evade or Acrobatics 50%+. Must be Unburdened with NO armor. Evade rolls 1 grade easier.
- **Vaulting:** Req. Acrobatics 50%+. Must be Unburdened. Using a pole (quarterstaff length+) at running/sprinting speed: Easy Acrobatics roll to vault. Horizontal vault: up to twice own height +1d4+6 feet. Vertical vault: up to pole length (6-9 feet) but land prone. Critical Success horizontal: distance x1.5. Critical Success vertical: land on feet.
- **Weapon Precision:** Use STR+DEX (instead of STR+SIZ) for Damage Modifier with small weapons if better. Cap at weapon's normal damage die. Covers clubs, daggers, garrotes, knives, shortswords, main gauche, rapiers, unarmed; also darts, slings, short bows, javelins.

**Rank 2 (2 EXP rolls each; also Sneak Attack +1d2 melee bonus):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic (within racial max).
- **Defensive Reflexes:** Must be Unburdened and in light armor or less. Once per combat: re-roll failed Parry or Evade without Luck Point. Cannot re-roll same attack twice or combine with Luck Point.
- **Read Languages:** Req. Oath 70%+; 1 month guild training. Guild members only. Read any language partially; main use is notes, instructions, maps. Base skill: INTx2 (Professional). GM rolls secretly. Success: INT/4 pieces of information. Critical: INT/2 pieces. Failure: nothing. Fumble: false information.

**Rank 3 (3 EXP rolls each; also Sneak Attack +1d4 melee, +1d2 ranged):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes II:** Req. Defensive Reflexes. As Defensive Reflexes but twice per combat.
- **Use Arcane Scrolls:** Req. Oath 90%+; 1 month guild training. Guild members only. Cast any magic-user scroll spell without Read Magic. Chance = INTx5%. Failed roll = automatic Fumble (reverses spell or redirects to reader, GM's choice).

**Rank 4 (4 EXP rolls each; also Sneak Attack +1d6 melee, +1d4 ranged):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes III:** Req. Defensive Reflexes II. Three times per combat.

**Rank 5 (5 EXP rolls each; also Sneak Attack +1d8 melee, +1d6 ranged):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes IV:** Req. Defensive Reflexes III. Four times per combat.
