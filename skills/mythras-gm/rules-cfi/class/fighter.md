## Fighter

Fighters are professional soldiers, mercenaries, and warriors. They excel in armed combat, interpose themselves between danger and allies, and wield the full range of weapons with skill. While lacking magic, they gain the strongest weapon mastery chain of any class.

**Common Species/Culture:** Any.
**Important Characteristics:** STR, CON, DEX.

## Alignment and Oath

- Alignment: No restriction.
- Oath: None required.

## Class Skills

- **Standard:** Athletics, Boating or Ride, Brawn, Combat Skill (Fighter)*, Endurance, Evade, Unarmed*.
  - * Both Combat Skill and Unarmed receive an additional +5% from Combat Proficiency.
- **Professional:** Craft (any), Engineering, Gambling, Intimidation, Lore (Military History), Lore (Strategy and Tactics), Oratory, Survival or Navigate.

## Armor and Weapon Proficiency

Proficient with all armor (light and heavy) and all shields. Combat Skill includes all basic and martial weapons.

## Starting Abilities (Rank 1, all granted automatically)

- **Combat Proficiency:** +5% to Combat Skill and Unarmed skill (not counted for Rank advancement).
- **Weapon Specialization:** Choose one weapon (or shield) to specialize in.
  - *Melee Weapon:* +5% Combat Skill with that weapon (does not aid Rank Advancement); gain a free bonus Parry with this weapon (no Action Point cost).
  - *Ranged Weapon:* May fire first before Initiative if weapon and ammo are readied and not surprised. Aiming within Close Range takes 1 Turn (not a full Round) and makes the attack 1 grade easier (extra Rounds beyond first provide no further benefit). Reduce reload time of mastered weapon by 1 (0 reload = Free Action to ready).
  - *Shields:* +10% Combat Skill with ALL shields (no Rank Advancement). All shields count as 1 Size category larger for Parrying damage (Large->Huge, Huge->Enormous, Enormous->Colossal).

Also, during character creation choose one Rank 1 Ranked Ability for free (if prerequisites met).

## Ranked Abilities

**Rank 1 (1 EXP roll each):**
- **Forceful Strike:** Req. Brawn and Combat Skill 50%+. Reduce skill 1 grade on next melee attack to increase Damage Modifier by 2 grades.
- **Just a Scratch:** Req. Endurance 50%+. Once/day after a 1+ hour break: regain HP in one location equal to Healing Rate (no effect on Major Wounds).
- **Mounted Combat:** Req. Ride 50%+. Combat skills not capped by Ride skill when mounted. Mounted charge does not incur 1-step penalty to hit. Ignore first penalty grade of any combat Ride roll. Human nomads and elven riders may also apply bonuses when using short bow from mount.
- **Second Wind:** Req. Endurance 50%+. Once/day after 1+ hour break: instantly recover up to 3 lost Fatigue levels beyond normal recovery.
- **Skirmishing:** Req. Athletics and Combat Skill 50%+. May launch a ranged attack while using the Hustle Action to run (not sprinting). Attack percentage may not exceed Athletics skill. Attack may occur before or after the move.
- **Weapon Precision:** Use STR+DEX (instead of STR+SIZ) for Damage Modifier with small weapons if better result. Cap at weapon's normal damage die. Covers clubs, daggers, garrotes, knives, shortswords, main gauche, rapiers, unarmed; also darts, slings, short bows, javelins.
- **Weapon Specialization:** Req. Combat Skill 50%+. Choose another Weapon Specialization.

**Rank 2 (2 EXP rolls each; Weapon Master is automatic if prerequisites met):**
- **Weapon Master** (automatic if prerequisites met): Req. Weapon Specialization in chosen weapon + Combat Skill 70%+. One weapon only -- cannot take if already mastered another. Doubles Critical chance of mastered weapon. Chosen melee weapon gains +1 step Damage Modifier; Ranged Weapon gains the same bonus within Close Range.
- **Brute Strength:** Once/day: Brawn skill 1 grade easier for one Action. Suffer 1 Fatigue level after.
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic (within racial max).
- **Sweeping Strike:** Req. Forceful Strike + Combat Skill 70%+. Single melee attack roll applied to 2 targets within reach and line of sight. Each resolves separately. Cannot sweep past a friendly unit between opponents. One Sweeping Strike per Round.
- **Weapon Specialization:** Req. Combat Skill 70%+. Another Weapon Specialization.

**Rank 3 (3 EXP rolls each; High Master is automatic if prerequisites met):**
- **High Master** (automatic if prerequisites met): Req. Weapon Mastery + Combat Skill 90%+.
  - Melee: Free Action riposte after successful Parry at 1 grade of Difficulty. Chosen weapon treated as 1 grade larger (Large->Huge, Huge->Enormous, Enormous->Colossal) for deflecting damage.
  - Ranged: May shift Hit Location roll to adjacent location (within Close Range), as per Marksman Special Effect.
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes:** Must be Unburdened, in no more than light armor. Once per combat: re-roll a failed Parry or Evade without Luck Point. Cannot re-roll same attack twice or combine with Luck Point.
- **Greater Sweeping Strike:** Req. Sweeping Strike + Combat Skill 90%+. As Sweeping Strike but against ALL enemies in reach and line of sight. Still one per Round.
- **Weapon Specialization:** Req. Combat Skill 90%+. Another Weapon Specialization.

**Rank 4 (4 EXP rolls each; Grand Master is automatic if prerequisites met):**
- **Grand Master** (automatic if prerequisites met): Req. Weapon High Mastery + Combat Skill 110%+.
  - Melee: +1 further step Damage Modifier (total +2 grades including Weapon Specialization).
  - Ranged: Reduce reload time of mastered weapon by another point (0 = Free Action).
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Greater Brute Strength:** Req. Brute Strength. Twice/day. First use: no Fatigue penalty. Second use: 1 Fatigue level lost.
- **Weapon Specialization:** Req. Combat Skill 110%+. Another Weapon Specialization.

**Rank 5 (5 EXP rolls each; Legendary Master is automatic if prerequisites met):**
- **Legendary Master** (automatic if prerequisites met): Req. Weapon Grand Mastery + Combat Skill 130%+.
  - Melee: Weapon treated as 2 grades larger when Parrying (Large->Enormous, Huge->Colossal).
  - Archer: Nock 2 arrows, fire at 1 grade Difficulty; or 3 arrows at 2 grades Difficulty. Targets within Close Range; multiple targets must be within 5 feet of each other.
  - Slinger/blade thrower: As archer.
  - Crossbow master: Reduce reload time 1 further point (0 = Free Action).
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes II:** Req. Defensive Reflexes. As Defensive Reflexes but twice per combat.
- **Weapon Specialization:** Req. Combat Skill 130%+. Another Weapon Specialization.
