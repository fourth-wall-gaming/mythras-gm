## Magic-User (Mage)

Mages study the esoteric, seeking spells in old tombs and spending free time memorizing them. Weak at low ranks, they have the potential to become the most powerful class -- burning armies and collapsing castles.

**Common Species/Culture:** Any, but typically Civilized and Nomadic humans, gnomes, elves, half-elves. Gnomes tend toward the School of Illusion.
**Important Characteristics:** DEX, INT, POW.

## Alignment and Oath

- Alignment: No restriction.
- Oath: None required.

## Class Skills

- **Standard:** Evade, First Aid, Influence, Insight, Locale, Perception, Willpower.
- **Professional:** Arcane Casting and Arcane Knowledge automatically; plus 3 from: Culture (any), Language (any), Lore (Alchemy), Lore (any).

## Armor and Weapon Proficiency

No armor or shield proficiency. Arcane spells require precise gestures hindered by armor. Combat Skill can only be developed with Bonus Skill Points and includes only: daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows.

## Starting Abilities (Rank 1)

- **Spellcaster:** All Arcane spells require precise gestures; armor interferes with casting (see Casting in Armor rules). Number of spells in memory per rank is shown on the Mage Rank Table.
- **Starting Spells:** Always begins with Read Magic (Rank 0 cantrip) plus 2 cantrips (Rank 0) per 1/10 Arcane Knowledge (Arcane Knowledge x 0.1). If qualifying as Rank 1, also knows 1 Rank 1 spell per 1/10 Arcane Knowledge. Spells are recorded in a spell book and must be memorized; see Memorizing Spells.

During character creation, also choose one Rank 1 Ability for free (if prerequisites met).

## Ranked Abilities

**Rank 1 (1 EXP roll each):**
- **Powerful Concentration:** Req. Arcane Casting 50%+. Willpower tests to maintain Concentration on spells are 1 Difficulty Grade easier.
- **Mental Strength:** Req. Arcane Casting 60%+. Once/day: Willpower checks vs. mind-targeting magic 2 grades easier (beguiling, charm, domination, hold, hypnosis, Magic Jar, possession, sleep, suggestion, psionic mind blast). Also 1 grade easier to Disbelieve illusions/phantasms. Does not stack with elves' Sleep/Charm resistance but broadens effect.
- **Weapon Precision:** Use STR+DEX (instead of STR+SIZ) for Damage Modifier with small weapons if better. Cap at weapon's normal damage die. Covers clubs, daggers, garrotes, knives, shortswords, main gauche, rapiers, unarmed; also darts, slings, short bows, javelins.

**Rank 2 (2 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic (within racial max).
- **Mental Strength II:** Req. Mental Strength + Arcane Knowledge 80%+. As Mental Strength but twice/day.
- **Ranged Touch (X):** Req. Willpower 80%+. Give a Touch-range spell a 30-foot range. Treat as 1 rank higher for memorization (e.g. Invisibility (Ranged) = Rank 2 spell).

**Rank 3 (3 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes:** Must be Unburdened and in no more than light armor. Once per combat: re-roll a failed Parry or Evade without Luck Point. Cannot re-roll same attack twice or combine with Luck Point.
- **Mental Strength III:** Req. Mental Strength II + Arcane Knowledge 100%+. Three times/day.

**Rank 4 (4 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Mental Strength IV:** Req. Mental Strength III + Arcane Knowledge 120%+. Four times/day.

**Rank 5 (5 EXP rolls each):**
- **Characteristic Increase:** +1 to any Characteristic.
- **Defensive Reflexes II:** Req. Defensive Reflexes. As Defensive Reflexes but twice per combat.
- **Mental Strength V:** Req. Mental Strength IV + Arcane Knowledge 140%+. Five times/day.
