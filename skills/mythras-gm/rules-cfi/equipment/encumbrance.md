A character can carry **Things** up to their STR Characteristic without penalty.

**Standard Thing values:**
- 1H melee weapon or buckler = 1 Thing; 2H weapons and larger shields = 2 Things; daggers, small weapons = 1/2 Thing
- Light/heavy bow or light crossbow = 1 Thing; heavy crossbow or light repeating crossbow = 2 Things; sling = 1/2 Thing
- Quiver (20 arrows/bolts) = 0 additional (container free)
- Fully laden backpack (up to 10 Things) = 1 Thing
- Large sack = 2 Things; holds up to 10 Things or 1,000 coins
- Spell book, grimoire, tome = 1 Thing
- Basic tool kit = 1 Thing
- Purse/pouch (up to 100 coins or 20 sling stones or 10 lead bullets) = 0 additional (container free)
- Week's rations = 1 Thing; full waterskin = 1 Thing
- Every full 50 coins (any denomination) = 1/2 Thing
- 1 SIZ = 3 Things

**Penalties for excess Things:**
- Each Thing above STR: Initiative Bonus -1
- Every 6 Things above STR: Movement Rate -5 feet
- Things carried > STR: STR- or DEX-based skills 1 grade harder
- Things carried > 2x STR: those skills 2 grades harder

**Armor encumbrance:** Proficient wearers: worn armor = 0 Things. Unproficient or carrying: light armor = 1 Thing per location (7 total full suit); heavy armor = 2 Things per location (14 total full suit).
