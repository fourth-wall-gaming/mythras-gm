Armor reduces damage per hit location by its AP value. Armor cannot be layered. Cost is per location covered (7 locations total for a full suit).

**ENC:** Proficient wearers pay 0 Things for worn armor. Non-proficient or carried: light = 1 Thing/location (7 total); heavy = 2 Things/location (14 total).

**Light Armor**

| Type                             | AP | GP/Location |
| :------------------------------- | :-: | :---------: |
| Furs or Hides                    | 1  | 2           |
| Leather, Padded, or Quilted      | 2  | 3           |
| Studded Leather or Ring Mail     | 3  | 10          |
| Brigandine, Scale, or Laminated  | 4  | 30          |

**Heavy Armor**

| Type                       | AP | GP/Location |
| :------------------------- | :-: | :---------: |
| Chain Mail                 | 5  | 40          |
| Splint Mail or Banded Mail | 6  | 60          |
| Plate Mail                 | 7  | 70          |
| Full Plate                 | 8  | 80          |
