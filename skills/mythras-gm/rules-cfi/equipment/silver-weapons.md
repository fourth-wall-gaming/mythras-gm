Metal weapons may be crafted from **pure silver** to harm lycanthropes and similar creatures.

- **Cost:** 10x normal price
- **AP:** 1 less than normal
- **HP:** 2 less than normal
- **Damage vs armored foes:** -1 damage point (e.g. silver dagger deals 1d4 instead of 1d4+1)
