Range format: Close/Effective/Long (feet). At Long range: damage halved, Force reduced one step.
Dmg Mod Y/N: whether character's Damage Modifier applies.

**Range categories:**
- Close Range: Choose Location usable as non-Critical Special Effect (stationary/unaware target only).
- Effective Range: No significant modifiers.
- Long Range: Damage halved; Force reduced one step.
