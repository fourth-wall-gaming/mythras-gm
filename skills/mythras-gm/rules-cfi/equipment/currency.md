The standard unit is the **Gold Piece (GP)**. 1 GP = one day's food, drink, and lodging at a modest lifestyle (~7 GP/week).

**Exchange Rates**

| Coin              | CP  | SP   | GP    | PP     |
| :---------------- | --: | ---: | ----: | -----: |
| Copper Piece (CP) | 1   | 1/10 | 1/100 | 1/500  |
| Silver Piece (SP) | 10  | 1    | 1/10  | 1/50   |
| Gold Piece (GP)   | 100 | 10   | 1     | 1/5    |
| Platinum Piece (PP)| 500| 50   | 5     | 1      |

Fractional costs shown with decimals: 1.5 GP = 1 GP + 5 SP. Prices assume good availability. Short supply can raise cost 10-1000% (1d100x10%).

**Livestock equivalents (some cultures):** large herd animal ~10 GP; pig/sheep ~2.5 GP; flock of hens ~1.5 GP.
