**Melee Weapon Traits:**

- **Barbed** -- If impaled, causes full (not half) damage when yanked free.
- **Entrapping** -- When used to Parry an attacking weapon, that weapon is treated as one size smaller (Large->Medium->Small). Weapons already Small are unaffected.
- **Flexible** -- When opponent uses a shield to Parry, the shield is treated as one size smaller (Huge->Large->Medium, etc.).
- **Mount** -- Must be used from the back of a moving mount.
- **Passive Block** -- Can passively block multiple contiguous Hit Locations.
- **Ranged Parry** -- Can parry Ranged weapons.
- **Set** -- Can be set to receive a foot or mounted charge.
- **Stealth** -- Must be used from ambush.
- **Thrown** -- Can be used as a Ranged Weapon as well as hand-to-hand.

**Ranged Weapon Traits:**

- **1H** -- May be fired one-handed; allows melee weapon or shield in other hand.
- **Dwarf** -- Only common in dwarf society.
- **Repeating** -- Holds a clip of 5 bolts; fire one per Action. Reload fresh clip: 3 Combat Actions. Reload spent clip with 5 bolts: 1 minute.
